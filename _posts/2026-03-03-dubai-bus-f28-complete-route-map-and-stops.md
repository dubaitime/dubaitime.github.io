## Bus Ticket Price

The fare for riding Bus F28 is based on the number of zones traveled, with a single zone costing AED 3. This pricing structure ensures an affordable and comfortable commute within Dubai. Multi-zone fares are available for longer journeys, offering a budget-friendly way to explore the city without compromising service quality. Discounts may apply for regular commuters or multiple tickets.

## Bus F28 Station Overview

### Emirates Tower Metro Seaside

The Emirates Tower Metro Seaside station serves as a vital connection point adjacent to the Emirates Towers area. Located close to the Emirates Towers metro station, it offers seamless access for commuters transferring between bus and metro services.

It features modern amenities, including comfortable waiting areas and real-time service updates, ensuring a smooth and efficient transit experience.

### White Crown Tower Bus Stop Intersection

The White Crown Tower Bus Stop is a key transit point where Bus Routes 21, 98E, F11, and F28 intersect. This station offers passengers easy access to various destinations across Dubai, enhancing connectivity for both daily commuters and visitors. Its strategic location near important landmarks and commercial areas makes it a convenient stop for those traveling through the city, providing seamless transfers between these bus routes.

### Satwa, Masjid

The Satwa, Masjid station is an important stop within the bustling Satwa neighborhood, known for its vibrant community and various local amenities. Situated near a prominent mosque, this station caters to both local residents and visitors.

It provides essential services for those traveling to and from the area, connecting them with key destinations across the city. Its central location in Satwa ensures easy access to surrounding shops, eateries, and other services.

## Bus Ticket Price

The standard bus fare in Abu Dhabi is AED 2, plus an additional AED 0.05 for every kilometer traveled, making it an economical choice for daily travel within Abu Dhabi. This fare structure applies across the entire route, offering affordable access to various destinations.

## Bus Booking

Bus 111 does not have a dedicated online booking system. For real-time updates and schedules, travelers can use public transport apps or check local transit websites for the latest information.

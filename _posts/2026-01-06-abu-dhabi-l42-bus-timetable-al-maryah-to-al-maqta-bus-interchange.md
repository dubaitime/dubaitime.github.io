Ticket Pricing: Abu Dhabi’s cashless payment system called Hafilat Card allows passengers to pay for their journey. ## Journey Experience

A beautiful journey from Al Maryah to Al Maqta bus interchange can be done using the L42 bus.

Passengers along the way can peer down and see parts of the city’s pulsating commercial and cultural districts. At Al Maqta Bus Interchange, near Maqta Bridge mesmerized by the views of the city skyline, and accessible to important suburban areas, the ride comes to a stop. This is a comfortable way to see Abu Dhabi’s best attractions and neighborhoods.

Bus N41 (overnight) is one of its key routes, running between Corniche Hospital and ADNEC (Abu Dhabi National Exhibition Centre) and providing an important late-night connection across the city. In this guide, you are going to get a complete review of the N41 route, its operational time, stops, and tips for traveling on this route. As a resident or visitor, the N41 guarantees an easy journey throughout the night.

Bus N41 TimetableFor the full schedule, visit the official AD Mobility website or follow this link for the N41 Bus Timings.

Ticket Pricing: Hafilat Card is a convenient cashless payment system by Abu Dhabi for paying for Bus N41. Journeys within one zone cost AED 2, with an extra charge of AED 0.05 per kilometer beyond that first zone. It is very easy to top up your Hafilat card online or in any of the kiosks spread all over the city and enjoy a smooth travel experience.

N41 bus route is connecting the important locations in Abu Dhabi and covers the residential as well as commercial areas. Below is an overview of the main stops along the route:

2. Al Mina Street: A lively area filled with shopping, dining, and entertainment options.

3. Hamdan Street: A central business and commercial district known for its retail outlets and business establishments.

8. Ministry of Economy: The central government office responsible for economic policies and regulations.

9. Sultan Bin Zayed The First Street: A busy street lined with commercial and residential buildings.

10. Liwa Center: A shopping and commercial center offering various services and retail outlets.

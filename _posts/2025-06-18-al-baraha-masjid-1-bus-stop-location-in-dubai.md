## Bus Route Details

The Al Baraha Masjid 1 Bus Stop is serviced by the C15 bus route, providing frequent and reliable transportation throughout the day to Mamzar Beach Park Terminus.

- C15 Route: The C15 route connects Al Baraha Masjid 1 with Mamzar Beach Park Terminus, running from early morning until late at night. Buses start operating at 06:10 AM, with departures approximately every 30 minutes. The last bus leaves at 11:10 PM. This route offers consistent connectivity to Mamzar Beach Park Terminus, a key destination for leisure and relaxation.

The C15 route ensures easy access to Mamzar Beach Park Terminus, making it a convenient option for both daily commutes and recreational visits.

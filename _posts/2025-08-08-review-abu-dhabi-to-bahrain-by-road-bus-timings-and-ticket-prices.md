## Overview of Bus Travel: Duration, Cost, and Distance

The journey from Abu Dhabi to Bahrain by bus is an extensive trip, taking approximately 31 hours and 12 minutes. This lengthy travel time allows passengers to enjoy the varied landscapes along the route. Bus services are operated by several providers, each offering different routes and schedules. The cost of a bus ticket ranges from AED 1,038 to AED 2,074, depending on the operator and route. The road distance between Abu Dhabi and Bahrain is about 819.9 km, while the direct distance is approximately 498 km.

## Bus 10 Station Overview

### Gold Souq Bus Station · 1

Gold Souq Bus Station, located in the heart of Dubai’s historic Gold Souq district, serves as a vital transit point for both locals and tourists. This station is strategically positioned near the bustling market area, offering easy access to a variety of shops and attractions.

Equipped with essential amenities such as sheltered waiting areas, ticketing services, and real-time schedule updates, the station ensures a comfortable travel experience. Its central location enhances connectivity to other major bus routes and key areas of interest within Dubai, making it a crucial part of the city’s public transportation network.

### British Council Bus Stop Intersection

This strategic location offers passengers seamless connections across different routes, enhancing the overall convenience and efficiency of travel in the area. The bus stop's integration with these routes ensures accessible and well-connected options for both daily commuters and occasional travelers.

### Al Quoz Bus Station · 6

Al Quoz Bus Station is a key transportation hub situated in the Al Quoz industrial and residential area. This station is well-frequented by commuters traveling to and from the various commercial and residential zones of Al Quoz.

The station’s location supports easy access to local businesses and residential complexes, making it an important link in the broader network of Dubai’s public transportation system.

Reg Bus 202 is a vital part of Abu Dhabi's public transportation network, connecting Al Rahbah, Al Yaseer Street/Al Rahba Hospital to Al Nahyan, Abu Dhabi Bus Station. This route serves both residents and visitors, offering a reliable, affordable travel option through key areas of the city. Whether you're commuting to work, visiting landmarks, or running errands, Bus 202 provides convenient access to essential destinations across Abu Dhabi.

## Route Overview

Reg Bus 202 starts its journey at Al Rahbah, Al Yaseer Street, and makes several stops before reaching Abu Dhabi Bus Station. Key locations along the route include:
1. Al Rahba Hospital  The starting point of the route, serving residents and visitors.

2. Abu Al Darda Mosque  A peaceful stop for those visiting this mosque.

3. Al Wahda Sport Club Academy  A stop for sports enthusiasts.

4. Federal Government Office  Serves passengers heading to government offices.

5. Badr Haji Mosque  Another prominent mosque along the route.

6. Al Shahama Park  A green escape for families and visitors.

7. Al Mirsal Street  A central road providing easy access to surrounding areas.

8. Emirates Park Zoo  A stop for families and tourists.

9. Al Shahama School  Key stop for students and educational purposes.

10. F1 Assembly Point  A transit point for various commuters.

11. Al Widayhi Street  Another key stop connecting residential areas.

12. Deerfields Mall  A popular shopping destination.

13. ADNOC  Serving those working or visiting this leading energy company.

## Accessibility Features

Reg Bus 202 is designed with accessibility in mind, featuring ramps, priority seating for differently-abled passengers, and audio-visual aids for those with hearing or vision impairments. This ensures a comfortable and inclusive travel experience for all passengers.

## Journey Experience

A trip on Bus 202 from Al Rahba Hospital to Abu Dhabi Bus Station offers passengers a long and scenic journey through the city. Starting in the quieter outskirts near Al Rahba, the route gradually moves into more urbanized areas, passing through key residential and commercial districts.

The journey also provides convenient access to several local attractions, healthcare facilities, and bustling markets, giving a well-rounded view of Abu Dhabi’s diverse landscape. Whether commuting for work or leisure, this route ensures a smooth connection between the suburbs and the city center.

## Travel Tips

To make your trip more enjoyable, consider traveling during off-peak hours to avoid crowds. Stay updated on real-time schedules and any route changes by downloading the Abu Dhabi Department of Transport app. Additionally, keeping your Hafilat Smart Card topped up will ensure a hassle-free experience.

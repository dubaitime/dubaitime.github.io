## Route Overview

Some of Abu Dhabi’s major landmarks and services are easily accessible by Bus AR1. Below are the key stops along the route:

1. Al Reem Mall: Starting point, a major shopping destination.

2. Marina Square: A bustling area with offices, shops, and dining options.

3. The Galleria Al Maryah Island: A luxury shopping center.

4. Sheikh Hazza Bin Sultan Mosque: A beautiful mosque reflecting traditional Islamic architecture.

5. City Seasons Al Hamra Hotel: A popular hotel offering comfortable stays.

6. Lifeline Hospital: A well-known healthcare facility.

7. NMC Specialty Hospital: Another key hospital offering specialized medical services.

8. Qasr Al Hosn: A historical landmark and cultural heritage site.

## Journey Experience

A great tour of Abu Dhabi’s key landmarks can be had by taking Bus AR1 between Al Reem Mall and Marina Mall and back. The bus starts at Al Reem Mall, passes through Marina Square, The Galleria on Al Maryah Island and the Sheikh Hazza Bin Sultan Mosque with its Islamic architecture. The stops include City Seasons Al Hamra Hotel, Lifeline, NMC Specialty Hospitals, and Qasr Al Hosn.

It also includes the route to Khalidiya Park, Sheikha Fatima Park, and the Corniche which provides some relaxing green space and some great views onto the waterfront. The trip spans from Marina Mall (a famous mall and leisure site) to a very modern part of the city but also shows a more traditional part. A dynamic mix of cultural sites, green spaces and vibrant urban life is all there for travelers in one journey.

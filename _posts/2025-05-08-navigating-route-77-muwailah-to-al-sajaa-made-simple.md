Route 77 is a crucial bus service in Sharjah, connecting the Muwailah Terminal to the industrial hub of Al Saja’a. Whether you're commuting for work or traveling for leisure, this route offers a practical and budget-friendly way to navigate key areas of the city. With regular services and convenient stops along major roads, Route 77 makes it easy to move between Sharjah’s residential and industrial zones.

## Route Overview

The journey on Route 77 begins at Muwailah Terminal and ends at Al Saja’a Terminal, passing through the following important stops:
1. Meliha Road

2. Emirates Road

3. Al Dhaid Road

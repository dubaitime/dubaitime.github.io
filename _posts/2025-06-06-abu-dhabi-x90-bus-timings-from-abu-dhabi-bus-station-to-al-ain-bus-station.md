## Journey Experience

The Abu Dhabi to Al Ain Bus X90 is the name of the bus which is a long ride but a comfortable one and passes through some main stops on route such as Baniyas Court and Tawam Hospital.

The road also gives access to Al Ain Zoo and Jebel Hafeet mountain, which provides a breath taking views. Passengers riding this bus ride are available to enjoy the difference of bustling Abu Dhabi city life and soothing Al Ain deserts beauty in similar style.

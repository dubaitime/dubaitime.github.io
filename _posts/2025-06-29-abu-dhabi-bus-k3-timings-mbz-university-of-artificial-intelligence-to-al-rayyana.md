## Journey Experience

Bus K3 is a scenic and informative ride through some of Abu Dhabi’s most famous areas. The route starts from MBZ University of Artificial Intelligence and goes through the green spaces before it passes by MASDAR Eco Residences and IRENA HQ.

When the bus goes towards Khalifa City, Souq, you will get to see people enjoying local markets and residential neighborhoods. The K3 route stops at several places near educational institutions, shopping centres and commercial hubs, giving passengers a brief idea of the dynamic and multi faceted life in Abu Dhabi. The trip offers easy access to a lot of key areas for work and play.

## Bus C15 Station Overview

### Deira City Center Bus Station · 2

Deira City Center Bus Station · 2 is a significant transit hub situated near the Deira City Center Mall, one of Dubai’s major shopping and entertainment destinations. This bus station is equipped with modern amenities, including sheltered waiting areas, ticketing services, and real-time schedule updates.

### Mamzar Beach Park Terminus

Mamzar Beach Park Terminus is a key bus station located close to Mamzar Beach Park, a well-known recreational area in Dubai. This station provides essential transit services for beachgoers, families, and visitors enjoying the park's facilities.

The station features amenities such as sheltered waiting areas and current schedule information to ensure a comfortable travel experience. The terminus plays a crucial role in facilitating transportation to this popular leisure destination.

## Tourist Destination Along the Way

Here are some notable attractions and destinations along the route:
1. City Centre Metro · 2: This area is part of the City Centre group of shopping malls, offering a range of retail outlets, dining options, and entertainment.

2. DNATA: While primarily a major air services provider, DNATA's location in the area means it's well-connected and accessible for travelers.

3. Al Bakhit Center: A commercial hub that includes various shops and eateries.

4. Hamarain Center: Known for its shopping options and proximity to other commercial areas.

5. Abu Hail, Post Office: This location provides essential postal services and is near various local amenities.

6. Hamriya Port: An industrial port area, while not a typical tourist destination, is significant for those interested in Dubai's trade and maritime activities.

7. Sheikh Almar Masjid: A local mosque that provides insight into the cultural and religious practices of Dubai.

8. Al Mamzar Masjid: Another prominent mosque in the area, reflecting the architectural and cultural heritage of the region.

9. Mamzar Beach Park Terminus: Located near Al Mamzar Beach Park, this is a popular destination for leisure activities. The park itself offers beautiful beaches, picnicking areas, and recreational facilities.

## Journey Experience

Bus Route C15 from Deira City Center Bus Station to Mamzar Beach Park Terminus takes you on a pleasant journey through some key spots in Dubai. Starting at the busy Deira City Center, a popular shopping destination, the bus passes through residential and commercial areas, offering glimpses of local life.

As you head towards Al Mamzar, you’ll pass by landmarks such as the Al Muraqqabat area, known for its vibrant dining scene. The journey ends at Mamzar Beach Park, a beautiful waterfront park with sandy beaches, picnic spots, and breathtaking views of the Arabian Gulf, making it a perfect spot to relax after a long trip.

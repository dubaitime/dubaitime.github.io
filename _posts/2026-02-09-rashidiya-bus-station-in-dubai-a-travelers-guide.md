## Bus Route Details

Rashidiya Bus Station is serviced by a wide range of bus routes, providing convenient transportation options for travelers heading to key areas in Dubai. Some of the major routes include:

- F03: Mirdif  Stops at Etihad Mall and Uptown Mirdif.

- F05: Mizhar  Stops at Arabian Center.
- F06: Rashidiya.
- F10: Al Warqa  Stops at Etihad Mall, Uptown Mirdif, and City Centre Mirdif.
- F60: Mirdif  Stops at Uptown Mirdif and City Centre Mirdif.
- F61: Al Warqa.

Additional services from Centrepoint Metro Station/Rashidiya Bus Station include:

- 11B: Al Awir  Stops at Mushrif Park.

- 20: Al Nahda to Al Warsan  Stops at International City and Dragon Mart.
- 44: Al Ghubaiba Bus Station to Rashidiya  Stops at Sharaf DG, BurJuman, and Oud Metha Metro Stations.
- 310: International City  Stops at Dragon Mart.
- 320: Dubai Outsource City  Stops at Dubai Silicon Oasis and Dubai Academic City.
- 367: Etisalat Bus Station to Dubai Silicon Oasis  Stops at Mirdif City Centre, Dragon Mart, and Dubai Academic City.
- 102: Seasonal bus service to Global Village.

These routes provide frequent service throughout the day, ensuring accessibility for both early and late travelers.

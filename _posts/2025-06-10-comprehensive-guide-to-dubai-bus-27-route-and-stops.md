## Bus Ticket Price

The exact fare for Dubai's 27 bus route depends on the number of zones you travel through, but you can expect it to be around AED 5. This estimation is based on typical Dubai bus fares and the short distance covered by route 27. To get the most accurate price for your trip, use the Dubai Roads and Transport Authority's (RTA) journey planner.

## Bus Booking

Passengers can avail themselves of bus services directly at designated stops without prior booking. For more information on schedules, fares, and route details, passengers can visit the Roads and Transport Authority (RTA) website or contact customer service for assistance.

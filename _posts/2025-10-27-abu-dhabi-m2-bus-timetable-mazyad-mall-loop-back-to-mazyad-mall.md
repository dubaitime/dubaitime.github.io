## Travel Tips for Bus M2

- Plan Ahead: Review the bus schedule to avoid peak travel times and ensure timely departures.

- Use a Hafilat Card: This enables quick, hassle-free payments for a smooth travel experience.
- Monitor Real-Time Updates: Use the AD Mobility app to stay updated on bus schedules and avoid delays.
- Travel During Off-Peak Hours: For a quieter ride, consider traveling outside of peak hours.

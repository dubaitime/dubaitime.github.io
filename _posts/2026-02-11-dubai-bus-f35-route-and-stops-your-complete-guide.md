## Bus F35 Station Overview

### Mall of the Emirates Bus Station

The Mall of the Emirates Bus Station is a vital transportation hub situated within the Mall of the Emirates complex, one of Dubai's premier shopping destinations. Strategically positioned to serve both shoppers and commuters, the station offers a convenient link to various parts of the city.

It is equipped with modern amenities, including comfortable waiting areas, ticketing services, and real-time bus arrival information.

### Al Barsha 1 B Bus Stop Intersection

The Al Barsha 1 B Bus Stop is a key transit point where Bus Routes F35 and F33 intersect. Located in the bustling Al Barsha area, this stop provides passengers with convenient connections to various parts of the city. Whether you're commuting to nearby residential areas or popular destinations, this bus stop offers a strategic location for easy travel within the neighborhood.

### Al Barsha Bus Stop at Dubai National School 1 Intersection

The Al Barsha Bus Stop at Dubai National School 1 serves as a key junction where Bus Route F35 and Bus Route 84 intersect. This strategic location provides convenient connections for passengers traveling between different areas of the city, enhancing mobility and accessibility for both daily commuters and occasional travelers.

### Al Barsha Heights, Ramee Guestline Hotel Apartment Bus Stop Intersection

The Al Barsha Heights, Ramee Guestline Hotel Apartment Bus Stop is a key intersection for Bus Routes 84, F31, and F35. Its location near the Ramee Guestline Hotel Apartment ensures easy access to local amenities and destinations.

### Al Barsha E 2 Bus Stop Intersection

The Al Barsha E 2 Bus Stop serves as an important transit hub where Bus Routes 84 and F35 converge. Its strategic location ensures seamless connectivity to nearby residential and commercial zones.

### Intersection at Al Barsha, Veterinary Clinic 1 Bus Stop

The Al Barsha, Veterinary Clinic 1 Bus Stop serves as a crucial junction for F35 and F33. This intersection provides passengers with efficient transfer options between these routes, enhancing connectivity and convenience for travelers in the Al Barsha area.

## Bus Ticket Price

Traveling on the F35 bus route is economical, with ticket prices typically in the range of AED 3-7 per journey. The cost may vary based on the distance traveled and the type of NOL card used.

## Bus Booking

To book a ride on the F35 bus, passengers can use the RTA app or visit the Mall of the Emirates Bus Station. While online booking for specific buses might not be available, the RTA app provides real-time bus schedules and updates.

Steps to check bus schedules and plan your trip via the RTA app:
1. Download the RTA app from the App Store or Google Play.

2. Open the app and navigate to the 'Public Transport' section.

3. Enter your starting point and destination to view available routes.

4. Select bus F35 to see the schedule and next arrival times.

5. Plan your trip accordingly and head to the bus stop.

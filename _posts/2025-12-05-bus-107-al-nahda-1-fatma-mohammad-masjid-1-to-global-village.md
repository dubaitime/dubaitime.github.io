Dubai’s public transport system is famed for its efficiency and the extent to which it reaches the city’s busy districts. The RTA Bus 107 which is the main route connecting Al Nahda 1 Fatma Mohammad Masjid 1 to Global Village serves residents and tourists. This is an important route between residential areas and one of Dubai’s most popular leisure destinations. In this article we are going to cover the operational details, route overview, and tips for your trip on the 107 bus.

## Operational Details

There are no operational schedule for Bus 107 available, but you can check on their official RTA website or mobile app for real time update. Nol Card is used for payment by travelers, which is a single and smooth way of accessing all public transport modes in Dubai. Within one zone, a single trip costs AED 3. Recharging Nol Cards is possible online, at metro stations and at designated kiosks throughout the city.

## Route Overview

It links key points between Al Nahda 1 Fatma Mohammad Masjid 1 and Global Village. Below are some major stops along the route:

1. Al Nahda 1 Fatma Mohammad Masjid 1  A residential hub with easy access to various facilities and amenities.

2. Global Village  A top destination for cultural, shopping, and entertainment experiences, featuring pavilions from around the world, live performances, and thrilling rides.

It is a lifeline for those coming from the popular residential communities to Dubai’s most sought after entertainment and cultural destination. Other Dubai bus routes include; F43 bus route which starts from Ibn Battuta station and goes to Discovery Gardens.

## Journey Experience

The RTA Bus 107 from Al Nahda 1 Fatma Mohammad Masjid 1 to Global Village provides a beautiful immersive journey. A long journey takes you through the cityscapes of vibrant cities, depicting Dubai’s juxtaposition of diverse residential neighbourhoods and a modern infrastructure.

At the route along you will see busy local markets, calm parks as well as cultural places that show character of the city, connected equally with tradition and innovation. Global Village is a must see destination to experience an international cultural extravaganza with pavilions from around the globe, thrilling rides, and one of a kind shopping. The Dubai charm is accessible for both the residents and tourists while you are on this bus route which is cheap yet convenient.

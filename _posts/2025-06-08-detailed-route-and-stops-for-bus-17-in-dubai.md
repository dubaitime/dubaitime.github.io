## Journey Experience

The 17 bus route from Wasl Oasis 2 to Sabkha Bus Station provides a long yet captivating journey through the heart of Dubai. Departing from Wasl Oasis 2, known for its modern residential developments and family-friendly amenities, passengers quickly find themselves immersed in the vibrant atmosphere of the city.

As the bus navigates through various neighborhoods, it passes by cultural landmarks such as the Dubai Frame, which offers stunning views of both old and new Dubai. The route also meanders through Al Mankhool, a bustling district filled with shops and eateries, providing a glimpse into everyday life in the city. Travelers can enjoy the scenic views of Dubai Creek as they approach Sabkha, a historic area where the traditional souks and markets await.

This trip not only highlights the contrasts of Dubai's dynamic landscape but also allows passengers to explore the rich cultural tapestry that defines the city.

Ticket Pricing: Abu Dhabi’s convenient cashless payment system, Hafilat Card can be used to make payments for Bus L43. Worries that the cost of the service would be prohibitive were also addressed by the authorities when they said that AED 2 is the fare for a journey in a single zone, with AED 0.05 per kilometer for extra distance. Hafilat cards can be easily topped up online or at kiosks across the city so passengers don’t have to deal with cash and can travel seamlessly.

## Journey Experience

A scenic trip through Abu Dhabi is Bus L43 from Al Maryah to Qaryat Al Beri Souq. Passengers along the way enjoy views of Electra Park and Mishrif Mall, as well as local life in residential neighborhoods.

The route finishes in the vicinity of the charming waterfront of Qaryat Al Beri Souq, where Arabian traditions and modern shopping and dining are combined together. This is a rare view of Abu Dhabi’s modernity and tradition. It’s a great way for tourists to look around the key sights of the city but without missing the culture proper. Whether for sightseeing or daily commuting the journey can be said to be among the comfortable and easy way of transportation across the city.

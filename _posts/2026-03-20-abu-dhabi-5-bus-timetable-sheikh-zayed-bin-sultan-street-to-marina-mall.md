Ticket Pricing: Travel on Bus 5 is facilitated through the Hafilat Card, which is Abu Dhabi’s cashless payment system. The fare for traveling within one zone is AED 2, with an additional charge of AED 0.05 per kilometer. Passengers can conveniently top up their Hafilat cards online or at kiosks across the city, ensuring a smooth and efficient payment process.

## Accessibility and Services

Bus 5 is designed to accommodate passengers with special needs, ensuring an inclusive travel experience. Key features include:

- Ramps for wheelchair access.

- Designated seating for those with mobility issues.
- Audio-visual aids to assist passengers with hearing or vision impairments.

This commitment to accessibility makes Bus 5 a suitable option for all commuters.

## Travel Tips for Bus 5

- Plan Your Journey: Check the bus timings in advance to minimize waiting times, especially during peak hours.

- Use the Hafilat Smart Card: This allows for efficient, cashless payments on all Abu Dhabi buses.
- Stay Updated: Download the AD Mobility app for real-time bus updates and schedules.
- Travel During Off-Peak Hours: For a more comfortable experience, try to avoid traveling during the morning and afternoon rush hours.

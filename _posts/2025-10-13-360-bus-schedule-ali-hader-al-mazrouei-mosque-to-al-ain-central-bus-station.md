Abu Dhabi’s extensive public transport network is designed to connect various parts of the city and beyond, and Reg Bus 360 is a prime example of that. This route links Ali Hader Al Mazrouei Mosque to Al Ain Central Bus Station, passing through key residential, commercial, and community hubs. Whether you're commuting to work, visiting educational institutions, or exploring Al Ain, Bus 360 provides a reliable and affordable way to travel through Abu Dhabi’s diverse landscape.

## Operational Details

Bus 360 operates frequently throughout the day, offering regular services in both directions.

Bus 360 Timetable

For the complete schedule, you can visit the official AD Mobility website or follow this link to access Bus 360 timings.

Ticket Pricing: The fare for the bus is AED 2.00 plus AED 0.05 per kilometer, with payments conveniently made using the Hafilat Smart Card. This card can be easily topped up at kiosks or online, offering a hassle-free, cashless option for frequent travelers.

## Route Overview

The 360 Bus starts at Ali Hader Al Mazrouei Mosque and ends at Al Ain Central Bus Station, stopping at important community facilities, schools, and markets along the way. Below are some key stops along the route:
1. Ali Hader Al Mazrouei Mosque  The starting point of the route.

2. Souq  A vibrant marketplace.

3. Atfal Hilal Kindergarten  Serving the local education community.

4. Sultan Bin Zaied Mosque  A prominent place of worship.

5. Nahil Park  A recreational park for the community.

6. Nahil Petrol Station  A convenient stop for fuel and amenities.

7. Nahil Girls School  A local educational institution.

8. Salem Obaid Al Ameri Mosque  A peaceful mosque stop.

9. Gate 10, Exit 35, Exit 30, and Gate 5  Access points along the route connecting to rural areas.

10. Farm and Wadi Al Hamam  Scenic countryside stops.

11. Fodder Factory  An industrial stop along the route.

12. Khalfan Sultan Al Ketbi Mosque  Another significant mosque on the way.

13. Al Ain School  A stop serving local students.

## Must-See Rides and Attractions

Ferrari World is famous for its incredible rides that cater to every type of visitor, whether you’re looking for thrills, family fun, or Ferrari-themed experiences.
1. Formula Rossa  The World’s Fastest Roller Coaster

-item

-item

3. Mission Ferrari  A High-Speed Dark Ride

-item

## Other Services and Facilities

Ferrari World offers a wide range of amenities to ensure your visit is comfortable and enjoyable:

- Lockers and luggage storage are available at the entrance for AED 3550.

- Stroller rentals for AED 40 and baby changing facilities are located near the entrance.
- Prayer rooms and smoking areas are also accessible on-site.
- Feeling peckish? The park has several dining options, though Mamma Rossella will be closed for maintenance from September 9 to October 3, 2024.

Ferrari World Abu Dhabi is an unmissable experience for visitors looking to combine high-octane thrills with luxury and style. Whether you’re here to ride the world’s fastest roller coaster or enjoy a family-friendly adventure, the park offers something for everyone. Remember to check the park’s ride availability and opening hours before your trip, and take advantage of the free shuttle services for hassle-free transport to and from Yas Island.

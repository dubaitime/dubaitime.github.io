## Accessibility Features

Reg Bus 120 is equipped with accessibility features designed to ensure a smooth journey for everyone. The buses include ramps and designated seating areas for differently-abled passengers. Audio-visual aids are also available to assist those with hearing or vision impairments, ensuring that all passengers have a comfortable and inclusive travel experience.

## Connectivity and Transfer Points

Reg Bus 120 integrates seamlessly with other bus routes at major stops such as Al Maqta Bus Interchange and Abu Dhabi Bus Station, making it easy for passengers to transfer to other buses or continue their journeys. These transfer points connect to other parts of Abu Dhabi and beyond, enhancing the overall convenience of the city’s public transportation network.

## Journey Experience

A trip on the 111 bus route from Saadiyat Public Beach to Mazyad Mall offers a diverse journey through some of Abu Dhabi's most scenic and vibrant areas. Starting at Saadiyat Island, famous for its pristine beaches and cultural landmarks like the Louvre Abu Dhabi, the route provides passengers with beautiful coastal views before moving into the heart of the city.

The journey culminates at Mazyad Mall, a bustling commercial hub in Mohammed Bin Zayed City, known for its variety of shops, restaurants, and family-friendly attractions. This long journey gives a well-rounded glimpse of Abu Dhabi’s urban charm and natural beauty.

## Travel Tips

For the best travel experience, consider traveling during off-peak hours to avoid busy buses, especially if you’re visiting popular spots like Mazyad Mall or Saadiyat Public Beach. Be sure to download the Abu Dhabi Department of Transport app for real-time updates on schedules and routes, which can be helpful for planning your journey. Additionally, keeping a portable phone charger handy ensures that you stay connected while on the go.

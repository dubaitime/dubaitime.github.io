## 1. Bus Route from Mussafah Shabiya to Rashidiya (Best Overall)

This route offers a great balance between cost and travel time, combining bus and metro services for a smooth journey.

- Estimated Total Travel Time: 2 hours 40 minutes

- Cost: AED 32-44

Journey Overview:

Step 1: Start at Mussafah Shabiya Bus Station  2 hours 13 minutes, AED 32-44, Bus: E102 (Regular service), Stop: Al Jafiliya Bus Station.

Step 2: Transfer at Al Jafiliya Bus Station  3 minutes, Walk to Max Metro Station. This brief transfer involves a short walk to connect with the metro service.

Step 3: Ride the Metro to Centerpoint Metro Station  24 minutes, Metro Line: MRed (Regular service). Upon arriving at Centerpoint Metro Station, you will be just a short distance from your final destination in Rashidiya.

- Plan Ahead: Check the latest bus and metro schedules online to avoid any surprises on travel day.

- Keep Essentials Handy: Make sure you have your Nol card ready for easy access to public transport.

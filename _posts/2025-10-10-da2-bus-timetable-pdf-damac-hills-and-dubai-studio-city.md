## Accessibility and Services

The DA2 Bus is designed with passenger comfort and inclusivity in mind. Key accessibility features include:

- Ramps and Low Floors: Facilitating easy access for wheelchair users and passengers with strollers.

- Designated Seating: Reserved seats for the elderly and individuals with special needs.
- Audio-Visual Aids: Announcements and displays to assist passengers with visual or hearing impairments.

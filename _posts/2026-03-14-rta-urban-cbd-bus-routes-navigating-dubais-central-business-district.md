## Dubai Bus Fares

Dubai’s bus fares are zone-based, reflecting the distance traveled. The city is divided into seven zones, and fares vary according to the number of zones crossed. Transfers between buses, metro, trams, and water buses within 30 minutes are charged as a single trip. Delayed transfers incur additional charges.

Fare Payment Methods

Fares are paid using NOL cards, with the following rates:

| Card Type | Fare within 1 Zone | Fare for 2 Adjacent Zones | Fare for More than 2 Zones |
|---|---|---|---|
| Silver Card | AED 3 | AED 5 | AED 7.5 |
| Gold Card | AED 6 | AED 10 | AED 15 |
| Blue Personal Card | AED 3 | AED 5 | AED 7.5 |
| Red Card (Ticket) | AED 4 | AED 6 | AED 8.5 |

Discounts are available for students, social affairs recipients, senior citizens, and holders of blue personal cards. Free travel is available for People of Determination and children aged 5 years or under. Penalties apply for invalid, expired, or unauthorized NOL cards, with fines up to AED 500 for using fake cards.

## General Guidelines on Using Public Buses in Dubai

To ensure a pleasant experience for all passengers, adhere to the following rules:

- Reserved Seating: The three rows behind the driver are reserved for ladies and children.

- Prohibitions: Eating, drinking, smoking, spitting, littering, and carrying animals or heavy luggage are prohibited.
- Noise and Instruments: Avoid creating noise or playing musical instruments on the bus.

## Comparison of RTA Dubai Bus Route Types

| Bus Route Type | Purpose | Coverage Area | Key Characteristics |
|---|---|---|---|
| RTA Dubai Express Bus Routes | Fast and direct connections to major destinations | Major hubs and key locations within Dubai | Fewer stops; faster travel time; prefixed with "E" |
| RTA Dubai Feeder Bus Routes | Connects residential areas to main metro stations | Residential neighborhoods and metro stations | Smaller, local routes feeding into larger transport systems |
| RTA Urban CBD Bus Routes | Serves the Central Business District (CBD) | Central business areas of Dubai | High frequency; caters to office commuters |
| RTA Urban Bus Routes | General city transportation | Various city areas across Dubai | Extensive network; connects different neighborhoods and commercial areas |
| RTA Intercity Routes | Links Dubai to neighboring cities | Routes to cities like Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ajman, etc. | Longer routes; prefixed with "E" for intercity travel |

Dubai’s Urban CBD bus routes offer an essential link between key business and residential areas in the city. With comprehensive coverage, modern facilities, and efficient service, these routes ensure that navigating Dubai’s Central Business District is both convenient and comfortable.

The fare for Bus 44 follows the standard Dubai bus fare structure. ## Bus 44 Station Overview

### Ghubaiba Bus Station · 2

Ghubaiba Bus Station is one of Dubai's busiest and most significant transport hubs, located in the Al Ghubaiba area. This station is known for its comprehensive facilities, including ample seating, ticketing services, and real-time bus schedule displays. Its strategic location makes it a central point for passengers traveling across Dubai, with easy access to nearby markets, historic sites, and the Dubai Creek.

### Belhasa Driving Center Bus Stop Intersection

The Belhasa Driving Center Bus Stop serves as a key intersection for Bus Route 44 and Bus Route C4. This location provides essential connectivity between these routes, facilitating efficient travel for passengers in the area. With its strategic position, the bus stop offers convenient transfer options and access to various destinations, making it an important hub for daily commutes and travel needs.

### Al Rashidiya, Street 33A, Al Jahiz Boys School Bus Stop Intersection

The Al Rashidiya, Street 33A, Al Jahiz Boys School Bus Stop is a key intersection point where Bus Routes 44 and 11A converge. This strategic location provides efficient connections between different parts of the city, facilitating convenient travel for residents and commuters in the Al Rashidiya area. The stop's positioning ensures accessible and seamless transit options for those utilizing these bus routes.

### Rashidiya Bus Station Parking

Rashidiya Bus Station Parking serves as a vital transit point located near the Rashidiya Metro Station, offering convenient access to the northern parts of Dubai. This station is equipped with essential amenities such as parking facilities, sheltered waiting areas, and real-time updates on bus schedules.

Its proximity to the Rashidiya Metro makes it an important interchange for passengers looking to connect between bus and metro services. The station’s accessibility and connectivity make it a crucial hub for residents and visitors traveling to various parts of the city.

## Bus Route Details

The Al Warqa’a, Mass Supermarket Bus Stop is served by several bus routes, each offering frequent and reliable service throughout the day.

- F23 Route: This route connects Al Warqa’a with Al Nahda 1, Terminus. Buses start at 05:13 AM and run approximately every 15 minutes until 08:42 PM. The final bus departs at 08:29 PM.

- 20A Route: Serving Al Warsan 3 and RTA Staff Accommodation, this route begins service at 05:34 AM with buses running every 30 minutes. The last bus leaves at 03:05 PM.
- 24 Route: Running between Al Nahda, The Sheffield Private School 1 and Dubai Festival City, Festival Centre, this route operates from 05:36 AM, with frequent buses every 15 minutes. Service continues until 03:51 PM.
- X22 Route: Connecting Business Bay Metro Landside and Al Qusais Industrial Area 2 Terminus, the X22 route starts at 06:12 AM, with buses every 15 minutes until 08:00 PM.
- F23A Route: This route provides service to Stadium Bus Station. Buses start at 06:13 AM and operate every 15 minutes throughout the day, with the last bus at 04:32 PM.

Each route offers specific destinations and times, ensuring convenient and efficient travel options for various needs.

## Bus Ticket Price

The Dubai 81 bus fare likely falls around AED 3 as it operates within a limited area (The Dubai Mall to Mall Of Emirates).

## Bus Booking

While Dubai's RTA (Roads and Transport Authority) primarily operates on a first-come, first-served basis for bus services, passengers can check for real-time bus schedules and updates through official RTA channels and apps. Online booking options for specific bus routes may vary, so it's advisable to check the latest information for any updates or changes in service offerings.

## Bus Route Details

Al Ghubaiba Bus Station is a major transit hub, providing connections to various parts of Dubai and beyond. The station is serviced by numerous routes, ensuring frequent and reliable transport options. Key routes include:

- 29: Connects to Dubai Mall from Bay 1.

- 44: Serves Rashidiya from Bay 2.
- 61: Travels to Samari Residences from Bay 3.
- C05: Links to Gold Souq from Bay 4.
- N55: Provides late-night service to Al Maktoum International Airport from Bay 6.
- 61D: Connects to Nadd Al Shiba Clinic from Bay 6.
- X13: Serves Al Satwa from Bay 7.
- 8: Connects to Ibn Battuta from Bay 7.
- E100: Provides service to Abu Dhabi Central from Bay 9.
- E201: Travels to Al Ain from Bay 12.
- E306: Links to Al Jubail, Sharjah, from Bay 14.

These routes operate throughout the day, offering flexible options for both early and late travelers. For real-time bus arrival information, passengers can SMS ‘D’ followed by the bus stop ID to 5223.

Ticket Pricing: Payments for Bus 210 are made through the Hafilat Card, Abu Dhabi’s cashless payment system. For travel within one zone, the fare is AED 2, with additional charges of AED 0.05 per kilometer. Passengers can conveniently top up their Hafilat cards online or at kiosks across the city for a smooth, cash-free experience.

## Accessibility and Services

Bus 210 is equipped to accommodate passengers with special needs, ensuring that everyone can enjoy a comfortable journey. The buses feature:

- Ramps for wheelchair access.

- Designated seating for those with mobility issues.
- Audio-visual aids for passengers with hearing or vision impairments.

This makes Bus 210 an inclusive option for all commuters.

## Journey Experience

A trip on Bus 210 from Al Shahama Bus Station to Dalma Mall offers a scenic and varied journey through Abu Dhabi’s urban and suburban landscapes.

Notable along the route is Burjeel Medical City, a leading healthcare facility, and the Bohra Islamic Cultural Complex, highlighting the area’s cultural diversity. Passing through residential areas like Al Reef, with its schools and local markets, the journey concludes at Dalma Mall, one of Abu Dhabi’s largest shopping centers, offering both practical travel and a unique view of the city's development.

## Travel Tips for Bus 210

- Plan Ahead: Check the bus timings to avoid unnecessary waiting, especially during peak hours.

- Use the Hafilat Smart Card: This allows for quick, cashless payment on all Abu Dhabi buses.
- Monitor Bus Updates: Use the AD Mobility app for real-time updates and schedules to optimize your journey.
- Avoid Peak Times: If you prefer a quieter ride, avoid traveling during the busiest periods in the morning and afternoon.

## Bus 7 Station Overview

### Al Quoz Bus Station · 1

The Al Quoz Bus Station is a central transit point serving the Al Quoz industrial and residential areas. It is strategically located to provide easy access to various commercial and business zones within Al Quoz. The station features modern amenities such as sheltered waiting areas and real-time bus schedule updates, ensuring a comfortable and efficient travel experience. Its position makes it a vital link for both local commuters and those traveling to the broader Dubai region.

### Business Bay Metro Station Seaside Bus Stop Intersection

Business Bay Metro Station Seaside Bus Stop is a crucial intersection where Bus Routes 7, 9, 14, 91, and F20 converge. This stop serves as a vital connection point for passengers traveling within the Business Bay area and to various destinations across Dubai. Its strategic location near major business and residential areas makes it a convenient choice for daily commuters and travelers seeking easy access to key parts of the city.

### Satwa, Masjid

This station is conveniently located near a prominent mosque, serving the local community and visitors. It provides essential transportation links within the Satwa area and to other parts of Dubai.

## Journey Experience

The 7 bus route from Al Quoz Bus Station to Satwa Masjid and back offers a long but fascinating journey through Dubai, blending industrial, commercial, and community life.

As the bus nears Satwa, the bustling streets become more vibrant with small shops, local eateries, and lively markets, reflecting the neighborhood’s cultural diversity. Satwa Masjid, located in this culturally rich area, serves as the endpoint before the return trip. Despite its length, the route provides a unique perspective on Dubai’s urban and community dynamics, making it a rewarding experience for those interested in exploring the city’s diverse character.

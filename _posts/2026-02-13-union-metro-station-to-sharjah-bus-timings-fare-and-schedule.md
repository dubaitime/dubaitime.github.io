Starting from Union Metro Station in Deira, Dubai, you can easily reach Sharjah’s bustling areas by taking advantage of the efficient public transit options. This guide provides an overview of the best bus routes, estimated fares, and tips for a smooth journey from Union Metro Station to Sharjah.

## 1. Direct Bus Route from Union Metro Station to Sharjah (Best Option)

For a straightforward journey, the direct E303 bus from Union Metro Station offers a balance of speed and cost, making it the top choice.

- Estimated Travel Time: 44 minutes

- Fare: AED 58

Journey Overview:

Step 1: Begin with a short 9-minute walk to Union Square Bus Station 5.

Step3: Located in central Sharjah, this is a great starting point for exploring local attractions and amenities.

With minimal transfers, this route is the quickest and most convenient option.

## Travel Tips

- Best Time to Travel: Mornings and early afternoons are generally less crowded. Avoid peak hours (79 AM and 57 PM) to reduce wait times and enjoy a quieter journey.

- Landmarks to Look Out For: If you have time, explore Al Qasba in Sharjah, famous for its cafes, waterfront, and Ferris wheel, which is just a short distance from the final bus stop.
- Packing Essentials: Remember to bring your Nol card for convenient fare payments, a water bottle, and comfortable clothing for the walk between transfer points.

Some other interesting trips you can explore are from Union Metro Station to Global Village and the trip from Union Metro Station to Al Ghubaiba Bus Station.

Choosing public transportation from Union Metro Station to Sharjah is a cost-effective and straightforward way to travel. Whether you opt for the direct E303 bus route or the metro-bus combination, each option provides an efficient connection between Dubai and Sharjah. Enjoy the journey and take in the sights of the city along the way! Safe travels.

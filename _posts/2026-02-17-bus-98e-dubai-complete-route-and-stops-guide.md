## Bus Ticket Price

The fare for the 98E bus route within 1 zone is AED 3, payable using a Nol card, which is mandatory for all public transport in Dubai. Discounts are available for students, senior citizens, and people of determination. This affordable fare structure makes commuting via the 98E bus a cost-effective option for daily travelers.

## Bus Booking

The 98E bus operates on a first-come, first-served basis, with no pre-booking required—passengers can board at any designated stop. To plan your journey, the RTA Dubai app offers real-time tracking and route information. For those seeking more convenience, ride-hailing apps like Careem provide flexible travel options, especially during peak hours when buses may be crowded, ensuring a hassle-free ride along the 98E route.

## Bus 98E Station Overview

### Al Quoz Bus Station · 2

Al Quoz Bus Station serves as a major transit hub in the bustling industrial and residential area of Al Quoz. The station is equipped with essential amenities such as seating areas and real-time bus information, ensuring a comfortable and efficient travel experience.

### White Crown Tower Bus Stop Intersection

The White Crown Tower Bus Stop is a key transit point where Bus Routes 21, 98E, F11, and F28 intersect. This station offers passengers easy access to various destinations across Dubai, enhancing connectivity for both daily commuters and visitors. Its strategic location near important landmarks and commercial areas makes it a convenient stop for those traveling through the city, providing seamless transfers between these bus routes.

### Safest Way

The Safest Way stop is conveniently located near a popular supermarket in Al Quoz, making it a busy station for both shoppers and commuters. This stop caters to the daily needs of residents and workers in the area, providing easy access to public transportation. The station is well-maintained and strategically positioned to serve those looking to travel quickly and efficiently within the Al Quoz district.

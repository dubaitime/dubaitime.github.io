Whether you're commuting for work, shopping, or leisure, Al Qusais Bus Station provides a reliable and convenient option for daily travelers.

## Bus Route Details

Al Qusais Bus Station serves as a crucial hub in Dubai's transportation network, providing access to various key destinations. Below are the primary bus routes available at the station:

- Bus Route 33: Bay 1  Connecting passengers to Al Ghubaiba.

- Bus Route 32C: Bay 2  Linking Al Qusais Bus Station with Al Satwa.
- Bus Route 63E: Bay 3  Providing service between Al Qusais and Al Quoz.
- Bus Route 13B: Bay 4  Traveling to the Gold Souq.
- Bus Route 34: Bay 5  Connecting to Al Khawaneej 2 and Etisalat.
- Bus Route 97: Bay 6  Providing transportation to Jabal Ali Industrial Area.
- Bus Route 13A and 13D: Bay 7 and 8  Both heading to Gold Souq.

For real-time bus arrival updates, passengers can SMS 'D' followed by the bus stop ID to 5223.

## Nearby Destinations

The strategic location of Al Qusais Bus Station offers convenient access to a variety of local amenities, making it a valuable transit point for commuters and visitors in the area. The station is close to restaurants, major buildings, and key streets, facilitating easy travel within Dubai.

Restaurants

- Al Salama Restaurant: A popular spot offering local and international dishes.

- Aziz Afghan Restaurant: Known for its authentic Afghan cuisine.
- Azlan Darabar Restaurant: Offers a mix of flavorful Middle Eastern and South Asian food.

Landmarks

- Dulsco Labour Accommodation: A prominent residential complex in the area.

- Shaklan Hypermarket: A local supermarket offering a wide range of products.
- Transguard Group Accommodation: A key housing facility near the station.

Streets

- Algeria Street: A major road providing easy access to the surrounding area.

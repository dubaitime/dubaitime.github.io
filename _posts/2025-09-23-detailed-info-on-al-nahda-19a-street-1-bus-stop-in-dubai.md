## Bus Route Details

- 22 Route: This route connects Al Nahda with Deira City Center Bus Station and Al Nahda 1. Buses commence service at 05:00 AM, running approximately every 30 minutes until the last bus at 08:00 PM. It provides a crucial link for commuters traveling between Al Nahda and Deira City Center.

- 24 Route: The 24 route links Al Nahda 2 with Dubai Festival City, starting from 05:15 AM and operating with 30-minute intervals throughout the day, concluding at 08:15 PM.
- F08 Route: Connecting Dubai Festival City with Al Nahda 2, this route begins service at 05:30 AM and operates every 30 minutes until 08:30 PM. It offers a reliable option for passengers traveling between these two key areas.
- X13 Route: Running from Lulu Village to Al Satwa Bus Station, the X13 route starts at 05:45 AM and runs at 30-minute intervals until 08:45 PM. This route serves those traveling across various central locations within Dubai.

Traveling between Dubai and Al Ain City is a common journey for both residents and tourists in the UAE, thanks to the efficient public transportation options. If you are looking for a reliable and affordable way to make the trip, this guide will outline key bus routes, fares, schedules, and helpful tips to ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience. Whether traveling for leisure or work, you’ll find everything you need to know about the bus options available.

## 1. Line 104 and E201 Buses - Best Option

Combining the Line 104 bus from Global Village with the E201 bus offers a convenient route to Al Ain City with just one transfer. This route is optimal for those looking for a balance between travel time and minimal transfers.

- Route: Global Village (Dubai) to Al Ain Central Bus Station (Al Ain)

- Estimated Travel Time: 2 hours 56 minutes
- Fare: AED 2851
- Frequency: Regularly throughout the day

Journey Overview:

Step 1: Begin your journey at Global Village in Dubai. Take the Line 104 bus heading towards Al Ghubaiba Bus Station. The trip takes about 1 hour and stops at key points such as Rebat Street.

- Bus Line: 104

- Duration: Approximately 1 hour
- Key Stops: Al Ghubaiba Bus Station, Al Karama

Step 2: Transfer at Al Ghubaiba Bus Station. From here, board the E201 bus bound for Al Ain. This bus operates regularly and takes about 2 hours and 15 minutes, stopping at various landmarks along the way.

- Bus Line: E201

- Key Stops: Wasl Club, Nad Al Sheba, Al Qattara Oasis, Al Ain Gift Markets
- Duration: 2 hours 15 minutes

Step 3: Arrive at Al Ain Central Bus Station. From here, you can easily explore the city or take local transport to your destination.

## 2. Line 102, Metro, and E201 Buses - Alternate Option

- Route: Global Village (Dubai) via Metro and E201 to Al Ain Central Bus Station

- Estimated Travel Time: 3 hours 4 minutes
- Fare: AED 3461

Journey Overview:

Step 1: Take the Line 102 bus from Global Village to Al Ghubaiba Bus Station. This leg of the trip lasts about 1 hour.

- Bus Line: 102

- Duration: 1 hour

Step 2: From Al Ghubaiba Bus Station, take the metro to Union Metro Station and transfer to the E201 bus heading to Al Ain.

- Metro Transfer: Approximately 45 minutes

- Bus Line: E201
- Duration: 2 hours 15 minutes

Step 3: Arrive at Al Ain Central Bus Station, ready to explore the attractions of Al Ain City.

## 4. Line 103 and E303 Buses - Longest Route

If you start from Union Metro Station and are not in a hurry, this route offers a scenic journey to Al Ain.

- Route: Union Metro Station (Dubai) to Al Ain Central Bus Station

- Estimated Travel Time: 5 hours 14 minutes
- Fare: AED 4156

Journey Overview:

Step 1: From Union Metro Station, walk to Union Square Bus Station and take the Line 103 bus to Al Karama.

- Bus Line: 103

- Duration: 1 hour

Step 2: Transfer to E303 bus from Union Square Bus Station to Sharjah’s Al Jubail Bus Station, then continue with Line 118 to Al Ain Central Bus Station.

- Key Stops: Salah Al Din Metro, Sharjah Mamzar, Al Ain Gift Markets

- Duration: 2 hours 45 minutes

## Travel Tips

- Check Schedules: Buses have fixed timings, so planning ahead will save you time. Use the RTA app or website for real-time updates.

- Avoid Peak Hours: Rush hours (5 PM to 7 PM) can make buses crowded; consider traveling during off-peak times.
- Bring Essentials: The long journey can be tiring, so bring water and snacks to stay refreshed.
- Nearby Attractions: If starting at Union Metro Station, consider visiting Deira’s markets before departure.

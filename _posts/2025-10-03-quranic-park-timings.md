Opened in 2019, this park offers visitors a serene environment to explore the stories and miracles of the Quran in an interactive and educational setting. Contents

### Quranic Park Timings

Quranic Park is located at Al Khawaneej, [Dubai, UAE](/category/dubai/), and the park's opening hours are as follows:- Saturday to Thursday: 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM

- Friday: 3:00 PM to 10:00 PM

However, key attractions within the park, such as the Glass House and Cave of Miracles, have their own schedules:- Saturday to Thursday: 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM

- Friday: 3:00 PM to 9:00 PM![Quranic Park Dubai](https://cdn.emzeth.com/img/2024/06/Quranic-Park-Dubai.jpg)

One of the main highlights of the park, the Cave of Miracles, offers an immersive experience through interactive displays showcasing seven significant miracles mentioned in the Quran.

Also known as the Quranic Botanical Garden, this climate-controlled greenhouse houses 29 different plants and trees referenced in the Quran and Hadith.

From olive trees to pomegranates, each plant is labeled with information about its benefits and Quranic significance. The Glass House provides a tranquil environment for visitors to learn about these plants and their uses??.

### The Lake

This beautiful lake represents the miracle of Prophet Musa (Moses) parting the Red Sea. Visitors can walk through a pathway in the middle of the lake, reflecting on the significance of this event while enjoying the serene surroundings?.

### The Orchards

Scattered throughout the park are 12 orchards, each dedicated to a different plant mentioned in the Quran.

Quranic Park is designed to ensure a comfortable and enjoyable experience for all visitors.

- Outdoor Theatre: A venue for cultural events such as concerts and plays.
- Solar Trees: Innovative structures that provide shade and are powered by solar panels.
- On-site WiFi: Free Wi-Fi throughout the park to keep visitors connected.
- Restrooms: Clean and conveniently located throughout the park.
- Food and Beverage Vendors: Various options for refreshments.
- Shaded Seating Areas: Ideal for taking a break and enjoying the park's tranquil atmosphere.
- Playground: A fun area for children.
- Prayer Rooms: Available for visitors to perform their daily prayers.
- Jogging Track: A soft track around the park's perimeter for jogging enthusiasts?

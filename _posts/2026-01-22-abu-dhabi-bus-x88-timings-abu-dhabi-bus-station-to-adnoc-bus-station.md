## Journey Experience

It was a long journey, but enjoyable journey on the X88 bus from Abu Dhabi Bus Station to ADNOC Bus Station in Abu Dhabi. That route presented views of the city’s convivial mix of modernity and tradition, including a sliver of the iconic Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque, a magnificent Islamic ode to architecture.

When it’s comfortable and accessible, or even, extending that ride for hours doesn’t seem so bad, and the perfect bus to do this on is one you’ll have made friends with, especially if you’re looking to know Abu Dhabi’s other charms.

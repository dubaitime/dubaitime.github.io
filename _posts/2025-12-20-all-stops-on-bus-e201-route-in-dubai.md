## Bus E201 Station Overview

### Ghubaiba Bus Station · 12

Ghubaiba Bus Station · 12 is a pivotal transit hub in Dubai, where Bus E201 makes a crucial stop. Located in the vibrant Ghubaiba area, this station is equipped with modern amenities such as comfortable seating, ticket counters, and real-time schedule displays.

### Al Ain Bus Station

Al Ain Bus Station serves as a key terminal for Bus E201, providing a vital link between Dubai and the Al Ain region. This station features essential facilities including waiting areas, ticketing services, and local amenities. Its role in connecting major cities within the UAE makes it an important stop for travelers heading to or from Al Ain. The station's comprehensive services and strategic location facilitate convenient and efficient travel, supporting both long-distance commutes and regional connections.

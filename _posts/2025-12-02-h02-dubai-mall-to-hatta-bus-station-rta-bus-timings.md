Traveling between Dubai and Hatta is now more convenient with the H02 bus route operated by the Roads and Transport Authority (RTA). This guide will give you all the information you need about the H02 bus timings, fares, and features. Whether you're a resident or a tourist, this comfortable and affordable bus ride will take you through the scenic landscapes of the UAE in just two hours.

## Travel Duration

The total journey from Dubai Mall to Hatta Bus Station takes around 2 hours, depending on traffic.

## H02 Bus Route Overview

The H02 bus route is designed to connect travelers from Dubai Mall to Hatta Bus Station in a comfortable and affordable way. The bus departs every two hours, with fares starting at AED 25 per ride. On this journey, the H02 bus makes six stops, ensuring accessibility to various points of interest:
1. Dubai Mall, Main Entrance 1

2. Hatta, DM Staff Accommodation

3. Hatta, Hatta Fort Hotel

4. Hatta, Emirates NBD

5. Hatta, Sheikh Khalifa Masjid

6. Hatta, Bus Station

Some other Dubai bus routes you can check out include the H04 bus route, which runs from Hatta Bus Station to Hatta Dam, as well as the 55 bus route, which travels from Al Satwa Bus Station to International City.

## H02 Bus Fare and NOL Card Usage

One of the biggest advantages of the H02 bus is its affordability. The fare for a one-way trip is AED 25, payable via the RTA’s NOL card system.

## Onboard Features

The H02 bus comes equipped with several features to make your journey more comfortable:

- Air conditioning: Stay cool during the ride, even during the hottest months.

- Reclining seats: Adjust your seat for a more relaxed journey.
- Free Wi-Fi: Enjoy uninterrupted internet access throughout the trip.
- Priority seating: Designated seats for women, children, and those with special needs.

## Additional Tips for a Smooth Journey

- Download the RTA App: Stay updated with live bus timings and route changes by using the RTA’s app.

- Avoid peak hours: The bus can get crowded during peak times, so plan your trip for early morning or mid-afternoon to avoid congestion.
- Bring your NOL card: Make sure you have enough credit on your NOL card for the ride, as cash payments are not accepted.

## Journey Experience

The H02 bus route from Dubai Mall to Hatta Bus Station offers a long but comfortable journey, covering 130 kilometers in approximately two hours. As the bus passes through urban Dubai and transitions into the serene landscapes of Hatta, passengers are treated to a unique experience.

Hatta itself is known for its stunning mountain views and outdoor activities like kayaking at Hatta Dam and exploring the historic Hatta Heritage Village. This route not only provides a convenient and affordable travel option but also gives passengers a scenic introduction to one of the UAE's hidden gems.

The H02 bus makes traveling between Dubai and Hatta both easy and affordable. Whether you're exploring Hatta's attractions or commuting, the H02 offers a reliable, comfortable journey at a fraction of the cost of a taxi. With air-conditioned coaches and convenient stops, passengers can sit back, relax, and enjoy the scenic ride through the UAE’s diverse landscapes.

## Bus 8 Station Overview

### Al Baraha Bus Station

Al Baraha Bus Station is a key transit point located in the bustling Al Baraha neighborhood. Serving as a major hub for local and intercity bus routes, it offers passengers easy access to surrounding areas and key city destinations. The station is well-equipped with amenities such as seating areas, ticket counters, and restrooms, ensuring a comfortable experience for travelers.

### Ibn Battuta Bus Station · 1

Ibn Battuta Bus Station is strategically positioned near the famous Ibn Battuta Mall, providing a convenient link for shoppers, commuters, and tourists alike. As a central hub in the Jebel Ali area, it connects passengers to various parts of Dubai. The station is modern and spacious, offering facilities such as digital information screens, ample seating, and easy connections to the metro and other bus routes.

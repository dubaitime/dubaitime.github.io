## Bus Ticket Price

The fare for Bus Route F81 typically ranges from AED 4 to AED 7, depending on the distance traveled. This pricing structure makes the route an economical choice for daily commutes and occasional trips within Dubai.

## Bus Booking

While Bus F81 does not offer online booking options, passengers can purchase tickets directly at the bus station or on board the bus. For a more convenient travel experience, the RTA’s Nol card system is accepted on this route, allowing for easy and efficient payment. The Nol card can be used across all public transportation in Dubai, providing a seamless travel experience.

## Intersecting at Damascus Road 3 Bus Stop

The Damascus Road 3 Bus Stop serves as a major intersection for multiple bus routes, including 17, 43, 13B, C26, F8, F81, and X28. This key transit point provides seamless connections between these diverse routes, offering passengers efficient travel options across different areas. The station’s central location and well-maintained facilities enhance the convenience and accessibility of public transportation in the region.

## Al Khulafa’a Al Rashideen Boys School 2 Intersection

The Al Khulafa’a Al Rashideen Boys School 2 bus stop is a crucial transit point where several key bus routes intersect. This location connects Bus Route 43, F81, X28, and C18, offering passengers seamless travel options across various parts of the city. Its strategic position facilitates easy transfers and enhances connectivity, making it an important hub for commuters and travelers in the area.

## Touris Destination Along the Way

Bus Route F81 passes through several notable areas and landmarks in Dubai:
1. Dubai Grand Hotel: Located on Damascus Road, this hotel offers comfortable accommodations and is situated close to the vibrant commercial and cultural areas of Dubai.

2. Union Cooperative Society: A well-known retail destination in Al Nahda, providing a variety of goods from groceries to household items.

3. Al Nahda Metro Station: Although not a tourist attraction itself, it connects to various parts of the city and can facilitate access to nearby attractions.

4. Dubai Airport Free Zone Metro: This area is significant for its business hubs and is close to the Dubai Airport Free Zone, which houses several multinational companies.

5. Galadari Driving School: Known for providing comprehensive driving courses and licensing services, this is a practical destination for residents seeking driving education.

While the route primarily serves industrial and residential areas, these stops offer convenience for accessing nearby attractions, retail centers, and essential services.

## Journey Experience

A journey on the F81 bus route from Qusais Industrial Area, Terminus to Al Nahda Metro offers a glimpse into both the industrial and residential sides of Dubai.

The route also passes through a range of local shops and eateries, giving a sense of everyday life in these neighborhoods. Once at Al Nahda Metro, riders can connect to the metro system, making it a convenient option for commuters. Along the way, you might spot nearby attractions such as the Al Nahda Pond Park, a popular spot for families and fitness enthusiasts. This trip offers both practicality and an authentic feel of Dubai’s urban landscape.

## Traveling with Careem

With the Careem app, you can easily travel to Al Nahda Metro, the furthest point on the F81 bus route starting from Qusais Industrial Area, Terminus. The ride typically costs between AED 30 and AED 35, providing a convenient and comfortable option for reaching this key transit hub.

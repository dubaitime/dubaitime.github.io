## E700 Bus Route Schedule and Details

The E700 bus route operates between Fujairah Bus Station and Union Square Bus Station in Dubai. Here are the general departure timings:

- First bus from Fujairah: 5:30 AM

- Last bus from Fujairah: 12:00 AM (midnight, next day)
- Frequency: Approximately every 90 minutes
- Travel Time: About 2 hours and 30 minutes, subject to traffic conditions
- Ticket Price: 25 AED per person

## Bus Features and Amenities

The E700 bus is equipped with several features to enhance your travel experience:

- Air Conditioning: Keeps passengers comfortable during the summer heat.

- Adjustable Seating: Allows passengers to recline and adjust their seats for comfort.
- Priority Seating: Specially marked seats for pregnant women, senior citizens, and people with disabilities.
- Free Wi-Fi: Available for passengers to stay connected during their journey.
- Onboard Displays: Announce the next stop, helping new passengers navigate their journey.

## Tips for Traveling

- Arrive Early: Arriving at the bus station at least 30 minutes before departure ensures a smooth boarding process.
- Comfort: Pack essentials for a comfortable journey, including water, snacks, and entertainment for longer trips.

Traveling from Abu Dhabi to Salalah by bus offers an opportunity to experience the diverse landscapes between the UAE and Oman.

## Overview of Bus Travel: Duration, Cost, and Distance

The journey by bus from Abu Dhabi to Salalah is a lengthy but comfortable experience, spanning approximately 21 hours and 10 minutes. This extended travel time allows passengers to appreciate the changing scenery along the route. The cost of a bus ticket ranges from AED 128 to AED 191, making it an affordable choice for those looking to explore Oman. The distance between Abu Dhabi and Salalah is around 832.0 km, with a road distance of approximately 1244.5 km.

## Catching the Bus

- From Abu Dhabi: Bus services from Abu Dhabi to Salalah, operated by Mwasalat, depart from Abu Dhabi Central Bus Station.

- Arrival in Salalah: The buses arrive at Al-Buraimi Bus Station in Salalah.

## Additional Tips

- Check for Updates: Before embarking on your journey, check the latest schedules and travel updates on the Mwasalat website or contact their customer service for any changes or delays.
- Comfort Essentials: Pack essentials for a long journey, such as water, snacks, a travel pillow, and entertainment like books or music, to make your trip more comfortable.
- Prepare for Transfers: Since there are no direct buses, be prepared for transfers at various points along the route. Keep your belongings secure and allow extra time for transfers.
- Travel Documents: Ensure you have all necessary travel documents, including your passport and any required visas, ready for inspection at checkpoints.
- Local Currency: Carry some Omani rials for any purchases or expenses during your stay in Salalah, as not all places may accept credit cards.

## Travel Tips for the Khor Fakkan–Sharjah Route
- **Plan Ahead:** Bus services such as **Bus 116** operate on fixed schedules. Checking departure times in advance using official transport websites or mobile apps can significantly reduce waiting time.  
- **Avoid Rush Hours:** Morning and late afternoon periods are usually the busiest. If possible, travel outside peak hours to enjoy a more comfortable ride.  
- **Familiarize Yourself with Stops:** Knowing the main stops along the route helps prevent confusion. The journey typically starts at **Al Zubara Bus Station (Khor Fakkan)** and ends at **Al Jubail Bus Station (Sharjah)**, passing through several key locations along the way.  
- **Prepare Your Payment:** Carry a prepaid **Nol card** and some cash as backup. Payment methods can vary, so it’s best to confirm accepted options beforehand to avoid inconvenience.  
- **Comfort and Safety:** Buses are air-conditioned and designed for passenger comfort, but they can become crowded during busy times. Boarding early increases your chances of finding a seat and enjoying a safer, more relaxed journey.

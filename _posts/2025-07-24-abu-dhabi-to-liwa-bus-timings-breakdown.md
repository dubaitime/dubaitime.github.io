Planning a trip from Abu Dhabi to Liwa? Whether you're a resident or a tourist eager to explore the majestic landscapes of the Liwa Oasis, taking the bus is one of the most convenient and affordable options. This guide will break down the essential details for your journey, ensuring you have a smooth, hassle-free trip.

## Bus Route: Abu Dhabi to Liwa (Best Overall Option)

For travelers looking for a budget-friendly and reliable way to get to Liwa, the X60 bus route is your best bet. Here are the key details:

- Estimated Travel Time: 3 hours and 15 minutes

- Fare Estimate: AED 28 - 43
- Bus Frequency: Every 4 hours

Journey Overview:

Step 1: Head to the Abu Dhabi Central Bus Station.

Step 2: Take the X60 bus towards Mezaira'a Bus Station, Liwa.

## Tips for a Smooth Journey

- Check the Timings: Buses run every four hours, so it’s important to plan your trip accordingly. You can check the latest schedules on the Abu Dhabi Department of Transport’s website or app. Or you can check the Bus X60 Timings here.

- Bring Essentials: The journey is over three hours long, so bring water, snacks, and something to entertain you during the ride. While the buses are comfortable, you won’t have access to food or drinks onboard.
- Plan Ahead for Liwa: Once you reach Liwa, public transport options are limited. Make arrangements in advance for taxis or other transport if you plan to explore the area further. Many travelers also rent cars to better navigate the sprawling desert region.

With regular bus services and affordable fares, the X60 route offers a stress-free way to reach the breathtaking Liwa Oasis. Make sure to plan ahead, check the bus timings, and get ready for a memorable journey to one of the UAE’s hidden gems!

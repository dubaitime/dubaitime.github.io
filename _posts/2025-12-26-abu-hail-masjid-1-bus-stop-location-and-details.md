## Bus Route Details

The Abu Hail, Masjid 1 Bus Stop is serviced by the C14, 13D, and C18 routes, providing frequent connectivity throughout the day.

- C14 Route: Operating between Abu Hail and Al Nahda 1, Sama Residence, buses on this route start at 12:49 PM and run at regular intervals until 3:25 PM, ensuring reliable service for travelers heading to Al Nahda 1.

- 13D Route: Connecting Abu Hail to Al Qusais Bus Station, the 13D route offers service from 1:02 PM to 3:21 PM, with buses arriving every 15-30 minutes.
- C18 Route: Serving the route between Abu Hail and Al Qusais 1, the C18 route runs from 1:08 PM to 3:27 PM, providing consistent travel options throughout its operating hours.

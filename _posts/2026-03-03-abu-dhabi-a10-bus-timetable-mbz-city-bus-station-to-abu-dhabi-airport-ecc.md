Ticket Pricing: Passengers can conveniently pay for A10 bus trips using the Hafilat Card, Abu Dhabi’s cashless payment system. The fare for travel within one zone is AED 2, with an additional charge of AED 0.05 per kilometer for longer journeys. Hafilat Cards can be easily topped up online or at kiosks throughout Abu Dhabi, making the travel experience smooth and efficient.

## Accessibility and Services

The A10 bus is designed to accommodate passengers with special needs, ensuring an inclusive travel experience. The buses feature:

- Ramps for wheelchair access.

- Designated seating for individuals with mobility challenges.
- Audio-visual aids for passengers with hearing or vision impairments.

This commitment to accessibility makes the A10 a suitable option for all commuters.

## Nearby Attractions and Landmarks

Throughout the journey, passengers on the A10 bus can explore several notable attractions and landmarks:
1. Emirates Heritage Club  A cultural hub where visitors can learn about the UAE's traditions and heritage.

2. Mazyad Mall and Capital Mall  Popular shopping destinations offering a variety of retail and dining options.

3. Wahat Al Karamah  A beautiful memorial that pays tribute to the sacrifices made by Emirati soldiers, providing a reflective space for visitors.

## Journey Experience

Taking the A10 bus from MBZ City Bus Station to Abu Dhabi Airport ECC offers a convenient journey through diverse sights. Starting in Musaffah, known for its industrial significance, the trip moves through Mohamed Bin Zayed City, showcasing residential neighborhoods and local amenities.

The bus also stops at popular shopping destinations such as Mazyad Mall and Capital Mall, ideal for last-minute retail therapy. Culminating at Abu Dhabi Airport ECC, this route provides practical transportation while offering glimpses of the city’s vibrant mix of modernity and tradition.

## Travel Tips for Bus A10

- Plan Ahead: Check the bus timings to avoid unnecessary waiting, especially during peak hours.

- Use the Hafilat Smart Card: This allows for quick, cashless payment on all Abu Dhabi buses.
- Monitor Bus Updates: Use the AD Mobility app for real-time updates and schedules to optimize your journey.
- Avoid Peak Times: If you prefer a quieter ride, avoid traveling during the busiest periods in the morning and afternoon.

Ticket Pricing: Bus K2 payments are done with Hafilat Card and the standard fare is AED 2 per journey in a single zone. Additional fares may be charged for longer trips. It’s also convenient to recharge your Hafilat card at kiosks or online.

## Route Overview

Bus K2 serves a variety of important stops, provides easy access to major services, landmarks and busy places in Abu Dhabi. Below are the major stops along the route:

1. Khalifa City, Souq: Starting point, a lively market area.

2. Sas Al Nakhl School: Nearby educational institution.

3. Etisalat Data Center: Key telecommunications facility.

4. NMC Royal Hospital: Renowned healthcare facility.

5. Al Nawafil Street: Access to residential areas.

6. Al Tashreef Street: Busy thoroughfare in the city.

7. Al Mashariq Street: Commercial district.

8. Al Nusour Street: Residential neighborhood access.

9. Al Nadheem Street: Important city street.

10. Al Madhhar Street: Key road in the area.

11. ADNOC: Prominent gas station and facility.

## Journey Experience

A fascinating journey through Abu Dhabi was a bus ride on Bus K2 from Khalifa City, Souq to ADNOC and looping back. The city’s modern infrastructure was on display with key stops at NMC Royal Hospital, Horizon Private School and Gardens Plaza.

The trip to Abu Dhabi was unique to showcase where modern architecture meets busy streets combined with green spaces, providing a great option to experience the city.

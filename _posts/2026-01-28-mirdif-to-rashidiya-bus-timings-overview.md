Looking to head from Mirdif to Rashidiya? No matter if you're aiming to save time or keep costs low, we've got your options covered. From quick bus rides to seamless transfers, here's how you can get from Mirdif to Rashidiya efficiently. By the end of this guide, you'll have a clear picture of the best routes for your budget and schedule.

## 1. Direct Bus Route

This direct bus route offers a quick and affordable way to travel from Mirdif to Al Rashidiya.

- Estimated Travel Time: 10 minutes

- Cost: AED 2-3

Journey Overview:

Step 1: Catch the bus at Imam Abu Hanifa Masjid 2 in Mirdif.

Step 2: Take the 36A bus to Centerpoint Metro Bus Station in Al Rashidiya.

Stops along the way include Mirdiff, Private School 2, before reaching Centerpoint Metro Bus Station.

## 2. Bus Route with Transfer

If you’re traveling beyond Centerpoint, this route offers an easy transfer at Centerpoint Metro Station, with an additional leg to Rashidiya 4.

- Estimated Travel Time: 18-20 minutes

Journey Overview:

Step 1: Start at Imam Abu Hanifa Masjid 2 and take the 36A, F10, or F60 bus to Centerpoint Metro Bus Station.

Step 2: From Centerpoint, transfer to the F06 bus and continue to Rashidiya 4. This option works well for travelers looking to reach a specific destination in Rashidiya or nearby areas.

## Travel Tips

- Best Time to Travel: Avoid peak hours (7-9 AM and 5-7 PM) to ensure a faster, smoother trip.

- Nol Card: Make sure your Nol card is topped up before boarding the bus. You can easily refill at metro stations or Nol card outlets.
- Bus Frequency: Buses run every 15 minutes, so you won't have to wait long, even during peak hours.
- Check Bus Timings: Always check bus timings using the RTA Dubai app or website to get real-time updates on departures. Or you can check here for the Bus 36A Timings, Bus F10 Timings, Bus F60 Timings or Bus F06 Timings.

Whether you're heading to Al Rashidiya for work or leisure, the bus system offers a reliable and affordable way to get there. Opt for the direct bus if you're in a rush or need to get to the metro quickly. If flexibility is your priority, the transfer option allows you to explore more of Rashidiya. Either way, you're in for a hassle-free ride!

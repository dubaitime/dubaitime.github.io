## Bus 407 Station Overview

### Ghurair Al Ojaan Mosque

The Ghurair Al Ojaan Mosque bus station is an important transit point located near the prominent Ghurair Al Ojaan Mosque in Dubai. This station serves the surrounding residential and commercial areas, providing convenient access for visitors and locals attending the mosque or traveling through the neighborhood.

The station is equipped with basic amenities, including seating and information displays, ensuring a comfortable waiting experience. Its proximity to the mosque makes it a key stop for those visiting the area for religious or community activities.

### Al Raha Shopping Centre

The Al Raha Shopping Centre bus station is strategically situated near the Al Raha Shopping Centre, a popular retail and leisure destination. This station facilitates easy access to the shopping center and its surrounding commercial zone, catering to both shoppers and local residents.

Its location provides a valuable connection to the shopping and entertainment options available at Al Raha, making it a vital part of the local transit infrastructure.

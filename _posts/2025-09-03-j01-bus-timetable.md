Traveling on the J01 bus is both affordable and convenient. Passengers can use the Nol card, a contactless smart card that is valid for all forms of public transport in Dubai, including buses, metros, and trams.

The fare for a one-way trip on the J01 route depends on the distance traveled, but it typically falls within the range of AED 3 to AED 7. This makes it a cost-effective option for daily commuting as well as occasional travel.## Journey Experience

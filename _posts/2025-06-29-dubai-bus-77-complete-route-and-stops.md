These buses run pretty often throughout the day, so you're not left hanging around forever. It's a real godsend for getting around one of Dubai's busiest areas without losing your mind in traffic.

The fare for Bus 77 is economical and adheres to Dubai’s public transportation pricing standards. A standard single-zone ticket costs AED 3. The pricing structure is designed to be budget-friendly, catering to various travel needs.

For real-time updates on bus timings and service status, the RTA Dubai app provides live tracking and schedule information, ensuring you stay informed about your journey. ## Bus 77 Station Overview

### Baniyas Square Metro

The Baniyas Square Metro station is a major transit hub located in the heart of Dubai’s bustling downtown area. As a stop for Bus 77, it provides seamless connectivity with the Dubai Metro system, making it an essential junction for commuters traveling to and from various parts of the city.

The station features modern amenities, including ticket counters and information displays, which help facilitate smooth transfers and efficient travel. Its central location offers easy access to surrounding business and shopping districts, enhancing convenience for both daily commuters and visitors.

Al Rigga Metro Station A2 Bus Stop Intersection

The Al Rigga Metro Station A2 Bus Stop is a key transit point where Bus Routes C09 and 77 intersect. This stop provides essential connections within the Al Rigga area, facilitating seamless travel between different parts of the city. Its strategic location near the Al Rigga Metro Station enhances accessibility for commuters, offering convenient access to both local and extended destinations.

### Al Garhoud, Cambridge International School

The Al Garhoud, Cambridge International School bus stop serves as a crucial point for Bus 77, particularly for students, parents, and staff associated with the nearby educational institution. This station is strategically positioned to provide easy access to the Cambridge International School and surrounding residential areas.

It is well-equipped to handle the high volume of passengers during peak school hours, offering reliable and frequent service to facilitate smooth commutes. The stop’s location within a vibrant community ensures that passengers have convenient access to local amenities and services.

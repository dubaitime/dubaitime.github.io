As we know, The Dubai and Sharjah transport route is one of the busiest routes in the UAE. One of the most frequently used means of transportation for this route is the bus. And there is E303 bus route that connect Dubai and Sharjah city.

This route, managed by the Roads and Transport Authority (RTA) of Dubai and the Sharjah Roads and Transport Authority (SRTA), offers an efficient and affordable way for commuters to travel. Operating from [Union Square Metro Station in Dubai](/union-to-ras-al-khaimah-bus-timings/) to A [l Jubail Bus Station in Sharjah](/sharjah-to-abu-dhabi-bus-timings/), the E303 offers frequent service with modern, comfortable buses equipped with air conditioning and priority seating. ## E303 Bus Timing

For those of you who want to take a ride E303 Bus, you must know the timing or schedule of the bus. From Union Square Metro Station, the first bus departs at 6:30 am and the last bust depart at 12.15 am. During peak hours, the bus runs every 15 minutes. while during off-peak hours, the frequency is every 20 minutes​.

The E303 bus fare is reasonably priced, making it an affordable option for daily commuters. A one-way ticket costs AED 12.

The buses on the E303 route are modern and well-equipped, ensuring a comfortable journey for passengers. Key features include:- Air Conditioning: Essential for comfort in the UAE’s hot climate.

- Priority Seating: Reserved seats for women, children, and elderly passengers.
- Comfortable Seating: Plush seats designed for a relaxing ride.

## Journey Experience

The E303 bus journey from Union Square to Al Jubail Bus Station is a long but captivating ride, offering a mix of Dubai’s urban vibrancy and Sharjah’s cultural charm. The route then passes through Al Mamzar, known for its beautiful beaches and Al Mamzar Beach Park, a popular spot for families.

The journey ends at Al Jubail Bus Station, a major terminal connecting travelers across the UAE. Though the trip is lengthy, it provides a diverse look at both cities, making it an enriching experience for those exploring the area.## Conclusion

The E303 bus route is an essential transportation link between Dubai and Sharjah. With convenient stops at key locations in both emirates, the E303 ensures accessibility and ease of travel.

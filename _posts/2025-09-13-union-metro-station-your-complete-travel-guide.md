## Bus Route Details

Union Metro Station is a crucial hub connecting Dubai to several major destinations through its reliable bus services. The station is primarily served by routes E700, E400, and E303, offering frequent trips throughout the day. These routes provide seamless connectivity to key locations:

- E700: Links Dubai to Fujairah, with a stop at Union Square Bus Station.

- E400: Offers service from Dubai to Ajman, passing through Al Jubail Bus Station.
- E303: Connects Dubai to Sharjah, making stops at important points along the way.

These routes operate early in the morning and continue until late in the evening, ensuring flexibility for both early and late travelers.

## Bus 20A Station Overview

### Al Nahda 1, Terminus

Al Nahda 1, Terminus is a key bus station situated in the Al Nahda 1 area, serving as a central transit point for residents and commuters.

It offers amenities such as comfortable seating, ticketing services, and real-time schedule updates, ensuring a smooth travel experience. Its strategic location in a busy commercial and residential area provides easy access to local shops, services, and nearby neighborhoods. By facilitating seamless connections between various routes, the terminus plays a crucial role in efficient transportation within and beyond the Al Nahda area, making it a vital hub for commuters across Dubai.

### Airport Terminal 2, Departure Bus Stop Intersection

Situated conveniently at the departure area of Dubai International Airport, this station is designed for seamless connectivity, providing quick and direct access to the terminal.

With modern amenities like digital screens displaying real-time flight and bus schedule information, it ensures a hassle-free travel experience for passengers. Whether heading to or from the airport, this well-maintained stop offers efficient transit options across the city.

### Al Warsan 3, RTA Staff Accommodation, 2

This station provides essential amenities such as sheltered waiting areas and up-to-date schedule information, ensuring a comfortable and efficient commuting experience for RTA employees.

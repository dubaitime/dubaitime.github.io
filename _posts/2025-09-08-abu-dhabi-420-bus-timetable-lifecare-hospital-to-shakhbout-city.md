## Accessibility and Services

The Bus 420 is designed to be inclusive, ensuring that all passengers can travel comfortably. Here are some key accessibility features:

- Ramps for wheelchair access: Passengers with mobility impairments can easily board and disembark.

- Designated seating for those with mobility issues: Reserved seating ensures comfort for passengers with special needs.
- Audio-visual aids: The bus is equipped with announcements and visual displays to assist passengers with hearing or vision impairments.

## Travel Tips for Bus 420

- Plan Ahead: Review the bus timings to avoid delays, especially during peak hours.

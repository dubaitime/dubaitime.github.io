Ticket Pricing:

- Standard Fare: AED 2.00, with an additional AED 0.05 per kilometer.

- Payment Methods: Passengers can use the Hafilat Smart Card for easy fare payment, which can be topped up at various points throughout Abu Dhabi. For more details on fares and top-up locations, visit the official website of the Abu Dhabi Department of Transport.

5. Green Mart Market  A convenient stop for shoppers.

11. Al Khawarizmi International College  An important educational institution.

12. Al Bahya Park  A green escape for recreation and relaxation.

13. Al Naham Street  A central residential and commercial area.

## Accessibility Features

The buses feature ramps for easy boarding, dedicated seating for passengers with special needs, and audio-visual aids to assist those with hearing or vision impairments. These features make the bus route accessible and convenient for everyone.

## Connectivity and Transfer Points

Reg Bus 201 connects seamlessly with other bus routes at Al Shahama Bus Station and Abu Dhabi Bus Station, providing easy transfer options for those looking to explore more of the city. Whether you’re heading to a mall, mosque, or residential area, Bus 201 ensures smooth transitions between key parts of Abu Dhabi.

As the route weaves through Abu Dhabi, passengers get to experience a mix of serene neighborhoods and bustling commercial districts.

## Travel Tips

To avoid crowded buses, try traveling during off-peak hours. It’s also a good idea to check the Abu Dhabi Department of Transport app for real-time updates on bus schedules and any potential delays. Be sure to keep your Hafilat Smart Card topped up to ensure you’re ready to board, and always stay mindful of your belongings during the journey.

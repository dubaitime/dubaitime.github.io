## Journey Experience

Bus R1 from Al Wathbah (Razeen Workers City 3) to MBZ City Bus Station was a nice ride  a good way to get around Abu Dhabi. The trip went through residential and industrial areas and the vast desert lands as well as the city’s fast growth. Located conveniently close to nearby areas, the MBZ City Bus Station is a good place to see a quieter side of Abu Dhabi, away from the bustling city centre.

Passengers on the bus could watch the vast open spaces and developing infrastructure that will soon shape the city as it made its way through the less commercial areas. The route is an efficient and comfortable ride for both those traveling for work and for leisure.

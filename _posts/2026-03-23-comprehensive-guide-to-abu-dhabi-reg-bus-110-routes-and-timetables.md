Reg Bus 110 is a key player in Abu Dhabi’s public transportation network, connecting the busy Al Danah district with the industrial zone of Mussafah. Serving both residents and visitors, this route offers a reliable and affordable travel option, running from Sultan Bin Zayed Street/Khalifa Bin Zayed Street to Street 16/National Petroleum Corporation.

As Abu Dhabi continues to grow, Reg Bus 110 remains essential, linking commercial, residential, and industrial areas, providing easy access to workplaces, shopping centers, and other important destinations.

## Route Overview

Reg Bus 110 starts its journey at Al Danah, Sultan Bin Zayed Street/Khalifa Bin Zayed Street, and ends at Mussafah, Street 16/National Petroleum Corporation.
1. Capital Park  A popular spot for relaxation and outdoor activities.

2. Gold Center  A renowned commercial hub for jewelry and retail shopping.

3. Mariam Bint Said Mosque  A peaceful mosque situated along the route.

4. Abu Dhabi Bus Station  A major hub connecting passengers to various bus routes.

5. Mohamed Bin Khalifa Street  A central road in Abu Dhabi.

6. Al Jazeera Sports Club  A venue for sports and recreational activities.

7. Al Maqta Bus Interchange  A key transfer point for connecting bus routes.

8. Street 15  A mixed-use area with both commercial and residential buildings.

9. Civil Defence  Near Abu Dhabi’s emergency services.

10. Street 11  A notable residential area.

## Accessibility Features

Reg Bus 110 is designed with accessibility in mind, featuring ramps, designated seating for differently-abled passengers, and audio-visual aids for those with hearing or vision impairments. This ensures a comfortable and inclusive experience for all passengers, allowing everyone to travel with ease and dignity across the city.

## Connectivity and Transfer Points

Reg Bus 110 integrates seamlessly with other bus routes, particularly at Al Maqta Bus Interchange and Abu Dhabi Bus Station, making transfers easy and helping travelers reach destinations across Abu Dhabi and beyond. This network will also integrate with future metro services, further enhancing connectivity.

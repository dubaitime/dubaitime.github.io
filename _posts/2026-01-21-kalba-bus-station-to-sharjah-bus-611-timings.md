Ticket Pricing: Traveling on Bus 611 is straightforward with the Sayer Card. The fare for a one-way trip from Al Jubail Bus Terminal in Sharjah to Kalba is 21 AED, with slightly reduced fares for earlier stops. Passengers can easily pay using cash or the recommended Sayer Card, which offers a convenient and potentially discounted cashless payment method.

## Route Overview

Bus 611 provides an extensive route, connecting several significant towns and locations between Kalba and Sharjah. Below is a detailed list of the key stops along the way:
1. Kalba, Al Sidra Park  The starting point for many travelers, located near local parks and residential areas.

2. Kalba, Mosque Thabet Bin Aqzam  A religious site serving the local community.

3. Kalba Medical Centre  A primary healthcare facility catering to the medical needs of Kalba residents.

4. Thabit bin Qais Mosque  A historical mosque situated along the route.

5. Bait Sheikh Saeed Bin Hamad Al Qasimi  A cultural and historical landmark.

6. Kalba Corniche 2  A scenic area along the coast, offering beautiful views of the sea.

7. Kalba Corniche 1  Another popular stop along Kalba's coastal area.

8. Fujairah, Choithrams Supermarket  A well-known shopping destination in Fujairah, ideal for stocking up on essentials.

9. Masafi, Police Station  A key stop in Masafi, near public service facilities.

10. Dhaid, Post Office  A central location in Dhaid, with postal and administrative services.

11. Sharjah Airport Terminal  A major hub for travelers flying in and out of Sharjah.

12. Al Jubail Bus Terminal (Sharjah)  The final stop in Sharjah, providing easy connections to other parts of the emirate.

## Accessibility and Services

Bus 611 is designed with various features to accommodate all passengers:

- Wheelchair Accessibility: The bus is equipped with ramps and spaces to accommodate passengers with disabilities.

- Priority Seating: There are reserved seats for elderly passengers and those with special needs.
- Real-Time Updates: Passengers can stay informed with audio and visual announcements for upcoming stops and real-time information.

These features ensure that Bus 611 remains a comfortable and accessible transportation option for everyone.

## Travel Tips for Bus 611

- Plan Ahead: Check the bus timings in advance to ensure you catch your bus on time, especially during peak hours.

- Sharjah Transport Card Ready: Ensure your card has sufficient balance for a smooth and hassle-free ride.
- Travel Light: For a more comfortable experience, avoid carrying excessive baggage.
- Stay Informed: Use the Sharjah Transport app for real-time updates on bus arrivals and departures.

As everybody knows, Dubai transportation is really pampering the passengers. We can go anywhere or anyplace in Dubai by taking the bus. If you're navigating Dubai's bustling cityscape, the RTA X25 bus route is an essential line to familiarize yourself with.

Running from the bustling Al Karama Bus Station to the innovative hub of Dubai Silicon Oasis, X25 bus route covers a variety of key stops and landmarks, making it an essential part of the city's public transportation network.

## X25 Bus Route

The route of X25 bus is one of the most important route buses in Dubai, it connects two important places, Al Karama Bus Station and Dubai Outsourcing Zone.

As everybody knows, Al Karama bus station is very strategic places, this area is known for its bustling markets, diverse food scene, and close proximity to several key metro stations.

And here are several stops of X25 Bus route

- Karama 1 and 2:
- Schools Health Center
- BurJuman Metro Bus Stop B and C
- Oud Metha Metro Bus Stop
- Silicon Oasis Headquarter:
- Axis 1 Residence
- Dubai Academic City
- Dubai Outsourcing Zone

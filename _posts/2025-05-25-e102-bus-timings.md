## Fare and Planning Notes

E102 is commonly used as a budget-friendly intercity bus, but fares and boarding rules can change. Check the current fare in the RTA planner, station information boards, or ticket counters before travel.

## When E102 Is a Good Option

- Daily commuting between Dubai and Mussafah
- Airport-area transfers without driving
- Budget-conscious travel between the two emirates
- Trips that need a direct intercity bus rather than a Metro-plus-taxi combination

## Travel Tips

- Intercity buses can fill quickly around commuter peaks, so arrive early for the departure you want.
- Keep enough balance or the correct payment method ready before boarding.
- If you are heading beyond Mussafah, plan your onward local connection in advance.

## Summary

The E102 remains one of the most useful Dubai-Abu Dhabi bus links for riders who need a direct intercity option. Save the timetable, verify the latest departure with RTA, and plan your onward connection before you set off.

Ticket Pricing: The ticketing system for Reg Bus 161 is straightforward and budget-friendly, making it an affordable option for commuters:

- Cash Ticket: AED 2.00, with an additional AED 0.05 per kilometer.

- Payment Methods: Passengers can use the Hafilat Smart Card, which can be topped up online or at various kiosks around the city. This card ensures a quick and cashless way to pay for your journey, offering added convenience to frequent travelers.

## Accessibility Features

Reg Bus 161 is designed to be accessible to all passengers, ensuring that everyone can travel comfortably. The buses are equipped with ramps, designated seating areas for differently-abled passengers, and audio-visual aids to assist those with hearing or vision impairments. This inclusive approach makes sure that all passengers, regardless of their mobility needs, can travel safely and comfortably.

## Travel Tips
1. Plan ahead: Check the bus schedule in advance to avoid waiting times.

2. Use the transport app: The Abu Dhabi Department of Transport app provides real-time updates on schedules and any potential delays.

3. Travel during off-peak hours: Avoid the morning and evening rush if possible for a more relaxed trip.

4. Carry your Hafilat Card: It’s a quicker and more convenient way to pay.

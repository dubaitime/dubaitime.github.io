## Bus Ticket Price

The fare for the Abu Dhabi M3 bus is the same as for other standard bus routes in the city. As of February 2024, the standard fare is AED 2, plus an additional AED 0.05 for every kilometer traveled. To ensure accurate fare deduction and access to bus services, you must use a Hafilat Smart Card.

## Fare

The fare for a journey from Al Rolla Terminal to Rashidiya Metro Station is 15 AED. If you're traveling from Rashidiya to Sharjah, the fare is 10 AED. The journey is around 30 km that take around 1 hour trip.

## Journey Experience

The 113 bus from Sharjah to Rashidiya offers a long yet enjoyable ride that showcases the UAE's rich culture and urban development. Departing from Sharjah, passengers can admire the bustling Al Qasba canal, known for its art and entertainment options, as well as historic sites like the Sharjah Museum of Islamic Civilization, reflecting the city's dedication to preserving its heritage.

As the bus crosses into Dubai, travelers catch glimpses of vibrant neighborhoods like Al Nahda and Al Mamzar, which feature a mix of local markets and modern shopping centers. The journey concludes in Rashidiya, near Dubai International Airport, making it a key area for travelers and expatriates. While the ride may be lengthy, the diverse landscapes and attractions along the way make it a fascinating trip for anyone exploring the UAE.

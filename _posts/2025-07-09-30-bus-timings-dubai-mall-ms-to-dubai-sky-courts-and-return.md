Ticket Pricing: The fare for Route 30 follows Dubai's standard RTA pricing. Passengers need to have a minimum balance of AED 7.50 on their Nol card before boarding to ensure a smooth travel experience and avoid fines. Nol cards can be easily topped up at various locations throughout the city.

## Route Overview

Bus 30 begins its journey at Dubai Mall Metro Station and ends at Dubai Sky Courts, making several essential stops along the way. These include residential neighborhoods, educational institutions, and commercial hubs, providing easy access to passengers at various points along the route. Below are some of the key stops:
1. Dubai Mall Metro Landside  The starting point, offering easy connections to Dubai Metro and other public transport services.

2. ENBD HQ  A stop near the headquarters of Emirates NBD.

3. Liwan Entrance, Liwan Mazaya 18, 20, 24  These stops serve the Liwan residential communities, providing access to various apartment complexes.

4. Liwan Mazaya 11, Geepas, R20, 6, 4, 10A  Additional stops in the Liwan area for various residential buildings.

5.

6. GEMS First Point School, The Aquila School, Al Sondos Orchid, Al Sondos Sage  Convenient stops for students, staff, and visitors to these educational institutions.

7. Dubai Sky Courts  The final destination, located near major residential complexes.

8. International Center For Biosaline Agriculture & EPPCO Muntazah Petrol Station  Stops serving the agricultural research center and a key fuel station.

9. Avenue Mall & ENBD HQ  Returning towards central Dubai, these stops provide access to shopping and commercial areas.

The route then loops back to Dubai Mall Metro Landside, completing the journey. This comprehensive route offers convenient transportation for both residents and visitors across various districts.

## Accessibility Features

The buses on this route are equipped with low floors for easier boarding, designated seating areas for people with disabilities, and audiovisual aids for those with hearing or vision impairments. This ensures an inclusive and comfortable journey for all travelers.

## Connectivity and Transfer Points

Starting at Dubai Mall Metro Landside, passengers can easily transfer to the Dubai Metro, as well as other bus routes. This provides seamless travel to various parts of the city. At its final stop, Dubai Sky Courts, passengers can connect to local services or private transport options, ensuring smooth onward travel.

## Journey Experience

A traveler recently took Bus 30 from Dubai Mall Metro Station to Dubai Sky Courts and back, experiencing a long yet scenic journey through Dubai's vibrant cityscape.

Along the way, the traveler noted the proximity to Global Village, a multicultural theme park, adding an interesting aspect to the trip. Upon reaching Dubai Sky Courts, a quieter residential area, the contrast between the serene surroundings and the bustling city center became apparent.

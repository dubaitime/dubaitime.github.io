## Bus 50 Station Overview

### Business Bay 2 Bus Station

The Business Bay 2 Bus Station is a key transit hub situated in the heart of Dubai's vibrant Business Bay district. This station caters to the needs of professionals and residents in the area, offering a range of amenities such as comfortable seating, ticketing services, and real-time bus schedule updates.

Its location supports efficient connectivity with nearby commercial and residential properties, as well as other transportation networks. The station's strategic placement ensures that passengers can easily access business centers, dining options, and entertainment venues within the Business Bay area.

### Al Warsan 1, Eppco 1 Bus Stop Intersection

The Al Warsan 1, Eppco 1 Bus Stop is a key junction where Routes 50, X23, 310, and E16 intersect. This bus stop serves as a vital hub for commuters traveling to and from the Al Warsan area, offering diverse route options that connect to various parts of the city. The intersection of these routes ensures convenient and efficient transit, making it an essential stop for daily travelers and residents.

### International City Bus Station

The International City Bus Station serves the expansive International City development, a major residential and commercial zone in Dubai.

Its position within the International City area facilitates easy access to nearby shops, services, and residential communities. The station plays an important role in connecting passengers to various destinations within the development and to other parts of the city.

## Bus Ticket Price

The fare for Bus 50 is affordable with AED 3-5, reflecting Dubai's public transport pricing structure, which offers economical travel options for both residents and visitors.

## Bus Booking

While online booking options for individual bus tickets may vary, passengers can access real-time schedules and plan their journeys using the Roads and Transport Authority (RTA) official app or website. This provides convenience and ensures travelers are informed about route changes or updates.

International City is also home to Dragon Mart, the largest Chinese trading hub outside China, making it a destination for unique shopping experiences.

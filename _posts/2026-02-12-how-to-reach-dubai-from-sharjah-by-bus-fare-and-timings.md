With bustling routes and strategic stops, making this journey is simple and accessible. Here’s a comprehensive guide on how to travel from Sharjah to Dubai by bus, covering routes, timings, fares, and travel tips to ensure a smooth ride.

## 1. Line 107 Bus - Best Option

- Route: Sahara Center (Sharjah) to Global Village 1 (Dubai)

- Estimated Travel Time: 1 hour 35 minutes
- Fare: AED 1720

Journey Overview:

Step 1: Head to the nearest bus stop at Sahara Center, which serves as a key pick-up point for the 107 bus. The Sahara Center is easily accessible for residents and visitors in the Sharjah area.

Step 2: The bus operates every three hours, so be sure to check the schedule in advance. The journey takes approximately 1 hour and 35 minutes, with stops along the way that include Al Nahda 1 and Terminus 2.

- Key Stops: Al Nahda 1, Terminus 2

Step 3: The bus drops you off at Global Village 1, a major attraction in Dubai where you can begin exploring the cultural and entertainment offerings.

## 2. Line 103 and E303 Buses - Alternative Option

For travelers starting from the Union Metro Station area, the Line 103 bus combined with the E303 bus provides an alternate route. This option covers more stops and may be preferable for those seeking flexibility or needing to visit specific locations.

- Route: Union Metro Station (Deira, Dubai) to Al Jubail Bus Station (Sharjah)

- Estimated Travel Time: 1 hour 51 minutes
- Fare: AED 1320

Journey Overview:

Step 1: Union Metro Station is a transportation hub with easy connections to bus services. Head to Union Square Bus Station, a short walk away, to catch the 103 bus.

Step 2: The 103 bus runs every hour from Union Square Bus Station and makes several convenient stops, including Rebat Street and Al Karama.

- Key Stops: Union Square, Rebat Street, Al Karama

Step 3: At Union Square Bus Station, transfer to the E303 bus, which heads towards Al Jubail Bus Station in Sharjah.

- Key Stops: Salah Al Din Metro, Reef Mall, Abu Hail, Sharjah Mamzar, Al Wahda Center

Step 4: The E303 bus will drop you at Al Jubail Bus Station, a central point in Sharjah that provides access to various parts of the city.

## Travel Tips

- Check the Schedule: Bus routes operate on fixed schedules, so planning ahead can save time and minimize waiting periods. Use the RTA website or app for real-time updates on bus timings.

- Avoid Peak Hours: Buses can be crowded during rush hours (typically 5 PM to 7 PM). Traveling during off-peak times will provide a more comfortable experience.
- Stay Close to Landmarks: If you have time, explore nearby attractions like the Gold Souk and Spice Souk in Deira before heading to your next destination. Likewise, enjoy some shopping at Sahara Center before catching your bus to Dubai.
- Global Village Tips: Be aware of the seasonal opening hours of Global Village to make the most of your visit, as they can vary throughout the year.

Some other interesting trips you can explore are from Dubai to Al Ain and the trip from Dubai to Ajman.

Traveling from Sharjah to Dubai by bus is a cost-effective and practical option, with routes tailored for both convenience and flexibility. The Line 107 bus offers a direct and simple journey, while the 103 and E303 buses provide a broader range of stops. By planning ahead and following these guidelines, you’ll have a seamless and enjoyable travel experience between these vibrant cities. Enjoy your adventure and explore everything that Dubai and Sharjah have to offer!

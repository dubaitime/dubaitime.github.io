## Accessibility and Services

Bus 401 is designed to accommodate passengers with special needs, ensuring a comfortable journey for everyone. The buses feature:

- Ramps for wheelchair access.

- Designated seating for passengers with mobility challenges.
- Audio-visual aids for those with hearing or vision impairments.

This inclusive design makes Bus 401 a great option for all commuters.

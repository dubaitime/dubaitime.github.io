## Bus Route Details

The Abu Hail, Clinic 1 Bus Stop is well-connected by a variety of bus routes, each serving different key destinations:

- Bus Route 23: This route connects Abu Hail with Oud Metha Bus Station and Al Nahda 1, offering frequent services every 20 minutes from 5:00 AM to 11:00 PM. It provides essential connectivity for travelers heading to and from these busy areas.

- Bus Route 64: Running between Gold Souq Bus Station and Ras Al Khor, the 64 route operates every 30 minutes from 5:15 AM to 10:45 PM. It is a reliable option for those traveling between these commercial and residential areas.
- Bus Route 13B: Serving the Gold Souq Bus Station and Al Qusais Bus Station, this route runs every 30 minutes from 5:30 AM to 10:30 PM. It facilitates movement between two major transit points in Dubai.
- Bus Route 13D: Similar to 13B, the 13D route also connects Gold Souq Bus Station with Al Qusais Bus Station, with departures every 30 minutes from 5:45 AM to 10:45 PM. This provides additional frequency for those traveling to Al Qusais.
- Bus Route C18: This route links Shaikh Rashid Colony with Oud Metha Bus Station, operating every 40 minutes from 6:00 AM to 11:00 PM.
- Bus Route X13: Connecting Lulu Village with Al Satwa Bus Station, the X13 route runs every 30 minutes from 5:45 AM to 11:15 PM.
- Bus Route E303: Offering a link between Union Bus Station and Al Jubail Bus Station, the E303 operates every 40 minutes from 6:00 AM to 10:30 PM. This route provides an important connection for longer journeys across the city.

Each of these routes contributes to the efficient operation of Dubai’s public transport network, ensuring that passengers have access to various destinations throughout the day.

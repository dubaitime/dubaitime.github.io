Opting for public transportation is not only economical but also provides an easy and accessible journey. Below is a detailed guide on the bus routes, fares, and travel tips to help make your journey smooth and enjoyable.

## Bus Route via Al Ghubaiba Bus Station (Best Overall)

- Estimated Travel Time: 2 hours 29 minutes

- Cost: AED 43-67

Journey Overview:

Step 1: Start at Al Ghubaiba Bus Station  5 minutes, Walk (450 meters) to Al Ghubaiba Metro Station 1, Al Karama.

Step 2: Take the Green Line Metro from Al Ghubaiba Metro Station 1, Al Karama  4 minutes, AED 35, Train (MGrn, every 10 minutes), and disembark at BurJuman Metro Station 1, Al Karama.

Step 3: Transfer at BurJuman Metro Station 1  14 minutes, AED 47, Red Line Metro (MRed, every 10 minutes), and exit at Airport Terminal 3 Metro Station, Dubai Festival City.

## Travel Tips

- Best Time to Travel: To avoid peak crowd times, plan your trip for early morning or late evening hours.

- Sightseeing: Consider stopping by the Ras Al Khaimah Corniche, a beautiful area to walk, relax, and enjoy views of the waterfront.
- Packing Essentials: Bring a travel-friendly bag, water, and some snacks to make the journey more comfortable. If you’re planning an extended visit, don’t forget sun protection and comfortable clothing.

Some other interesting trips you can explore are from Ghubaiba to Ibn Battuta and the trip from Ghubaiba to Ajman.

Traveling from Al Ghubaiba to Ras Al Khaimah by bus provides an economical and efficient way to explore the UAE’s natural beauty. Whether you choose the direct bus route or the combined metro-bus option, the journey offers comfort and affordability. Enjoy a scenic ride through the UAE, where the adventure starts the moment you step onto the bus. Safe travels, and don’t forget to take in the stunning desert landscapes along the way!

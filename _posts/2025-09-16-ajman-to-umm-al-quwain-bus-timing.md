## Bus Route

While there is no direct bus route from Ajman to Umm Al Quwain, travelers can embark on a seamless journey by taking a two-step route via Ras Al Khaimah (Al Hamra Bus Station).

The first leg of the journey begins at the main bus station in Ajman, where passengers board a bus bound for Ras Al Khaimah.**Read Also**: [Dubai to Fujairah Bus Timing](https://uaetiming. com/dubai-to-fujairah-bus-timing/) Upon reaching Al Hamra Bus Station, passengers transfer to another bus that continues the journey to Umm Al Quwain.

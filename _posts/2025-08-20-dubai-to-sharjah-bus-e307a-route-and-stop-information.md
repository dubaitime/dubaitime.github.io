## Bus Ticket Price

The ticket price for the E307A bus route is affordable, making it an attractive option for budget-conscious travelers. The fare typically ranges from AED 10 to AED 15, depending on the distance traveled. Passengers can pay for their journey using a Nol card, which can be recharged at any metro station, bus station, or online through the RTA website or app.

## Bus Booking

While there isn't a specific online booking system for individual seats on the E307A bus, passengers can easily plan their journey using the RTA Dubai app. This app provides comprehensive details on bus routes, schedules, and real-time tracking, allowing for a smooth and well-planned trip.

Additionally, Nol cards, which are used to pay for bus rides, can be recharged online via the RTA website or mobile app. For tourists or occasional travelers, day passes or multiple-day passes are also available, offering unlimited travel within the selected period.

## Bus E307A Station Overview

### Abu Hail Bus Station · 6

Abu Hail Bus Station is a busy transit hub located in a vibrant area of Dubai, serving as a key stop for Bus E307A. The station is well-equipped with modern amenities, including shaded waiting areas and digital displays showing real-time bus information.

### Al Wahda Rd, Subway 1-2 Sharjah Bus Stop Intersection

The Al Wahda Rd, Subway 1-2 Sharjah Bus Stop is a key transit hub where Bus Routes E306, E307A, E307, E303, and E304 intersect. This central location provides seamless connections across Sharjah and beyond, making it easier for passengers to access various destinations. Its strategic position ensures convenient travel options for daily commuters and travelers in the region.

### Sharjah — Al Jubail Bus Station · 2

Sharjah, Al Jubail Bus Station 2 is one of Sharjah's main transport hubs, serving as a vital connection point between Sharjah, Dubai, and other regions. This station is where Bus Routes E303, E307, E303A, and E307A intersect, providing passengers with multiple commuting options.

Its central location near key landmarks in Sharjah makes it a convenient choice for both regular commuters and travelers, offering seamless access to destinations across the UAE.

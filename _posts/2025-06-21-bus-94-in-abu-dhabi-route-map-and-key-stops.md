## Bus Ticket Price

While the exact fare depends on distance traveled, expect the Abu Dhabi route 94 bus to cost around AED 4 which is typical for standard routes. For the most accurate price, use a Hafilat Smart Card (info on the Department of Transport website) or ask the bus driver upon boarding.

## Bus Booking

While online booking options specific to Bus 94 may not be available, passengers can easily access information on schedules and stops through various Abu Dhabi public transport apps and websites. Physical ticket purchases are available at bus stations and select outlets.

## Journey Experience

Taking bus route 94 from Wahat Al Karamah to Mamsha Al Saadiyat offers a scenic journey through Abu Dhabi’s highlights. Starting at Wahat Al Karamah, a memorial honoring the UAE’s martyrs, the trip reflects national pride.

Finally, arriving at Mamsha Al Saadiyat, you can enjoy the beautiful beachfront promenade that connects cultural landmarks like the Louvre Abu Dhabi, making it perfect for a leisurely stroll along the Arabian Gulf while soaking in the artistic ambiance of the area.

## Traveling with Careem

With the Careem app, travelers can conveniently reach Mamsha Al Saadiyat, the terminus of the 94 bus route starting from Wahat Al Karamah, at a fare ranging from AED 61 to 74. This option provides a flexible and comfortable way to explore the vibrant cultural and recreational offerings of Abu Dhabi's renowned beachfront destination.

Bus 94 stands out as a dependable and cost-effective option for navigating Abu Dhabi's diverse landscape, ensuring seamless travel experiences for both residents and visitors alike.

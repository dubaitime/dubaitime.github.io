Whether you’re traveling for a short holiday or a business trip, the visa-on-arrival process is designed to be straightforward and hassle-free.

## Visa-on-Arrival Process: Step-by-Step Guide

For eligible travelers, obtaining a visa on arrival is a simple process. Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you navigate through it easily:
1. Arrive at Zayed International Airport, Abu Dhabi. After landing, follow the signs for immigration and visa processing.

2. Approach the Visa Desk: There is no need to apply in advance if you are eligible for a visa on arrival. You will proceed directly to the visa desk upon arrival.

3. Submit Your Passport: Hand over your passport to the immigration officer. No additional documentation is required for most travelers. In some cases, you may need to show proof of onward travel or accommodation, so it's best to have these documents handy.

4. Get Your Visa Stamp: The immigration officer will stamp your passport with a free entry visa. The visa duration is typically 30 days, though this may vary depending on your nationality.

5. Enter Abu Dhabi: Once your visa is stamped, you’re free to explore Abu Dhabi! Enjoy your stay.

Pro Tip: Make sure your passport is valid for at least six months from your arrival date in the UAE.

## What If You’re Not Eligible for a Visa on Arrival?

If your country is not eligible for a visa on arrival, you will need to apply for a tourist or visit visa in advance. This can be done in several ways:

- Through a Travel Agency: If you’ve booked a package, your travel agent can assist in applying for the visa.
- At a UAE Embassy: Visit the nearest UAE embassy to apply for a visa. This is required for tourists, transit passengers, or long-term visitors.

## Special Visa Options: Golden Visa

For individuals looking to stay long-term or invest in the UAE, the Golden Visa is an attractive option. The application process involves:

- Applying for a nomination through the UAE government portal.

Once nominated, applicants can complete the process with the Federal Authority for Identity and Citizenship.

Getting a visa on arrival in Abu Dhabi is a smooth and efficient process for eligible travelers. By ensuring you have the necessary documents and understanding the process, you can look forward to a stress-free arrival. If you’re unsure about your visa status, it’s always best to check with the UAE government’s official website or contact your airline for assistance. Safe travels and enjoy your time in Abu Dhabi!

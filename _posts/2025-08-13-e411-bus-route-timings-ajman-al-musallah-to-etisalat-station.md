### Ticket Pricing

The fare for Bus E411 from Dubai to Ajman is AED 12 when using a Nol card, making it an affordable option for commuters. Departing from Etisalat Bus Station in Dubai and arriving at Al Musallah Bus Station in Ajman, passengers can enjoy a seamless, cashless travel experience with the convenience of the Nol card system.

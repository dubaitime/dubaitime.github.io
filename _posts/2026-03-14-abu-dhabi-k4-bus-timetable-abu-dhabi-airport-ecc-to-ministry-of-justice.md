With continuing efficient and accessible public transportation, Abu Dhabi’s public transport continues to lead the way with reliable travel options within the city. Bus K4 is an important route which connects Abu Dhabi Airport ECC (Exhibition Centre) to Ministry of Justice via Al Zeina, Al Raha Gardens and Khalifa city. In this guide, we provide step by step guide on Bus K4’s route, timings, major stops and travel tips to help you plan your journey.

Ticket Pricing: Payments are made with the Hafilat Card while standard fares are AED 2 per journey within a single zone, more for longer distances. For your convenience, cards can be recharged at kiosks or online.

## Route Overview

Bus K4 is a nice travel option which has stops in many of the important areas of the city. Below is a list of the key stops on the K4 route:

1. Abu Dhabi Airport ECC: The starting point, offering access to the exhibition center and the airport.

2. Abu Dhabi Airport Terminal A Arrival: A stop for those arriving at Terminal A.

3. Al Zeina: A residential and commercial area.

4. Al Raha Boulevard: A bustling area with a mix of shops and residences.

5. Al Bandar Street: Connecting the nearby Al Raha developments.

6. Al Raha Gardens Muzeyra Gate 19: A popular residential area.

7. Al Raha Gardens Al Maryah Gate 20: Another access point for Al Raha Gardens.

8. Al Raha Gardens Samra Gate 21: One of the main gates to Al Raha Gardens.

9. Raha International School: A stop near a prominent international school.

10. Al Rashid Street: Connecting various parts of the city.

## Nearby Attractions and Landmarks

1. Al Raha Gardens: A serene residential area with parks and amenities.

2. Al Forsan Central Mall: A major shopping and entertainment destination.

3. Ministry of Justice: The central government office offering legal services.

## Journey Experience

The journey from Abu Dhabi Airport ECC to Ministry of Justice on the K4 bus, is a long and very interesting ride through the very heart of the city.

Another stop is Al Forsan Central Mall, a go to shopping and eating hub as well as the Emirates ID Head Office. The route also offers a view of the Al Raha Beach area, a top spot for a rest and dining by waterfront.

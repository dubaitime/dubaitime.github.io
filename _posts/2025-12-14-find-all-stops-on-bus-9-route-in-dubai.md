## Bus 9 Station Overview

### Ghubaiba Bus Station · 45

Ghubaiba Bus Station is a significant transit hub serving the central area of Dubai. Its strategic location and comprehensive services make it a key point for commuters navigating Dubai's public transport system.

### Business Bay Metro Seaside

Business Bay Metro Station Seaside is strategically located in the vibrant Business Bay district, a major commercial and residential hub in Dubai. This station not only provides convenient access to the Dubai Metro but also serves as a crucial intersection for Bus Routes 7, 9, 14, 91, and F20.

Offering seamless connections to various parts of the city, it caters to the needs of daily commuters, professionals, and visitors. The station is equipped with modern amenities and is situated near waterfront developments and key business hubs, making it an essential transit point for those traveling within and beyond the Business Bay area.

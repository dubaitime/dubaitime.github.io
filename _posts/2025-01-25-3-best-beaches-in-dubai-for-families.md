Dubai has some of the most family-friendly beaches in the world. Here are the top three spots that combine clean sands, safe swimming, and great facilities.

## Kite Beach

Families seeking adventure will appreciate Kite Beach because it combines clean sands with exciting water sports and a dedicated kids play area. The beach also has a well-maintained promenade with cafes and food trucks nearby.

## Jumeirah Public Beach

Jumeirah Public Beach combines serene waters with shaded spaces alongside remarkable views of the Burj Al Arab. Entry is free, and lifeguards are on duty during peak hours.

## Marina Beach

The Marina Beach in Dubai Marina delivers calm waters alongside an energetic urban setting with convenient restaurant options — making it a perfect family outing destination close to public transport.

## Why Families Love Dubai Beaches

Dubai's public beaches are well-maintained, free to enter, and equipped with facilities including changing rooms, showers, and parking. Most operate under lifeguard supervision and are patrolled by beach police for visitor safety.

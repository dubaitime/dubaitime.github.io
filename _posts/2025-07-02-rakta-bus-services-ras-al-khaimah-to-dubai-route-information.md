RAKTA Bus Services provide a reliable and convenient means of traveling from Ras Al Khaimah to Dubai. With multiple departures throughout the day, this service offers a comfortable and affordable option for both daily commuters and occasional travelers. Whether you’re heading to Dubai’s Union Bus Station or The Dubai Mall, the RAKTA buses ensure a hassle-free journey across the emirates.

## Bus Route

RAKTA buses operate daily between Ras Al Khaimah and Dubai’s Union Bus Station. Departing from Ras Al Khaimah Bus Station, the route includes key stops like Al Hamra Mall, Umm Al Quwain, Crown Palace Hotel, and Al Jubail Bus Station before reaching Union Square Bus Station. Early morning trips run directly to Dubai, while later services make additional stops in Sharjah and Ajman, offering more flexible travel options for passengers.

Key Stops:
1. Ras Al Khaimah Bus Station

2. Al Hamra Mall

3. Umm Al Quwain

4. Crown Palace Hotel

5. Al Jubail Bus Station

6. Union Square Bus Station · 9

For those traveling to The Dubai Mall, RAKTA buses operate on Fridays, Saturdays, and Sundays. This route departs from Ras Al Khaimah Bus Station and heads straight to The Dubai Mall, providing a convenient transportation option for weekend shoppers and visitors.

Key Stops:
1. Ras Al Khaimah Bus Station

2. The Dubai Mall

## The Journey and Fare

Traveling by bus from Ras Al Khaimah to Dubai is both affordable and convenient. For trips to Union Bus Station, the fare is AED 27. Passengers can enjoy a comfortable, air-conditioned ride with dedicated seating for people of determination.

The service from Ras Al Khaimah to The Dubai Mall, available on weekends, costs AED 30, making it a great option for a weekend shopping trip or a visit to the iconic Dubai landmark.

Whether you're commuting daily or planning a weekend trip, the frequent bus schedules and comfortable rides ensure a pleasant journey across the emirates.

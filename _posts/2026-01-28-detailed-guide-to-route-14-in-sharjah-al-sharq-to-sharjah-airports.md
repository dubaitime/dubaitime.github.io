Route 14 is an essential bus service in Sharjah, linking the Al Sharq area with Sharjah International Airport. This route caters to both daily commuters and travelers, providing a reliable and affordable option for those needing transportation between the city center and the airport. With frequent services and well-planned stops, Route 14 plays a crucial role in Sharjah’s public transport network, ensuring a smooth and accessible journey for all.

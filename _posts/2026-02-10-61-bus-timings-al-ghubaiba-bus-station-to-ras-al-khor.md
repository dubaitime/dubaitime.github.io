1. Al Ghubaiba Bus Station  A central transport hub in Dubai, connecting various metro and bus routes.

2. Raffa  A key stop serving both residential and commercial areas.

3. Rolla  A busy commercial area near Bur Dubai.

4. Sharaf DG Metro 1  Serving the nearby metro station for easy connections to the red metro line.

7. Centre Point Shopping Centre  A well-known retail hub.

## Journey Experience

For those with time, the trip on Bus 61 from Al Ghubaiba Bus Station to Ras Al Khor is a long but pleasant journey through the beating heart of Dubai. Iconic landmarks like the BurJuman Metro, World Trade Centre Hotel and Centre Point Shopping Centre will be passed by passengers, and they will see, live, the dynamic urban life of Dubai.

A highlight of the trip is the Ras Al Khor Wildlife Sanctuary, an ideal place for relaxing in eco friendly environment, away from the hustle and bustle of Dubai, and to gaze at a rare natural sight of migrating birds such as flamingos found here. It also has views of Ras Al Khor waterfront where the nature and the industrial settings meet. The trip is comfortable, convenient and accessible with a fascinating and scenic look at Dubai’s varied attractions.

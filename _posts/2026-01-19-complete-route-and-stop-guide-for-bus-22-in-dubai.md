## Bus 22 Station Overview

### Dubai Courts

The Dubai Courts bus station is strategically located near the Dubai Courts complex, a central hub for legal and judicial services in Dubai. This station caters to individuals visiting or working at the courts, providing essential amenities such as sheltered waiting areas, ticketing services, and real-time schedule updates.

Its proximity to the courts makes it a convenient stop for those involved in legal proceedings or administrative matters, facilitating easy access to the judicial facilities. The station’s design and services are tailored to accommodate the needs of its users, ensuring a smooth and efficient transit experience.

## Intersection at Al Nahda, Park 1 Bus Stop

The Al Nahda, Park 1 Bus Stop is a key transit point where F21 and 22 intersect. This strategic location facilitates smooth connections between these routes, providing passengers with convenient options for travel in the Al Nahda area. The bus stop's central position enhances accessibility and streamlines the commuting experience for travelers using these routes.

### Al Nahda 1, Terminus

Al Nahda 1, Terminus is a key bus station situated in the Al Nahda 1 area, serving as a central transit point for residents and commuters.

It offers amenities such as comfortable seating, ticketing services, and real-time schedule updates, ensuring a smooth travel experience. Its strategic location in a busy commercial and residential area provides easy access to local shops, services, and nearby neighborhoods. By facilitating seamless connections between various routes, the terminus plays a crucial role in efficient transportation within and beyond the Al Nahda area, making it a vital hub for commuters across Dubai.

## Tourist Destination Along the Way

Several notable destinations and attractions are along the Bus 22 route:
1. Deira City Center: A major shopping mall in Deira offering a variety of retail outlets, dining options, and entertainment facilities.

2. City Centre Metro Station: Provides access to the Red Line of the Dubai Metro, connecting to various parts of the city and key attractions.

3. Shabab Al Ahli Club: A well-known sports club in Dubai, offering facilities for various sports and events.

4. Stadium Metro Station: Located near the Dubai Sports City, which hosts numerous sporting events and entertainment activities.

5. Dubai Women's College: Part of the Higher Colleges of Technology, this educational institution is an important center for higher education in Dubai.

6. Madina Supermarket: A local supermarket offering a range of groceries and goods, reflecting the local shopping experience.

7. Dubai Municipality Nursery: Although not a tourist attraction, it is a notable local institution.

8. Burj Al Nahda: A residential and commercial area offering a glimpse into local Dubai life.

9. Zaafranah: A vibrant locality with residential and commercial spaces, providing insight into Dubai's diverse neighborhoods.

10. Burj Al Noor: An area with various shops and local attractions.

These locations offer a mix of shopping, education, sports, and local culture, making Bus 22 a route that connects both practical destinations and interesting points of interest.

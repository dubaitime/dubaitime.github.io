If you're planning to travel from Al Ghubaiba in Dubai to Ajman, using public transportation is both cost-effective and scenic. Whether you prefer the direct routes via Sharjah or a combination of metro and bus, this guide covers everything you need to know about bus timings, fares, and schedules. Let's explore the options to make your trip smooth and budget-friendly.

## 1. Direct Bus Route via Al Jubail Bus Station (Best Overall)

A convenient and affordable choice, this route combines a direct bus from Dubai to Sharjah, followed by a transfer to reach Ajman.

- Estimated Travel Time: 1 hour 10 minutes

- Total Fare: AED 1518

Journey Details:

Step 1: Begin your journey at Al Ghubaiba Bus Station and board Bus E306 heading towards Sharjah. This leg of the trip takes approximately 45 minutes, with fares between AED 6 and AED 9. The E306 operates hourly, so you can easily plan around the next available departure. You will arrive at Al Jubail Bus Station in Sharjah, a central hub for transfers.

Step 2: At Al Jubail Bus Station, transfer to Bus Sh1 for the final portion of your journey to Ajman. This bus departs every 20 minutes and takes about 15 minutes to reach Al Musallah Bus Station in Ajman, with a fare of AED 9. This route, popular for its direct connection through Sharjah, is both efficient and budget-friendly.

## 2. Metro and Bus Combo Route

For those preferring a metro experience, the second option combines Dubai's metro system with a bus transfer, providing flexibility and frequent departure times.

- Estimated Travel Time: 1 hour 11 minutes

- Total Fare: AED 2326

Journey Details:

Step 1: Begin your journey with a brief 5-minute walk from Al Ghubaiba Bus Station to Al Ghubaiba Metro Station. Once there, board the MGrn line heading towards Al Karama, which will take you to BurJuman Metro Station in just 4 minutes. The fare for this segment ranges from AED 3 to AED 5.

## 3. Bus Route (Cheapest)

If you're looking for the most economical option, this route combines a walk, metro, and bus.

- Estimated Travel Time: 1 hour 19 minutes

- Total Fare: AED 1116

Journey Details:

Step 1: Start your journey at Al Ghubaiba Bus Station and take a brief 10-minute walk to Al Ghubaiba Metro Station. Once you arrive, board the MGrn line heading towards Etisalat by e& Metro Station. This train segment will take approximately 7 minutes, making stops at key locations such as Al Ras, Gold Souq, Baniyas Square, and Union Metro Station.

Step 2: Upon reaching Union Square Metro Station, transfer to Union Square Bus Station, which is just a 10-minute walk away. Once at the bus station, catch the E400 bus towards Ajman. This bus ride will take about 46 minutes and will stop at notable landmarks like Mahlab School and the Crown Palace Hotel in Ajman, making it a practical option for travelers seeking efficient public transport.

## Travel Tips for a Seamless Journey

- Timing and Frequency: Buses and trains operate frequently, with options from early morning to late night. However, be sure to check the specific operating hours, as schedules may vary by day.

- Peak Hours: To avoid heavy crowds, consider traveling outside typical peak hours (around 8-10 AM and 5-7 PM). These times can get busy as commuters travel between emirates.
- Nearby Amenities: Both Al Ghubaiba and Al Jubail Bus Stations are located near shopping and dining options, making it convenient to grab a meal or supplies for your journey.
- Accessibility: All buses and metro services in the UAE are equipped with accessibility features, including ramps and designated seating.

Some other interesting trips you can explore are from Ghubaiba to Global Village and the trip from Ghubaiba to Umm Al Quwain.

Traveling from Al Ghubaiba to Ajman by bus and metro is a practical way to explore the UAE on a budget. Whether you take the direct route through Sharjah, opt for a combination of metro and bus, or choose the most economical option, each journey offers a unique experience. With clear routes and affordable fares, your adventure from Dubai to Ajman promises to be enjoyable and cost-effective.

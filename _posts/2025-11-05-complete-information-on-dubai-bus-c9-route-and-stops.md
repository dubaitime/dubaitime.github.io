## Bus C9 Station Overview

### Al Qiyadah Metro

Al Qiyadah Metro Station is a prominent transit point located on the Dubai Metro's Red Line, providing essential connectivity to various parts of the city. This station is well-equipped with modern amenities, including ticketing counters, escalators, and sheltered waiting areas.

Its strategic location facilitates easy transfers between the metro and bus services, making it a key hub for commuters traveling through Dubai.

Al Rigga Metro Station A2 Bus Stop Intersection

The Al Rigga Metro Station A2 Bus Stop is a key transit point where Bus Routes C09 and 77 intersect. This stop provides essential connections within the Al Rigga area, facilitating seamless travel between different parts of the city. Its strategic location near the Al Rigga Metro Station enhances accessibility for commuters, offering convenient access to both local and extended destinations.

### bu Hail Center 2 Bus Stop Intersection

The Abu Hail Center 2 Bus Stop serves as a crucial intersection for Bus Routes 64, 13B, C09, and C10. This stop connects passengers to various destinations across Dubai, making it a vital hub for commuters in the Abu Hail area. Its proximity to the Abu Hail Center ensures convenient access to shopping, dining, and other services, catering to the needs of both locals and visitors.

### Al Ras Metro Bus Stop Intersection

This strategic location provides seamless connectivity for passengers traveling across various parts of the city, making it a vital hub for efficient travel and convenient transfers between different bus services.

### Satwa Bus Station · 13

Satwa Bus Station is a major bus terminal located in the vibrant Satwa neighborhood, serving as a central hub for numerous bus routes.

Its location in a busy commercial district ensures high passenger traffic, providing convenient access to local shops, markets, and other amenities. The station's role in connecting various bus routes and its proximity to key local attractions make it an important part of Dubai’s public transportation network.

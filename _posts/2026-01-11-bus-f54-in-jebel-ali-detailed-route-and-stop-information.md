## Bus F54 Station Overview

### Danube Metro Seaside

The Danube Metro Seaside station is a key stop for Bus F54, strategically located near the Danube Metro Station, which provides access to the adjacent seaside area. This station is well-equipped with essential amenities such as covered waiting areas and real-time schedule updates.

Its proximity to the metro station enhances connectivity and offers a convenient travel option for both daily commuters and visitors heading towards the seaside attractions. The station's location supports efficient transfers between bus and metro services, making it a vital hub in the local transportation network.

### Mideast Metals

he Mideast Metals station is another important stop for Bus F54, located close to the Mideast Metals industrial area. The station's placement near an industrial zone ensures accessibility for those traveling to and from business and commercial sites in the area. Its facilities and location support a smooth transit experience, contributing to the overall efficiency of the local bus network.

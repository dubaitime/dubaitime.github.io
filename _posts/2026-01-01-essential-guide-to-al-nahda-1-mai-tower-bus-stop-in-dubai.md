/wp: paragraph


wp: list- F22 Route: This route connects Stadium MS with several stops along Al Nahda. Buses start operating from 7:15 PM and run at regular intervals until 8:35 PM. The service provides convenient connectivity for evening commuters.

- F23 Route: Running from Stadium MS, this route begins service at 7:21 PM, with buses arriving every 15 minutes until 8:39 PM. It offers essential links for evening travel.
- F24 Route: This route operates between Stadium MS and Al Nahda, with buses available from 7:27 PM to 8:40 PM. It ensures frequent service during peak evening hours.
- C14 Route: Serving Al Safa and Al Nahda 1, Sama Residence, this route starts at 7:23 PM and runs until 8:40 PM. It provides reliable connections to various key locations in the area.

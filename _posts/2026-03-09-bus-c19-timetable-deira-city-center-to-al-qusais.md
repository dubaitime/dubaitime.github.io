Dubai’s dynamic infrastructure system includes an efficient and reliable system of public transportation, including metro, tram, bus and ferry, which serve the residents and visitors of the city. Bus C19 is one of the important routes of the extensive network which connects Deira City Center to Al Qusais. It is a vital commuter link between commercial centers, residential areas and major institutions.

Written in this article, we will get to know more about Bus C19 in details from its working schedule, route overview, and some useful tips for its ride specification.

New Route C26 Replaces C19 Bus Route

This C19 bus route has been still in old and unused form. Station DNATA 2 on this new line is a bus stop that is located alongside Deira City Centre.

## Route Overview

The Bus C19 route is an easy way to travel between Deira and Al Qusais and serves a number of important residential, commercial and service locations. The route includes the following notable stops:

1. Deira City Center Bus Station  A major commercial and transportation hub in Deira.

2. City Centre Metro Bus Stop A2  Located near the City Centre Deira Metro Station for easy transfers.

3. DNATA  The headquarters of the Dubai National Air Transport Association.

4. Ministry of Environment  A significant government office overseeing environmental regulations.

5. Al Bakhit Center  A local shopping and service center offering a variety of amenities.

6. Hamarain Center  A popular retail destination in the heart of Deira.

7. Abu Baker Al Siddique Metro Bus Stop 1  Close to the Abu Baker Al Siddique Metro Station, offering easy connections.

8. Dubai Scout Mission  A community hub for Dubai Scouts, hosting various events.

9. Al Jadeed Bakery  A famous bakery known for its local specialties.

## Journey Experience

A long trip but nevertheless, an interesting journey on a Bus C19 from Deira City Center to Al Qusais takes you through some of Dubai’s most dynamic areas.

The contrast between modern development and older, traditional buildings as it moves through the Al Qusais Industrial Area gives a unique look at Dubai’s rapid growth. The route also takes passengers past Dubai’s hotspots including the Union Cooperative Society in Al Twar and the Damascus Road area. This RTA bus route is not only the one that takes you close to places like Hamarain Center and Ahli Club, but it also gives you the opportunity to see the commercial as well as the residential side of Dubai.

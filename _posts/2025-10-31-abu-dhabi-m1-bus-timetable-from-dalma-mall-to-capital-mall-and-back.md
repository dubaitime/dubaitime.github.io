Hafilat cards can be easily topped up online or at kiosks located throughout the city, ensuring a seamless travel experience.

## Accessibility and Services

The M1 bus is designed to be accessible for all passengers, ensuring a comfortable journey for everyone. Features include:

- Ramps for wheelchair access.

- Designated seating for those with mobility challenges.
- Audio-visual aids for passengers with hearing or vision impairments.

These facilities make the M1 bus an inclusive travel option for all commuters.

## Travel Tips for Bus M1

- Plan Ahead: Check the bus timings to avoid long waits, especially during peak hours.

- Use the Hafilat Smart Card: This allows for quick and cashless payment on all Abu Dhabi buses.
- Monitor Bus Updates: Use the AD Mobility app for real-time updates and schedules to optimize your journey.
- Avoid Peak Times: For a more comfortable ride, consider traveling outside of busy periods in the morning and evening.

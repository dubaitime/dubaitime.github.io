## Bus Ticket Price

The fare for the E306 bus route is very reasonable, making it an attractive option for those commuting between Dubai and Sharjah. The ticket price is typically around AED 15-20, depending on the distance traveled. Passengers can pay using their Nol cards, which can be topped up online or at various ticket machines across Dubai.

Other RTA Dubai bus routes worth considering include the F62, which runs between Nadd Al Hamar and Umm Ramool before returning to Nadd Al Hamar, and the E411, connecting Ajman Al Musallah with Etisalat Station.

## Bus E306 Stations Overview

### Ghubaiba Bus Station · 14

Located in the historic district of Bur Dubai, it offers passengers easy access to the surrounding cultural and commercial attractions.

The station is well-equipped with modern amenities such as waiting areas, ticket counters, and real-time information displays, ensuring a comfortable and efficient travel experience.

### Al Wahda Rd, Subway 1-2 Sharjah Bus Stop Intersection

The Al Wahda Rd, Subway 1-2 Sharjah Bus Stop is a key transit hub where Bus Routes E306, E307A, E307, E303, and E304 intersect. This central location provides seamless connections across Sharjah and beyond, making it easier for passengers to access various destinations. Its strategic position ensures convenient travel options for daily commuters and travelers in the region.

### Sharjah — Al Jubail Bus Station · 1

As the terminus for Bus E306, it plays a vital role in linking Sharjah to Dubai's Ghubaiba Bus Station.

Its strategic location near Sharjah’s central market and other key landmarks makes it a convenient choice for both daily commuters and occasional travelers.

## Journey Experience

Starting from Al Ghubaiba, passengers pass traditional souks and Dubai Creek, a key historical waterway.

A unique highlight is the scenic Sharjah Corniche, showcasing the city’s waterfront charm. Though the journey is lengthy, it provides a captivating blend of historical landmarks and cultural attractions, making the trip both scenic and enriching.

## Travelling with Careem

Using the Careem app, you can easily reach Sharjah — Al Jubail Bus Station, which is the final stop on the E306 bus route, starting from Ghubaiba Bus Station. The ride offers a convenient and direct option, with a price range of AED 81-93.

In summary, Bus E306 offers a reliable and economical option for those traveling between Dubai and Sharjah, with convenient stops and frequent timings. Whether you're commuting for work or exploring the city, this bus service ensures a smooth and hassle-free journey.

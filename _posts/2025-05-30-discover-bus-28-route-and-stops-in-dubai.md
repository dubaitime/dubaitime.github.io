## Bus Ticket Price

The fare for Bus Route 28 typically ranges from AED 4 to AED 7, depending on the distance traveled. This pricing offers a cost-effective way to traverse various parts of Dubai, ensuring good value for the extensive route coverage.

## Bus Booking

While Bus Route 28 does not offer online booking options, tickets can be purchased directly at the bus station or on board the bus. For added convenience, passengers can use the RTA's Nol card system, which is accepted on all public transport within Dubai. This electronic fare card simplifies the payment process and can be recharged as needed, making travel across Dubai's public transport system seamless and efficient.

## Journey Experience

The bus route 28 from The Dubai Mall to Oud Metha Bus Station is a long but captivating journey that takes passengers through Dubai’s vibrant and diverse cityscape.

As the journey continues, the bus transitions into quieter areas like Karama, known for its traditional markets and street food, before arriving at Oud Metha, a lively district filled with schools, parks, and cultural spots like Dubai Creek Park and Wafi Mall. Despite its length, the route offers a fascinating mix of Dubai’s modern landmarks, historical charm, and community life, making it an enriching trip for both residents and visitors.

## Accessibility and Services

Bus B47 is equipped to accommodate passengers with special needs, ensuring that everyone can enjoy a comfortable journey. The buses feature:

- Ramps for wheelchair access.

- Designated seating for individuals with mobility challenges.
- Audio-visual aids for passengers with hearing or vision impairments.

This makes Bus B47 an inclusive option for all commuters.

## Journey Experience

Taking Bus B47 from Fatima College to Zaid Bin Al Mualla Mosque offers travelers an engaging journey through Abu Dhabi's vibrant landscape.

## Travel Tips for Bus B47

- Plan Ahead: Check the bus timings to avoid unnecessary waiting, especially during peak hours.

- Use the Hafilat Smart Card: This allows for quick, cashless payment on all Abu Dhabi buses.
- Monitor Bus Updates: Utilize the AD Mobility app for real-time updates and schedules to optimize your journey.
- Explore Local Stops: Take time to visit the attractions near each bus stop for a richer travel experience.

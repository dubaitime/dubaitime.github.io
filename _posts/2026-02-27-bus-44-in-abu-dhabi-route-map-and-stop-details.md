## Bus 44 Station Overview

### Cruise Terminal

The Cruise Terminal bus station is a key transit hub located near Abu Dhabi's port facilities, serving both international cruise passengers and local travelers. Positioned adjacent to the terminal, this station provides convenient access for those arriving or departing from cruise ships.

It offers essential services and amenities to ensure a smooth transition between maritime travel and land transportation. The station's strategic location facilitates easy access to the waterfront and surrounding attractions, making it a vital stop for tourists and cruise travelers.

### Abu Dhabi Ladies Club

The Abu Dhabi Ladies Club bus station is situated close to the renowned women’s recreational and social facility. The station supports easy access for those participating in events, fitness activities, or social gatherings at the club. Well-equipped with amenities, it provides a comfortable and practical stop for women and families visiting the club.

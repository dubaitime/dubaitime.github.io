Ticket Pricing: Travel on the X5 bus route requires a Hafilat Card, Abu Dhabi's cashless payment system. The fare is AED 2 for one zone, with an extra AED 0.05 per kilometer for longer distances. Passengers can quickly top up their cards online or at kiosks around the city, making for a hassle-free, cashless travel experience.

## Accessibility and Services

The buses feature:

- Ramps for wheelchair access.

- Designated seating for individuals with mobility challenges.
- Audio-visual aids to assist passengers with hearing or vision impairments.

This commitment to inclusivity ensures that everyone can enjoy a comfortable journey on the X5 route.

## Journey Experience

On a recent trip on the X5 bus from Corniche Hospital to Al Maqta Bus Interchange, passengers enjoyed a scenic view of Abu Dhabi's urban landscape. The journey began near the beautiful Corniche beach and continued along Zayed The First Street, showcasing Electra Park, a green oasis in the city.

Stops at Al Noor Hospital and the Abu Dhabi Men’s College connected the community to essential services and education. The trip concluded at Al Maqta Bus Interchange, enhancing accessibility throughout the city.

## Travel Tips for Bus X5

- Plan Ahead: Check the bus timings to minimize waiting times, especially during peak hours.

- Use the Hafilat Smart Card: This enables quick, cashless payments on all Abu Dhabi buses.
- Monitor Bus Updates: Utilize the AD Mobility app for real-time updates on schedules and routes.
- Avoid Peak Times: For a more comfortable journey, try to travel outside of busy periods.

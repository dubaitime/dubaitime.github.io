## Bus 67 Station Overview

### Endurance City Terminus

This station is designed to handle a significant volume of travelers with amenities such as sheltered waiting areas, ticketing services, and up-to-date schedule information.

The station’s facilities ensure a comfortable and convenient travel experience for all passengers.

### Intersecting Bus Routes at Burjuman Metro Bus Stop

The Burjuman Metro Bus Stop serves as a major transit hub where several bus routes intersect, enhancing connectivity for passengers throughout Dubai. Key routes that converge at this station include 66, 67, 61D, C03, C18, F70, X23, and X25.

This central location provides seamless travel options across different city areas, making it a vital stop for commuters and ensuring efficient access to various destinations.

### Ghubaiba Bus Station · 49

Ghubaiba Bus Station · 49 is an essential transit hub situated in the Ghubiba area of Dubai. It is well-equipped to handle a high flow of passengers, featuring amenities such as covered waiting areas, ticketing counters, and real-time bus schedule updates.

This station’s central location makes it a key point for accessing various local and regional destinations, offering seamless connectivity for commuters. Its proximity to major commercial and residential areas ensures that it serves as a vital part of the public transportation network, facilitating convenient and efficient travel.

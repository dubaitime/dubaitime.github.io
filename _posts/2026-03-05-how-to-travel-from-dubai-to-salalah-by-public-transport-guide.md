Traveling from Dubai to Salalah offers a unique opportunity to witness the changing landscapes between the UAE and Oman. Whether you're embarking on a scenic adventure or planning a budget-friendly journey, here's a comprehensive guide on how to travel between these two cities by public transport.

## Overview of Bus Travel: Duration, Cost, and Distance

The journey from Dubai to Salalah by bus is a long yet comfortable experience, spanning approximately 19 hours and 35 minutes. This extended travel time provides ample opportunity to enjoy the diverse landscapes and scenic views along the route.

Operated by Mwasalat, a leading transport provider in Oman, the buses ensure a safe and enjoyable journey for passengers. The cost of a bus ticket ranges from AED 125 to AED 183, making it an affordable choice for exploring Oman from the UAE. The distance between the two cities is approximately 910.9 km, with a road distance of around 1217.8 km.

## Catching the Bus

- From Dubai: Bus services from Dubai to Salalah, operated by Mwasalat, depart from Al Jubail Terminal station.

- Arrival in Salalah: The buses arrive at Al Azaiba station in Salalah.

## Accommodation Near Salalah

Upon arrival in Salalah, you'll find a wide range of accommodation options to suit different budgets. There are over 308 hotels available, with prices starting at AED 21 per night, catering to a variety of preferences. Additionally, many of these hotels offer convenient access to local attractions and amenities, enhancing your stay in this beautiful city.

## Additional Tips

- Check for Updates: Before your journey, check the latest schedules and travel updates on the Mwasalat website or contact their customer service for any changes or delays.
- Comfort Essentials: Bring along essentials for a long journey, such as water, snacks, a travel pillow, and entertainment like books or music, to make your trip more comfortable.
- Prepare for Transfers: Since there are no direct buses, be prepared for transfers at various points along the route. Keep your belongings secure and allow extra time for transfers.
- Travel Documents: Ensure you have all necessary travel documents, including your passport and any required visas, ready for inspection at checkpoints.
- Local Currency: Have some Omani rials on hand for any purchases or expenses during your stay in Salalah, as not all places may accept credit cards.

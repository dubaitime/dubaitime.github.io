## Bus F10 Station Overview

### Rashidiya Bus Station Parking

Rashidiya Bus Station Parking is a key facility located at the Rashidiya Bus Station, providing ample parking space for commuters and transit users. This station serves as a major transportation hub in Dubai, equipped with essential amenities such as comfortable seating, ticketing services, and real-time schedule updates.

The parking area is designed to accommodate a high volume of vehicles, making it convenient for passengers to park and access the bus services. Its integration with other transport options, including the Dubai Metro, ensures seamless connectivity to various parts of the city.

This strategic location provides seamless connections between these routes, facilitating convenient travel for passengers in the Al Warqa’a area. The stop supports efficient transit options and easy access to various destinations, enhancing travel convenience for local commuters.

### Intersection at Al Warqa’a

The intersection of Bus Routes F10 and F61 occurs at two significant stops in Al Warqa’a: Mass Supermarket and ASCANA 3 Building. The Mass Supermarket stop serves as a key point where both routes converge, providing convenient access to shopping and local amenities.

Similarly, the ASCANA 3 Building stop is another crucial intersection, linking these routes and offering connectivity to nearby residential and commercial areas. Both stops are equipped with essential amenities and real-time schedule updates, ensuring a seamless travel experience for passengers in the Al Warqa’a area.

### Dubai Safari Park

The Dubai Safari Park bus station serves as the gateway to the Dubai Safari Park, one of the city's premier wildlife attractions. This station is strategically located to provide easy access for visitors traveling to and from the park. It features essential amenities such as sheltered waiting areas and schedule displays to assist passengers.

Its proximity to the safari park makes it a convenient stop for families and tourists looking to explore the diverse wildlife and attractions within the park. The station plays a vital role in facilitating transportation to this popular destination, enhancing the overall visitor experience.

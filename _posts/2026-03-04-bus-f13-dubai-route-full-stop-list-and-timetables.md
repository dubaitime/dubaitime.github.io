## Bus F13 Station Overview

### Dubai Mall Metro Landside · 2

Dubai Mall Metro Landside Station is a key transportation hub located adjacent to the Dubai Mall, one of the city's major shopping and entertainment destinations. This station is well-equipped with amenities such as ticketing services and sheltered waiting areas. Its proximity to the Dubai Mall and the Burj Khalifa area makes it a convenient stop for both shopping enthusiasts and tourists exploring central Dubai. The station’s strategic location supports high passenger volumes, especially during peak shopping hours and events.

### 8 Boulevard Walk Tower

The 8 Boulevard Walk Tower station is a crucial transit hub situated in the heart of the Boulevard area, which is renowned for its residential and commercial developments. Positioned close to key attractions and office buildings, this station offers essential services for both daily commuters and visitors.

It features modern amenities such as comfortable waiting areas and real-time schedule updates, ensuring a smooth and efficient travel experience. Additionally, the station intersects with Bus Route 26, enhancing connectivity and providing convenient access to various destinations within the city. Its central location makes it an ideal point for reaching nearby amenities and destinations.

## Bus C28 Station Overview

### Mamzar Beach Park Terminus

Mamzar Beach Park Terminus is a prominent bus station located near the popular Mamzar Beach Park in Dubai. It serves as a key transit hub for passengers visiting the park and the surrounding area.

The station is well-equipped with essential amenities, including sheltered waiting areas and real-time bus schedule information, ensuring a comfortable and efficient travel experience. Its proximity to the beach park makes it a convenient choice for those heading to enjoy recreational activities and leisure time at one of Dubai’s scenic waterfronts.

### Intersection at Abu Hail, Masjid Al Salam 1 Bus Stop

The Abu Hail, Masjid Al Salam 1 Bus Stop serves as a crucial intersection point for multiple bus routes, including Bus Route C3 and Bus Route C28. This central location facilitates convenient transfers between these routes, enhancing connectivity for passengers traveling across various areas. The stop is well-equipped with essential amenities, providing a seamless transit experience and ensuring efficient travel throughout the city.

### Al Ras Metro Bus Stop Intersection

This strategic location provides seamless connectivity for passengers traveling across various parts of the city, making it a vital hub for efficient travel and convenient transfers between different bus services.

### Al Baraha Bus Station

Al Baraha Bus Station is a central transit point located in the Al Baraha area of Dubai. This bus station is a vital component of the local transportation network, providing access to various bus routes and connecting passengers to key destinations across the city.

The station features comfortable seating, ticketing services, and up-to-date schedule information, making it an essential hub for daily commuters and travelers.

## Bus Ticket Price

The fare for Bus 96 follows Dubai's standard public transport pricing. A single journey generally costs AED 5-12 with a Nol Card. The exact fare may vary depending on the card type and the number of travel zones crossed. For the most accurate fare information, passengers should consult the RTA Dubai app or check at metro stations. Remember to always have sufficient balance on your Nol Card for a seamless journey.

## Bus 96 Station Overview

### Al Quoz Bus Station · 8

Al Quoz Bus Station is a significant transit hub located in the heart of the Al Quoz industrial and commercial district. It serves as a vital connection point for various bus routes, facilitating travel across the city. The station is equipped with essential amenities including ticket counters, waiting areas, and restrooms, ensuring a convenient and efficient travel experience for both daily commuters and visitors. Its central location within a busy business area makes it a key stop for those traveling to and from Al Quoz.

### Danata (Dubai Investment Park)

Danata Bus Station, situated in Dubai Investment Park, provides crucial connectivity for residents and workers in this growing area. Located close to various commercial and industrial zones, the station supports seamless travel to and from major business hubs.

It features basic amenities such as seating and schedule information, catering to the needs of those commuting within the park and to other parts of Dubai. The station's placement in a prominent industrial area highlights its role in facilitating efficient transportation for a diverse range of passengers.

## Journey Experience

As the bus departs from Al Quoz, you will find yourself amidst a dynamic area known for its art scene, featuring numerous galleries and studios that attract artists and art lovers alike.

The route takes you through various neighborhoods, revealing the diverse architectural styles and urban landscapes that characterize Dubai. Notably, you'll pass close to the Mall of the Emirates, a major shopping destination famed for its indoor ski slope and luxury brands, making it a landmark of leisure and shopping.

As the bus continues towards Danata, the journey transitions into the more industrial zones of Dubai, where you can observe various commercial and logistical operations. Dubai Investment Park itself is a fascinating area, designed to support businesses and industries, showcasing the emirate’s focus on economic diversification. This trip not only highlights the contrasts within Dubai but also provides insights into its growth and development, making it an informative and enjoyable experience.

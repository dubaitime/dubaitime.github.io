## Bus F70 Station Overview

### Oud Metha Bus Station · 3

Oud Metha Bus Station · 3 is a significant transportation hub located in the Oud Metha area, serving as a major stop for various bus routes. This station is equipped with essential amenities, including sheltered waiting areas, ticketing services, and real-time bus schedule updates.

Its strategic location in a bustling commercial and residential neighborhood ensures easy access to local shops, services, and nearby attractions. The station’s facilities and central position make it a crucial point for commuters traveling within and beyond the Oud Metha area.

### Intersecting Bus Routes at Burjuman Metro Bus Stop

The Burjuman Metro Bus Stop serves as a major transit hub where several bus routes intersect, enhancing connectivity for passengers throughout Dubai. Key routes that converge at this station include 66, 67, 61D, C03, C18, F70, X23, and X25.

This central location provides seamless travel options across different city areas, making it a vital stop for commuters and ensuring efficient access to various destinations.

### Fahidi, Roundabout

Fahidi, Roundabout is a key bus stop situated at a major intersection in the historic Al Fahidi neighborhood. This station serves as a convenient transit point for passengers traveling through the area. It is well-equipped with amenities such as covered waiting areas and schedule information displays to ensure a comfortable and efficient travel experience.

Its location at the roundabout offers seamless connections to various local landmarks, commercial zones, and cultural sites, making it an important stop for both residents and visitors in the Fahidi area.

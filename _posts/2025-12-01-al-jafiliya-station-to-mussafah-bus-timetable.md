## Tips for Traveling from Al Jafiliya Station to Mussafah

- Load Your Nol Card: Check your Nol Card balance before you start your journey. Dubai’s easiest way to pay for public transport.
- Take the Metro and Bus Combination: From Al Jafiliya Metro Station on Dubai Metro Red Line, start your journey until Ibn Battuta Metro Station.
- Check Abu Dhabi Bus Services: After you arrive at Abu Dhabi Central Bus Station you can take a local bus or taxi to Mussafah. Abu Dhabi Public Transport app has the full routes and schedules detailed for your convenience.
- Plan for Traffic: To help you with your travel at a more graceful pace minus extra delays, the timings are between 00:00 AM to 9:00 AM as well as 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM.
- Stay Comfortable: It is better to wear breathable clothing, and wear comfortable shoes as you could have to transfer from one mode of transportation to another.
- Carry Snacks and Water: The travel might take a couple hours, therefore keep snacks on hand and a water bottle to keep you hydrated on your way.

## Route Overview

Starting at Rolla Terminal, Route 88X takes passengers through several key stops, ending at Al Saja’a Terminal. The journey covers important commercial, residential, and industrial areas, ensuring commuters have access to essential services and locations. Major stops along the route include:
1. Jubail terminal

2. Maliha Road

3. Wasit Street

4. Sharjah Airport

5. Emirates Identity Authority

6. Al Rahmaniyah Area

7. Al Saja'a New Industrial Area

Route 88X is a reliable and cost-effective transportation solution connecting Rolla and Al Saja’a. Whether you’re commuting for work or running daily errands, Route 88X ensures a convenient and seamless journey through some of Sharjah’s most important areas.

Abu Dhabi’s public transportation system offers a reliable and affordable way to navigate the city and its surroundings. Among its many routes, the X60 bus stands out for its journey from Abu Dhabi Bus Station to Liwa Mezaira’a Bus Station, connecting key destinations along the way. This guide provides an overview of the X60 route, detailing operational timings, major stops, and useful travel tips to enhance your journey.

Ticket Pricing: Passengers can conveniently pay their fare using the Hafilat Card, which is Abu Dhabi's cashless payment system. The fare for journeys within a single zone is AED 2, with an additional charge of AED 0.05 per kilometer for any extra distance. Hafilat cards can be topped up easily at various kiosks throughout the city, providing a smooth and hassle-free travel experience.

## Route Overview

The X60 bus route connects a variety of essential stops, catering to the needs of both commuters and tourists. Here’s a breakdown of key stops along the way:
1. Abu Dhabi Bus Station (Start): The primary transportation hub in the city.

2. Musaffah — Mohamed Bin Zayed City Bus Station: A critical interchange for local transit.

3. Madinat Zayed Bus Station: Serves as a gateway to the western region and nearby attractions.

4. Mezaira'a Bus Station: The endpoint, providing access to the Liwa area.

This well-planned route is designed to meet the practical needs of residents while also offering visitors an opportunity to explore significant areas around Abu Dhabi.

For other convenient options, check out the A40 bus that runs from Al Shahama Bus Station to Baniyas West Bus Station, or take the M2, which loops back to Mazyad Mall—both are excellent choices for navigating the area!

## Journey Experience

On a recent trip on Bus X60 from Abu Dhabi Bus Station to Liwa Mezaira’a Bus Station, travelers experienced a scenic journey through the city's vibrant landscape. ## Travel Tips for Bus X60

- Plan Your Trip: Review the bus schedule to avoid peak hours and ensure timely departures.

- Use a Hafilat Card: This payment method streamlines your journey and eliminates cash handling.
- Monitor Real-Time Updates: Utilize the AD Mobility app for live schedule updates to avoid delays.
- Travel During Off-Peak Hours: For a quieter ride, consider traveling outside of rush hours.

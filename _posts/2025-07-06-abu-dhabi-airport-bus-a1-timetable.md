## Route Overview

Key stops and areas served by A1 include:

- City Air Terminal
- Abu Dhabi Mall area
- Electra Park
- Madinat Zayed Shopping Centre
- Abu Dhabi Bus Station
- Mushrif Mall
- ADNEC
- Zayed Sports Stadium
- Airport terminals and cargo area
- Airport Free Zone
- Ghurair Al Ojaan Mosque

## Why Riders Use A1

- Airport-to-city travel without booking a taxi
- Budget-conscious trips into Abu Dhabi
- Access to major city transfer points such as the bus station and central commercial areas

## Travel Tips

- Confirm your closest terminal stop before boarding, especially if you are arriving from a flight.
- Keep a small time buffer because airport-area boarding can vary with traffic and passenger demand.
- If you are continuing deeper into Abu Dhabi, plan your onward bus or taxi transfer before leaving the airport.

## Summary

The A1 is a useful airport bus for travelers who want a direct public transport link from Abu Dhabi Airport ECC into the city. Save the timetable, check the official PDF for live updates, and plan your onward stop in advance if you are traveling with luggage.

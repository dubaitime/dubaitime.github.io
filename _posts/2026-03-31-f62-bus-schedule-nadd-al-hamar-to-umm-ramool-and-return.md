### Ticket Pricing

Bus F62 offers an affordable fare of AED 5 for travel between Nadd Al Hamar and Umm Ramool, making it an economical option for both daily commuters and occasional travelers. The fare structure for Dubai’s public transport system is based on the number of zones traveled, and since the F62 route typically covers one zone, the fare remains consistent at AED 5.

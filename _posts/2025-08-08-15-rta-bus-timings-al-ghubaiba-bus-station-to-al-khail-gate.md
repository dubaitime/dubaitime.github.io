## Accessibility Features

The 15 bus service is designed with inclusivity in mind. The fleet includes low-floor buses to facilitate easy boarding, designated seating for passengers with disabilities, and audio-visual aids to assist those with hearing or vision impairments. These features ensure that the 15 bus route accommodates all passengers, providing a comfortable travel experience.

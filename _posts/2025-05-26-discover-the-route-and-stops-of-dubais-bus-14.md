## Bus 14 Station Overview

### Oud Metha Bus Station · 8

Oud Metha Bus Station is a well-known transit hub in Dubai, offering a variety of amenities for passengers. Located in the bustling Oud Metha area, this station serves as a key stop for Bus 14, providing easy access to nearby schools, healthcare facilities, and shopping centers. The station's strategic location makes it an essential point for both daily commuters and those traveling through the area, with well-maintained facilities ensuring a comfortable transit experience.

### Business Bay Metro Station Seaside Bus Stop Intersection

Business Bay Metro Station Seaside Bus Stop is a crucial intersection where Bus Routes 7, 9, 14, 91, and F20 converge. This stop serves as a vital connection point for passengers traveling within the Business Bay area and to various destinations across Dubai. Its strategic location near major business and residential areas makes it a convenient choice for daily commuters and travelers seeking easy access to key parts of the city.

### Safa, Terminus

The Safa Terminus is the final stop on Bus 14's route, situated in the tranquil Safa area. This station provides passengers with access to nearby parks, residential areas, and local attractions. As a terminus, it marks the end of the route and is equipped with essential amenities for travelers concluding their journey.

## Journey Experience

The 14 bus route from Oud Metha Bus Station to Safa, Terminus, and back offers an extensive and intriguing journey through some of Dubai's noteworthy neighborhoods.

The route continues towards Safa, where travelers are treated to the lush greenery of Safa Park, a perfect spot for relaxation and picnics amid the bustling city.

The return journey allows for additional sightseeing opportunities, as the bus meanders through both modern developments and historical sites.

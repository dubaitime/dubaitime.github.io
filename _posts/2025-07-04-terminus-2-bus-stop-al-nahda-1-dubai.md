## Bus Route Details

The Al Nahda 1, Terminus 2 Bus Stop is served by several key bus routes:

- F23 Route: Runs frequently to Al Nahda 1, Terminus, with departures starting at 5:13 AM and continuing until 10:04 PM.

- 20A Route: Connects Al Nahda with Al Warsan 3, with buses operating from 5:34 AM to 8:29 AM and resuming from 12:35 PM to 3:34 PM. This route is suitable for those traveling to Al Warsan 3.
- 24 Route: Provides connections between Al Nahda and Dubai Festival City, with service from 5:36 AM to 10:01 PM. It offers a reliable option for those traveling to Dubai Festival City or nearby areas.
- 22 Route: Links Al Nahda with City Centre MS, operating from 6:40 AM to 9:35 PM.
- F23A Route: Runs to Stadium Bus Station, with departures from 6:13 AM to 4:43 PM.

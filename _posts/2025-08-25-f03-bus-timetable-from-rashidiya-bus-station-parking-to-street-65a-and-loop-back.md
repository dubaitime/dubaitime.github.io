Ticket Pricing: The cash ticket for Bus F03 is priced at AED 3, with payments made using the Nol card, a convenient cashless option for Dubai’s public transport system. The Nol card can be easily topped up at various locations throughout the city, ensuring smooth and hassle-free travel.

## Route Overview

The F03 bus route starts at Rashidiya Bus Station Parking and makes its way through key residential, shopping, and community areas before returning to the station. Below are some notable stops along the route:
1. Rashidiya Bus Station Parking  The starting and final stop of the route.

2. Etihad Mall  A popular shopping center with retail and dining options.

3. Mirdiff, Private School  A stop located near educational institutions in Mirdiff.

4. Uptown Mirdif  A major residential and commercial area with amenities.

5. Etisalat Tower  A stop near a key telecommunications hub.

6. Elyazia Beauty Center  Catering to visitors of local beauty and wellness services.

7. Mirdiff Masjid, Street 15  A community stop for local residents.

8. 12th Street  Access to residential neighborhoods.

9. Street 29B  A stop serving local residential areas.

10. Street 65A  Another key residential stop in the Mirdiff area.

11. 47th Street  Providing access to additional Mirdiff neighborhoods.

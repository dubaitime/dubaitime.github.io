The standard fare is AED 3 when using the Nol card, while discounted fares are available for students, senior citizens, and people of determination through the Nol card system.

## Bus F49 Station Overview

### Danube Metro Landside

Danube Metro Landside is a key station on Dubai's Red Line, offering seamless connections for commuters. Serving as a crucial transfer point for Bus Routes F47 and F49, this station provides easy access to nearby commercial and residential areas. Its proximity to the Danube outlet also makes it a popular choice for shoppers and those working in the area.

### Cleenol Middle East

Cleenol Middle East bus stop is strategically located near the industrial and commercial hub, catering primarily to employees and visitors of nearby businesses. This stop on the Bus F49 route provides essential connectivity to surrounding areas and offers a straightforward commute for those working in the industrial zone. Although it is a more functional stop, its proximity to key workplaces ensures that it remains a critical point on the F49 route, facilitating daily travel for many professionals.

If you're looking for a quick and budget-friendly way to travel from Union Metro Station to Dubai's Global Village, this guide will help you navigate your trip. Below, we’ll cover the bus route, timing, fare details, and travel tips to make your journey smooth and enjoyable.

## Bus Line 103 from Union Metro Station to Global Village

For the most straightforward route to Global Village, Bus Line 103 offers a direct service with minimal hassle and a reasonable fare.

- Route: Union Metro Station (Deira) to Global Village

- Estimated Travel Time: 1 hour 5 minutes
- Fare: AED 8-12

Journey Overview:

Once you arrive at Union Metro Station, head towards Union Square Bus Station, a short 10-minute walk away.

Step 2: Catch the 103 Bus at Union Square Bus Station At Union Square Bus Station, look for the 103 Bus heading towards Global Village. This direct bus line operates hourly and provides a convenient ride with only a few stops.

- Bus Line: 103

- Key Stops: Rebat Street and other convenient stops along the way
- Duration: Approximately 1 hour from Union Square to Global Village

Step 3: Arrive at Global Village The 103 Bus will take you directly to the entrance of Global Village, saving you time and additional transfers. You’ll be dropped off at the Global Village bus stop, making it easy to jump right into your day of exploration.

## Travel Tips

- Check the Schedule: The 103 Bus operates hourly, so planning your departure time can help you avoid long waits. You can check bus schedules on the RTA website or the RTA app for real-time information.

- Avoid Peak Times: If possible, avoid peak travel hours, typically from 5 PM to 7 PM, when buses can become crowded.
- Enjoy Nearby Attractions: If you have some time before or after your visit to Global Village, Deira is full of unique attractions like the Gold Souk and Spice Souk, just a short metro ride from Union Station.
- Global Village Hours: Make sure to check the Global Village opening hours, as they vary by season and day of the week. This helps you make the most of your time at the attraction.

Some other interesting trips you can explore are from Union Metro Station to Al Ghubaiba Bus Station and the trip from Union Metro Station to Sharjah.

Traveling from Union Metro Station to Global Village is a convenient, affordable, and straightforward journey using Dubai’s well-connected bus system. The 103 Bus line provides a direct route with minimal stops, making it a great option for visitors who want a hassle-free experience. Whether you’re exploring the bustling souks of Deira or getting ready to dive into the diverse experiences at Global Village, following these steps will ensure you have a smooth journey. Enjoy your trip!

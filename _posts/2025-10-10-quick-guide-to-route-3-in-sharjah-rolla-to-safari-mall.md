Route 3 is a vital bus route in Sharjah, connecting the bustling Rolla area to the popular Safari Mall. This route is essential for daily commuters and shoppers, providing a convenient and affordable way to navigate between these key locations. With frequent services and a range of accessible stops, it plays a crucial role in the city’s public transport network.

## Route Overview

The journey begins at Rolla Terminal and ends at Safari Mall Terminal. Key stops along Route 3 include:
1. Al Jubail Terminal

2. Central Souq

3. King Faisal Street

4. Jamal Abdul Naser Street

5. Al Khan Street

6. Second Industrial Street

7. Maliha Road

8. Muweilah Terminal

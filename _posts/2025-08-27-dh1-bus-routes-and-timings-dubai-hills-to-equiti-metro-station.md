## Accessibility and Services

The DH1 Bus is designed with inclusivity in mind, offering:

- Wheelchair-accessible ramps.

- Priority seating for passengers with mobility challenges.
- Audio-visual announcements for passengers with hearing or vision impairments.

These features ensure that all commuters can travel comfortably and safely.

Taking the DH1 bus from Dubai Hills to Equiti Metro Station offers a relaxing and scenic ride through some of Dubai’s most vibrant areas.

## Travel Tips for the DH1 Bus

- Plan Ahead: Check the DH1 schedule in advance to avoid long waiting times.

- Use the Nol Card: Ensure your Nol Card has sufficient balance for a hassle-free journey.
- Travel Off-Peak: For a more relaxed ride, consider avoiding peak hours.
- Stay Informed: Download the RTA app for real-time bus updates and route information.

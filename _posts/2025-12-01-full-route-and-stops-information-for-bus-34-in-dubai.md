## Bus 34 Station Overview

### Al Khawaneej 2, Industrial Area 4

The Al Khawaneej 2, Industrial Area 4 bus station is strategically positioned to serve the industrial and commercial sectors of Al Khawaneej. This station provides essential transit services for workers and businesses in the area, featuring amenities such as sheltered waiting areas, ticketing services, and real-time schedule updates.

Its location within a key industrial zone ensures convenient access for those working in or visiting the numerous commercial establishments and industrial facilities nearby. The station plays a crucial role in supporting efficient transportation and connectivity within this bustling area.

### Intersection of Bus Routes at Muhaisnah 4, Jamal Building

The bus stop at Muhaisnah 4, Jamal Building is a significant intersection for Bus Routes X28, 34, and 56. This key location connects these routes, providing efficient transit options and easy access to various destinations within the Muhaisnah area and beyond. The stop is equipped with essential amenities and real-time schedule updates to enhance the travel experience for passengers using these intersecting bus services.

### Intersection of Multiple Bus Routes at Al Qusais Bus Station

Al Qusais Bus Station is a vital transit hub in Dubai, where several important bus routes intersect, providing passengers with easy connections across the city. Routes 13A, 13B, 13D, 32C, and 33 all meet at this station, offering a wide range of travel options.

These routes cover various key locations within Dubai, making Al Qusais a strategic point for commuters seeking to switch buses efficiently.

### Etisalat Bus Station · 20

Etisalat Bus Station · 20 is a major transit hub located near the Etisalat Building, a significant landmark in Dubai’s telecommunications sector. This station is well-equipped with modern amenities, including sheltered waiting areas, ticketing services, and up-to-date bus schedule information.

Its proximity to the Etisalat Building makes it a convenient stop for employees, clients, and visitors associated with the telecommunications giant. The station’s facilities and strategic location enhance accessibility and provide efficient transportation solutions for those traveling to and from the Etisalat complex.

Bus 34 in Dubai travels through several areas that include notable landmarks and potential points of interest:

5. Etisalat Bus Station · 20: This station is close to several commercial and business areas, providing access to various services and local attractions.

Although the route mainly serves industrial and residential areas, it does pass by a few locations where local cultural and community experiences can be enjoyed.

Ticket Pricing: Payments for Bus M5 can be made using the Hafilat Card, Abu Dhabi’s convenient cashless payment system. For journeys within a single zone, fares are AED 2, with an extra charge of AED 0.05 per kilometer for additional distance. Passengers can easily top up their Hafilat cards online or at kiosks throughout the city, ensuring a hassle-free, cashless experience.

## Travel Tips for Bus M5

- Plan Ahead: Check the bus schedule to avoid peak travel times and ensure timely departures.

- Use a Hafilat Card: This makes payments quick and hassle-free, allowing for a smooth travel experience across Abu Dhabi.
- Monitor Real-Time Updates: Use the AD Mobility app to stay updated on schedules and avoid unexpected delays.
- Avoid Peak Hours: If you prefer a quieter ride, consider traveling during off-peak hours.

Ticket Pricing: The fare structure for Reg Bus 121 is simple and cost-effective, making it accessible for all passengers:

- Cash Ticket: AED 2.00, with an additional AED 0.05 per kilometer.

## Travel Tips

- Travel during off-peak hours: This will help you avoid crowds, especially at popular stops like Mazyad Mall or Abu Dhabi Bus Station.

- Use the Department of Transport App: Download the Abu Dhabi Department of Transport app for real-time updates on bus schedules and any potential delays.
- Carry a portable phone charger: Keep your devices powered during the trip, especially if you rely on your phone for navigation or communication.
- Plan ahead: Check the bus timings in advance to ensure smooth travel between appointments or activities.

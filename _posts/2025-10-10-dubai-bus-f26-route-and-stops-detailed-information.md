## Bus F26 Station Overview

### Equiti Metro Landside

The Equiti Metro Landside bus station is situated adjacent to the Equiti Metro Station, enhancing connectivity between bus and metro services. Located in a bustling area, this station serves as a critical transit point for passengers traveling to and from the metro, offering modern amenities such as comfortable seating, ticketing services, and real-time schedule updates.

Its integration with the metro system allows for seamless transfers, making it a convenient stop for daily commuters and travelers heading to nearby commercial and residential areas.

### Onpassive Terminus

The Onpassive Terminus is a key bus station located in the vicinity of Onpassive's business operations.

Its location supports easy access to the Onpassive offices and surrounding businesses, providing a practical transit solution for those working in or visiting the area. The station plays an important role in facilitating transportation within the business district and connecting passengers to other parts of the city.

## Bus Ticket Price

The bus ticket price for the F26 route is budget-friendly (typically around AED 3-5), offering an economical way for commuters to travel between Equiti Metro Landside and Onpassive Terminus. Prices are affordable and cater to both regular commuters and occasional travelers.

## Bus Booking

Bus tickets for the F26 route can be purchased directly from the bus driver upon boarding. While specific online booking options may not be available, passengers can check schedules and plan their journeys using official Dubai transport apps or websites.

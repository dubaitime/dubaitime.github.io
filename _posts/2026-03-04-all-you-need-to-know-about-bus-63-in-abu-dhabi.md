## Bus Ticket Price

As of February 2024, the standard bus fare in Abu Dhabi is AED 2, plus an additional AED 0.05 for every kilometer traveled. To use the bus, you'll need a Hafilat Smart Card, which can be easily purchased and recharged at various locations throughout the city.

## Bus Booking

Currently, Bus 63 does not offer online booking options. Passengers can board the bus directly at any of the designated stops along the route. For real-time updates on bus locations and schedules, passengers can use the Abu Dhabi Public Transport app or check the official website. This app provides information on bus timings, route maps, and live bus tracking to enhance the travel experience.

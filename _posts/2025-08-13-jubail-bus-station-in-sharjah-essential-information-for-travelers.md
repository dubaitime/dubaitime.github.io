## Bus Routes and Timings

Al Jubail Bus Station provides a number of important routes, ensuring that passengers can travel efficiently to their destinations. The station services the following key bus routes:

## Bus Route Details

- Bus Route 117: Connects Al Jubail Bus Terminal in Sharjah to Abu Dhabi, providing frequent services throughout the day.

- Bus Route E306: Runs between Ghubaiba Bus Station in Dubai and Al Jubail Bus Station in Sharjah, offering reliable transportation at regular intervals.

These routes run frequently throughout the day, providing flexibility for travelers heading to or from Sharjah, whether for work or leisure.

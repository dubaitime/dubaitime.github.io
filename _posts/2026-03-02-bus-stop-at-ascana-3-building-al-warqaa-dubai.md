## Bus Route Details

The Al Warqa’a, ASCANA 3 Building Bus Stop is served by two key bus routes:

- F10 Route: This route connects the bus stop to Dubai Safari Park and Al Warqa'a J. Buses operate approximately every 30 minutes, with first departures as early as 05:56 AM and continuing until the last service at 01:01 AM. This route provides frequent and reliable options for exploring local attractions and commuting within the Al Warqa area.

- F61 Route: Linking Al Warqa’a with Rashidiya Bus Station Parking, this route starts running at 04:32 AM and offers consistent service throughout the day with buses departing at regular intervals. The last bus departs at 00:50 AM.

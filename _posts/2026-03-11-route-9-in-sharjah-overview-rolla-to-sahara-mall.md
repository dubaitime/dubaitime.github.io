Route 9 is one of the most important bus routes in Sharjah, linking the bustling Rolla area with the popular Sahara Mall. Designed to serve both daily commuters and shoppers, this route offers a dependable and affordable way to travel between these key destinations. With its regular services and strategically placed stops, Route 9 is an essential part of Sharjah’s public transport network, ensuring a smooth and convenient travel experience.

## Route Overview

The journey on Route 9 begins at Rolla Terminal and concludes at Sahara Mall Terminal, stopping at several major landmarks along the way. Key stops include:
1. Al Jubail Terminal

2. Central Souq

3. King Faisal Street

4. Al Wahda Street

5. Al Khan Street

6. Al Tawaan Street

7. Sharjah Expo Center

8. Al Nahda Street

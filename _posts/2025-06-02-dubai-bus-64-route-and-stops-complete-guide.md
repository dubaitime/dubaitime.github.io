## Bus 64 Station Overview

### Gold Souq Bus Station · 7

Gold Souq Bus Station · 7 is a key transit hub located in the heart of Dubai’s bustling Gold Souq area.

It is well-equipped with essential amenities, including sheltered waiting areas and real-time schedule updates, ensuring a comfortable travel experience. The station’s central location provides easy access to local shops, commercial areas, and other attractions in the vicinity, making it an important part of the local transportation network.

### Hor Al Anz, Turnoff 1 Bus Stop Intersection

The Hor Al Anz, Turnoff 1 Bus Stop is a major transit hub where Bus Routes 5, 23, 64, 13B, 13D, and C18 intersect. This central location provides passengers with efficient connections between these diverse routes, facilitating seamless travel across various parts of Dubai. The stop's strategic placement ensures easy access to multiple destinations, making it a crucial point for commuters navigating the Hor Al Anz area.

### Intersecting Bus Routes at Abu Hail Clinic 1

This strategic location offers seamless connections between these routes, providing comprehensive travel options for passengers across various parts of Dubai. ### Abu Hail Center 2 Bus Stop Intersection

The Abu Hail Center 2 Bus Stop serves as a crucial intersection for Bus Routes 64, 13B, C09, and C10. This stop connects passengers to various destinations across Dubai, making it a vital hub for commuters in the Abu Hail area. Its proximity to the Abu Hail Center ensures convenient access to shopping, dining, and other services, catering to the needs of both locals and visitors.

### Al Qusais Metro Bus Stop Intersection

The Al Qusais Metro Bus Stop is a crucial transit hub where Bus Routes 13, 31, and 64 intersect. This central location facilitates seamless travel across various parts of the city, offering passengers easy connections to multiple destinations. Its strategic position and diverse route options enhance convenience for daily commuters and travelers navigating the Al Qusais area.

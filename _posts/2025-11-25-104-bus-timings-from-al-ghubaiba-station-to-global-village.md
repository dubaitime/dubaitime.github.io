Dubai’s public transport system is one of the most efficient and far reaching, joining their main districts smoothly. One of such important routes is the RTA Bus 104; connecting Al Ghubaiba Station to Global Village. The route currently serves as an important connection for commuters to both a world famous tourist attraction and popular residential areas. Bus 104 run time and main stops are highlighted in this article to enable you plan your journey with ease.

## Journey Experience

The RTA Bus 104 from Al Ghubaiba Station to Global Village provides a long, but highly interesting, ride across Dubai’s bustling city.

They also get a glimpse of Jumeirah Beach Road, home to luxury resorts and close to the popular sports and leisure water area: Kite Beach. As a ride to the destination, Global Village, it is an entertaining and visiting hub of international pavilions, shopping carts and live performances.

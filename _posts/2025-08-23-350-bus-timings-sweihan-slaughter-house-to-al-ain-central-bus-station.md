Abu Dhabi’s public transportation network offers efficient travel solutions across the city, and Reg Bus 350 is a great example. This route connects Sweihan Slaughter House with Al Ain Central Bus Station, providing an important link between rural areas and the city.

## Operational Details

Reg Bus 350 operates consistently throughout the day, offering multiple services in both directions. Here are the key timings:

| From Sweihan Slaughter House | From Al Ain Central |
|---|---|
| 08:00 | 06:00 |
| 12:00 | 10:00 |
| 16:00 | 14:00 |
| 20:00 | 18:00 |
| 00:00 | 22:00 |

Bus 350 Timetable

For the full schedule, you can visit the official AD Mobility website or follow the link to access the 350 Bus Timings.

Ticket Pricing: The fare for the bus is AED 2.00 plus AED 0.05 per kilometer, with passengers able to pay using the Hafilat Smart Card. This card can be conveniently topped up online or at kiosks, providing a seamless and cashless payment option for frequent travelers.

## Route Overview

The 350 Bus begins its journey at Sweihan Slaughter House and ends at Al Ain Central Bus Station, passing through a range of residential, commercial, and community stops. Below are some key stops along the route:
1. Sweihan Slaughter House  The starting point of the journey.

2. Sweihan Wedding Hall  A local venue for cultural and community events.

3. Sweihan Police Station  Serving the Sweihan community's security needs.

4. Emirates Post  A key postal service stop in the area.

5. Sweihan Boys School  A prominent educational institution in the town.

6. Sweihan Souq  A bustling marketplace offering local goods.

7. National Avian Research Center  A notable stop for those interested in environmental and wildlife research.

8. Ajban Road  A major road connecting rural areas to the city.

9. Al Yahar Mall  A popular shopping destination for locals.

10. Tawam Hospital  A vital healthcare center serving the community.

11. UAE University  One of the leading educational institutions in the UAE.

12. Al Jahili Park  A serene public space for recreation and relaxation.

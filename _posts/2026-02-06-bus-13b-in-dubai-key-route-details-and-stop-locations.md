## Bus 13B Station Overview

### Gold Souq Bus Station · 8

Gold Souq Bus Station · 8 is a prominent transit hub located near Dubai's famous Gold Souq, a bustling marketplace known for its extensive selection of gold jewelry and traditional goods. This station is well-equipped with amenities such as sheltered waiting areas, ticketing services, and real-time schedule updates.

Its central location makes it a convenient stop for shoppers, tourists, and locals visiting the Gold Souq. The station's facilities are designed to accommodate a high volume of passengers, ensuring a smooth and efficient travel experience for those exploring the vibrant market area.

### Hor Al Anz, Turnoff 1 Bus Stop Intersection

The Hor Al Anz, Turnoff 1 Bus Stop is a major transit hub where Bus Routes 5, 23, 64, 13B, 13D, and C18 intersect. This central location provides passengers with efficient connections between these diverse routes, facilitating seamless travel across various parts of Dubai. The stop's strategic placement ensures easy access to multiple destinations, making it a crucial point for commuters navigating the Hor Al Anz area.

### Airport Terminal 2, Departure Bus Stop Intersection

Situated conveniently at the departure area of Dubai International Airport, this station is designed for seamless connectivity, providing quick and direct access to the terminal.

With modern amenities like digital screens displaying real-time flight and bus schedule information, it ensures a hassle-free travel experience for passengers. Whether heading to or from the airport, this well-maintained stop offers efficient transit options across the city.

### Intersection of Bus Routes at Dafza

This key location facilitates convenient transfers between these routes, offering passengers efficient travel options within the Dafza area and beyond. The stop is equipped with essential amenities and real-time schedule updates, ensuring a smooth and accessible transit experience for all travelers.

### Intersecting Bus Routes at Abu Hail Clinic 1

This strategic location offers seamless connections between these routes, providing comprehensive travel options for passengers across various parts of Dubai. The intersection’s connectivity facilitates easy transfers and enhances accessibility for commuters traveling within the city, making it a central hub for efficient public transportation.

### bu Hail Center 2 Bus Stop Intersection

The Abu Hail Center 2 Bus Stop serves as a crucial intersection for Bus Routes 64, 13B, C09, and C10. This stop connects passengers to various destinations across Dubai, making it a vital hub for commuters in the Abu Hail area. Its proximity to the Abu Hail Center ensures convenient access to shopping, dining, and other services, catering to the needs of both locals and visitors.

### Intersecting at Damascus Road 3 Bus Stop

The Damascus Road 3 Bus Stop serves as a major intersection for multiple bus routes, including 17, 43, 13B, C26, F8, F81, and X28. This key transit point provides seamless connections between these diverse routes, offering passengers efficient travel options across different areas. The station’s central location and well-maintained facilities enhance the convenience and accessibility of public transportation in the region.

### Qusais Bus Station · 4

Qusais Bus Station · 4 serves as a key transportation point in the Al Qusais area, providing essential transit services for residents and commuters. The station features amenities such as comfortable seating, ticketing services, and current schedule information. Routes 13A, 13D, 32C, 33, and 34 all meet at this station, offering a wide range of travel options.

Its location within a busy commercial and residential district ensures easy access to local shops, services, and surrounding neighborhoods.

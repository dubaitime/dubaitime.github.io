Traveling between Hatta Bus Station and Hatta Dam has become more convenient with the H04 bus route operated by the Roads and Transport Authority (RTA). This guide will provide you with all the essential information about the H04 bus timings, stops, and fares. Whether you're a tourist exploring Hatta’s attractions or a resident on a day out, the H04 bus offers an affordable and easy way to reach some of the area's best-known landmarks, including Hatta Dam and Hatta Wadi Hub.

## H04 Bus Timings and Frequency

The H04 bus operates daily from 7 AM to 9 PM, making it simple to explore Hatta’s key attractions at your own pace. Below is a quick overview of the bus timings:

The bus operates at a 30-minute frequency throughout the day, allowing you to hop on and off easily at any of the tourist landmarks.

## Travel Duration

The total journey from Hatta Bus Station to Hatta Dam takes around 25 minutes, making it a quick and efficient way to reach one of Hatta’s most scenic spots. Along the way, the bus passes by the Hatta Heritage Village, Hatta Hill Park, and other local attractions, giving passengers a glimpse of the area’s rich culture and natural beauty.

## H04 Bus Route Overview

The H04 bus route is designed to provide easy access to Hatta’s most popular tourist destinations. It’s a circular route that starts and ends at Hatta Bus Station, covering 12 kilometers in total. With stops at places like Hatta Wadi Hub, where you can enjoy outdoor activities, and Hatta Dam, famous for its stunning views and kayaking, this bus route offers a cost-effective way to explore the region. The fare is incredibly affordable at just AED 2 per stop.

The main stops on the H04 route include:
1. Hatta Bus Station

2. Hatta Heritage Village

3. Hatta Wadi Hub

4. Hatta Hill Park

5. Hatta Dam

## H04 Bus Fare and NOL Card Usage

One of the biggest advantages of using the H04 bus is its affordability. The fare for each stop is just AED 2, payable using the RTA’s NOL card. If you're planning to visit multiple locations along the route, this low-cost option makes it easier to see more of Hatta without breaking the bank. Just remember to top up your NOL card before your trip, as cash payments are not accepted on the bus.

## Onboard Features

The H04 bus is equipped with several features to ensure a comfortable journey for passengers:

- Priority Seating: Reserved seating is available for women, children, and passengers with special needs.

These features make your trip comfortable, whether you’re traveling for a short distance or enjoying the scenic ride to Hatta Dam.

## Additional Tips for a Smooth Journey

- Download the RTA App: Use the app to track bus timings and receive updates on any changes to the schedule.

- Plan for a Leisurely Day: With buses running every 30 minutes, you can explore each landmark at your own pace without worrying about missing the next bus.
- Pack Light: If you’re planning to hike or explore the attractions, pack light and dress comfortably to make the most of your day.

As the bus winds its way through the charming landscapes, passengers can enjoy views of the Hajar Mountains and learn about Hatta's rich history. Known for its outdoor activities like kayaking at Hatta Dam and hiking trails, this affordable AED 2 ride is a perfect introduction to one of the UAE's most serene destinations.

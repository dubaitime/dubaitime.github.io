Located in Sharjah, Muwaileh Bus Station is a key transit hub served by Routes 3, 14X, 77, and E315, connecting passengers to destinations across the city.

## Bus Routes and Timings

Muwaileh Bus Terminal is served by several important routes that provide connectivity within Sharjah and beyond. Key bus routes include services to Al Rolla, Etisalat Bus Station, and Al Saja’a Terminal. Below are the major routes and their expected arrival times:

| Bus Route | Direction | Expected Arrival Time |
|---|---|---|
| 3 | Al Rolla Terminal | Every 30 minutes |
| 14X | Al Rolla Terminal | Every 20 minutes |
| 77 | Al Saja’a Terminal | Every 25 minutes |
| E315 | Etisalat Bus Station | Every 30 minutes |

These routes provide comprehensive coverage and make Muwaileh Bus Terminal a key transit point for passengers traveling to various parts of Sharjah and neighboring emirates.

## Bus Route Details

Muwaileh Bus Station is served by the following routes:

- Route 3: Connects to Al Rolla Terminal with buses every 30 minutes.

- Route 14X: Travels to Al Rolla Terminal every 20 minutes and returns to Muwaileh (alighting only).
- Route 77: Serves Al Saja’a Terminal every 25 minutes and returns to Muwaileh (alighting only).
- Route E315: Travels to Etisalat Bus Station with frequent service and returns to Muwaileh (alighting only).

## Bus Route Details

The E16 route operates between Al Sabkha Bus Station and Hatta Bus Station. Here’s an overview of the service:

- E16 Route: This route connects Al Sabkha Bus Station to Hatta Bus Station, covering key locations in Dubai. Buses run from early morning until late at night from the Awir, New Shabia 1 stop. This service provides essential connectivity for passengers traveling between major transit hubs and important destinations.

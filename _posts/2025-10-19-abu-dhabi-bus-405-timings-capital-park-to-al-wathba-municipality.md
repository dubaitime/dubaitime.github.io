## Journey Experience

A trip on Route 405 from Capital Park to Al Wathba Municipality offers a scenic journey through Abu Dhabi, blending urban vibrancy with suburban tranquility. Starting at peaceful Capital Park, passengers admire the Gold Center, a jewelry haven, before stopping at the beautifully designed Mariam Bint Said Mosque. The bus then arrives at the bustling Abu Dhabi Bus Station and continues along Mohamed Bin Khalifa Street, lined with shops and eateries.

The Souq provides a glimpse of local culture with traditional vendors. Finally, the route concludes at Al Wathba Municipality, near the Al Wathba Wetland Reserve, showcasing the unique blend of culture and commerce that defines Abu Dhabi.

## Travel Tips for Bus 405
1. Plan Ahead: Check the bus schedule to avoid long waits, especially during peak hours.

2. Use the Hafilat Card: Ensure your card is topped up for smooth, cashless travel.

3. Real-Time Tracking: Download the AD Mobility app to track Bus 405 in real-time.

4. Off-Peak Travel: Travel during non-peak times for a quieter and quicker journey.

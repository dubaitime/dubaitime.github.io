## Bus Ticket Price

The cost of traveling on Bus 31 is in line with standard Dubai public transport fares, which is AED 3. Passengers can use Nol cards for payment, with prices typically reflecting the distance traveled. Fares are generally affordable, making it an economical option for daily commuting and occasional travel. Additionally, Nol cards offer convenience and can be easily recharged online or at various stations.

## Bus Booking

There is no specific online booking system for individual seats on Bus 31. Nol cards, used for payment, can be recharged online via the RTA website or mobile app, adding convenience for frequent travelers. Day passes and multi-day passes are also available for those who require unlimited travel within a specified period.

## Bus 31 Station Overview

### Oud Al Mateena Roundabout Terminus

The Oud Al Mateena Roundabout Terminus serves as a key endpoint for Bus 31. Located in a quieter residential area, this terminus is essential for residents commuting to various parts of Dubai. Its strategic location near the roundabout allows for easy access to surrounding neighborhoods, ensuring efficient connectivity for daily commuters.

### Al Qusais Metro Bus Stop Intersection

The Al Qusais Metro Bus Stop is a crucial transit hub where Bus Routes 13, 31, and 64 intersect. This central location facilitates seamless travel across various parts of the city, offering passengers easy connections to multiple destinations. Its strategic position and diverse route options enhance convenience for daily commuters and travelers navigating the Al Qusais area.

### Airport Terminal 2, Departure

Situated conveniently at the departure area of Dubai International Airport, this station is designed for seamless connectivity, providing quick and direct access to the terminal.

With modern amenities like digital screens displaying real-time flight and bus schedule information, it ensures a hassle-free travel experience for passengers. Whether heading to or from the airport, this well-maintained stop offers efficient transit options across the city.

## Tourist Destination Along the Way

The Bus 31 route passes by several notable tourist attractions and destinations in Dubai:
1. Etihad Mall: Located on the route, this shopping center offers a variety of retail options, dining experiences, and entertainment facilities.

2. Dubai Modern School: While primarily an educational institution, the surrounding area often has community events that can be of interest.

3. Al Qusais Metro Station: Close to cultural and shopping spots, this area is well-connected for further exploration of Dubai.

4. Fortune Plaza Hotel: A notable hotel in the area, offering convenient accommodation for travelers.

5. Dubai Airport Free Zone Metro: Provides easy access to the Dubai Airport Free Zone, an important business district.

## Journey Experience

Taking bus route 31 from Oud Al Mateena Roundabout Terminus to Airport Terminal 2 offers a long yet intriguing journey through various districts of Dubai. Starting from Oud Al Mateena, a quieter residential area, the bus travels through the city's vibrant neighborhoods, giving passengers glimpses of local life and culture.

## Bus 66 Station Overview

### Ghubiba Bus Station · 48

Ghubiba Bus Station · 48 is a prominent transportation hub located in the Ghubiba area of Dubai. This station is well-equipped to handle a high volume of passengers, with amenities such as sheltered waiting areas, ticketing services, and real-time schedule updates.

Its central location provides convenient access to the surrounding neighborhoods, making it a vital part of the local transportation network. The station’s facilities and strategic position near key commercial and residential areas ensure an efficient transit experience for commuters.

### Intersecting Bus Routes at Burjuman Metro Bus Stop

The Burjuman Metro Bus Stop serves as a major transit hub where several bus routes intersect, enhancing connectivity for passengers throughout Dubai. Key routes that converge at this station include 66, 67, 61D, C03, C18, F70, X23, and X25.

This central location provides seamless travel options across different city areas, making it a vital stop for commuters and ensuring efficient access to various destinations.

### Faqa, Terminus / Al Faqa’a Family Park

Faqa, Terminus, also known as Al Faqa'a Family Park, is a significant bus station situated in the Faqa area. This terminus serves as a key transit point for passengers traveling within and beyond the region. The station offers essential amenities, including comfortable waiting areas and ticketing services, to enhance the travel experience.

Its proximity to the Al Faqa'a Family Park makes it a convenient stop for visitors to the park and local residents. The terminus plays an important role in providing efficient transportation options for the Faqa area and surrounding regions.

## Journey Experience

Taking Bus Route 66 from Ghubaiba Bus Station to Faqa, Terminus / Al Faqa'a Family Park offers a delightful journey through Dubai's diverse landscape. Starting at the bustling Ghubaiba, a major transport hub, the bus navigates through vibrant neighborhoods like Al Karama and Jumeirah, showcasing a blend of modern skyscrapers and traditional architecture, along with lively markets and quaint cafes that reflect the city’s rich culture.

As you travel further towards the outskirts, the urban scenery transitions to the tranquil beauty of Al Faqa'a, known for its family-friendly parks. Al Faqa'a Family Park is a highlight, featuring ample space for picnics, playgrounds, and recreational activities, making it an ideal spot for families to unwind and enjoy nature. This trip not only connects you to a peaceful retreat but also showcases the unique character of Dubai, blending urban excitement with serene landscapes.

Dubai Bus N55 is an essential part of Dubai's public transportation system, operating overnight to connect key locations across the city. This bus route provides a vital link between Ghubaiba Bus Station and Al Maktoum International Airport, catering to the needs of late-night travelers, shift workers, and early morning flyers. Its extensive route covers many popular destinations, hotels, and residential areas, ensuring convenient travel options during the late hours.

## Bus Ticket Price

As of February 2024, the standard fare for the Bus N55 is AED 3. This fare structure is consistent with other public buses in Dubai, making it an economical choice for late-night travel. Passengers will need a Nol card to pay for their journey, which can be purchased and recharged at various locations across the city.

## Bus Booking

Currently, Bus N55 does not offer an online booking system. Passengers can board the bus directly at any of the designated stops along the route. For real-time updates and schedules, passengers can use the RTA Dubai app or visit the RTA website. The app provides live bus arrival times, route maps, and other useful information to help plan your journey effectively.

Other RTA Dubai bus routes to consider include the F11, which passes through the Financial Center Metro Seaside station, and the X25, which connects Karama Bus Station with Dubai Silicon Oasis.

## Journey Experience

This late-night ride gives passengers a unique, quieter perspective of the city, passing by major landmarks such as Expo City Dubai, a symbol of the city’s global ambitions after hosting Expo 2020. Despite the extended travel time, the journey offers a serene experience, blending Dubai’s vibrant skyline with its expanding futuristic developments.

## Traveling with Careem

With the Careem app, you can travel to Al Maktoum International Airport, Arrivals, the furthest point on the N55 (overnight) bus route, starting from Ghubaiba Bus Station · 6. The price for this journey ranges from AED 184 to AED 224, offering a comfortable and convenient way to reach the airport while enjoying the extensive coverage of Dubai’s key areas provided by the N55 route.

Whether you’re traveling from central Dubai to the airport or exploring the city’s landmarks during the night, Bus N55 offers a reliable and affordable transportation option. Its extensive coverage ensures that you can reach major destinations and connect seamlessly with other parts of the city even during late hours. With frequent service and strategic stops, the N55 bus makes nighttime travel both convenient and accessible for all.

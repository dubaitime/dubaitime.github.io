Route 88 is a crucial bus service in Sharjah, connecting the busy Rolla Terminal with Al Saja’a, an important industrial area. Whether you're a daily commuter traveling for work or heading to Al Saja’a for other business, Route 88 offers a convenient and budget-friendly option. This route is designed to serve both residents and visitors, providing easy access to key locations along its path. With regular services and well-placed stops, Route 88 is an essential part of Sharjah's public transportation network.

## Route Overview

The journey on Route 88 begins at the Rolla Terminal and finishes at Al Saja’a. Along the way, passengers can expect several important stops, making travel between residential and industrial areas seamless. Key stops on this route include:
1. Al Arouba Street

2. Abu Tina

3. Al Qasimi Street

4. Dhaid Road

5. Sharjah Airport Terminal

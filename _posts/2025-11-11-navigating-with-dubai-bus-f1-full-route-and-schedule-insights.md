Ticket Pricing and Payment: Bus F1 accepts Nol cards, Dubai’s unified contactless payment system for public transportation, making it easy to pay for your journey. The fare for Bus F01 varies based on the number of zones traveled and the type of Nol card you use:

- Within 1 Zone: AED 3 (Silver/Personal Card), AED 6 (Gold Card), AED 4 (Red Ticket)

- 2 Adjacent Zones: AED 5 (Silver/Personal Card), AED 10 (Gold Card), AED 6 (Red Ticket)
- More than 2 Zones: AED 7.5 (Silver/Personal Card), AED 15 (Gold Card), AED 8.5 (Red Ticket)

To pay, tap your Nol card on the reader when boarding and exiting, and ensure it has at least AED 7.5, which can be topped up at any RTA service center or vending machine. For more details on Nol card options and fares, visit the Dubai Roads and Transport Authority (RTA) website.

## Bus Ticket Price

The fare for Bus 11 adheres to Dubai’s standard public transport pricing. A single journey generally costs AED 3 with a Nol Card. Prices may vary based on the card type and the number of travel zones crossed. For the most precise fare information, passengers should check the RTA Dubai app or inquire at metro stations. Be sure to have your Nol Card ready for a smooth trip.

## Bus 11 Station Overview

### Rashidiya Bus Station Parking

Rashidiya Bus Station Parking is a central transit hub serving Bus Route 11, strategically located in the Rashidiya area. This station provides extensive parking facilities, making it convenient for commuters to switch between their vehicles and public transport. The station is well-equipped with amenities such as ticket counters, waiting areas, and real-time bus schedule displays. Its proximity to major residential and commercial zones enhances accessibility for both daily commuters and visitors.

### Al Awir Immigration Office

The Al Awir Immigration Office station is a key stop for Bus Route 11, situated near the Al Awir Immigration Office. This station caters to individuals visiting the immigration office for various services and documentation. Its location provides easy access for those managing their immigration needs, offering a convenient link to the wider public transport network.

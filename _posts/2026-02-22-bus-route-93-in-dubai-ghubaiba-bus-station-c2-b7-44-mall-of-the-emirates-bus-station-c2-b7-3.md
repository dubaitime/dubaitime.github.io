## Journey Experience

Starting in the historic Bur Dubai district near Dubai Creek and the Al Fahidi Historical Neighborhood, the route offers a glimpse into the city’s rich cultural heritage.

As the bus moves through the city, it travels along bustling streets and past modern skyscrapers, including those on Sheikh Zayed Road, a symbol of Dubai’s rapid urban development. The journey ends at Mall of the Emirates, one of Dubai’s premier shopping destinations, famous for its indoor ski resort, Ski Dubai. Despite the lengthy ride, the route provides a unique view of Dubai’s mix of tradition and modernity, making it a compelling trip for both residents and visitors.

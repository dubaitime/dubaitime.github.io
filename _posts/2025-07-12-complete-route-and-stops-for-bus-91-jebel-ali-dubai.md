## Bus Ticket Price

The fare for Bus 91 is in line with the standard pricing for Dubai's public transport system. The fare is calculated based on the number of zones traveled, with the typical fare for a journey on Bus 91 ranging from AED 7-12, depending on the distance. Passengers can use their Nol card to pay for their journey, making it a convenient and cashless payment option. Discounted fares are available for students, senior citizens, and people of determination (people with disabilities).

## Bus Booking

While there is no need for advanced booking to use Bus 91, passengers can check real-time updates, plan routes, and manage their journeys using the RTA Dubai app. The app provides a user-friendly interface that allows passengers to track bus schedules, plan routes, and even estimate travel times. For those who prefer a more personalized experience, the Careem app offers an alternative option for direct rides to or from the bus stops, especially during peak hours or when carrying heavy luggage.

## Bus 91 Station Overview

### Ghubaiba Bus Station · 19

Ghubaiba Bus Station is one of Dubai's most prominent transit hubs, located in the historic Bur Dubai area. As a key stop for Bus 91, this station connects passengers to various parts of the city and beyond.

### Business Bay Metro Station Seaside Bus Stop Intersection

Business Bay Metro Station Seaside Bus Stop is a crucial intersection where Bus Routes 7, 9, 14, 91, and F20 converge. This stop serves as a vital connection point for passengers traveling within the Business Bay area and to various destinations across Dubai. Its strategic location near major business and residential areas makes it a convenient choice for daily commuters and travelers seeking easy access to key parts of the city.

### Jebel Ali Bus Station · 4

Jebel Ali Bus Station is situated in the industrial and residential area of Jebel Ali, serving as an important stop on Bus 91's route. This station provides easy access to the Jebel Ali Free Zone, Port, and surrounding residential communities. It features essential amenities to accommodate the needs of daily commuters and travelers working or living in the area. The station's strategic location makes it a vital transit point for those traveling to and from Dubai's southern regions.

## Tourist Destination Along the Way

Bus 91 traverses a mix of business and residential areas, with several key locations that might be of interest to tourists:
1. Business Bay Metro Seaside: While not a tourist attraction itself, the Business Bay area is known for its modern architecture and vibrant business environment, which might appeal to visitors interested in Dubai's urban development.

2. Dubai Municipality Center: Located in the heart of Dubai, this area offers a glimpse into the city's administrative and architectural landscape. It’s close to other central attractions.

3. Four Points Sheraton Hotel: Situated near the bus route, this hotel offers accommodation with a view of Dubai’s skyline and is conveniently located for exploring nearby attractions.

4. Jebel Ali Free Zone: Although primarily a business hub, the free zone area showcases Dubai's industrial and economic growth, which might be of interest to those exploring Dubai's economic sectors.

Embarking on Bus Route 91 from Ghubaiba Bus Station to Jebel Ali Bus Station takes you on an extensive journey through some of Dubai’s most intriguing neighborhoods and attractions.

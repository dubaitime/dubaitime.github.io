## Bus 56 Station Overview

### Danube Metro Landside

Danube Metro Landside bus station is located adjacent to the Danube Metro station, serving as a critical transportation link for passengers in the area. This station is equipped with essential amenities, including sheltered waiting areas, ticketing services, and real-time schedule updates.

Its strategic location near the metro station facilitates seamless transfers between bus and metro services, making it a convenient stop for daily commuters and travelers. The station's proximity to key commercial and residential areas ensures efficient connectivity and enhances the overall travel experience for passengers using this transit point.

### Intersection of Bus Routes at Muhaisnah 4, Jamal Building

The bus stop at Muhaisnah 4, Jamal Building is a significant intersection for Bus Routes X28, 34, and 56. This key location connects these routes, providing efficient transit options and easy access to various destinations within the Muhaisnah area and beyond. The stop is equipped with essential amenities and real-time schedule updates to enhance the travel experience for passengers using these intersecting bus services.

### Dubai World Central, Staff Village

This station features amenities such as sheltered waiting areas and up-to-date bus schedule information.

Its location within the staff village ensures convenient access for employees commuting to and from their workplaces within the DWC zone.

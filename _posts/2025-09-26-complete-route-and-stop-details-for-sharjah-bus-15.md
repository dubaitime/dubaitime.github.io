## Bus Timings

Bus 15 operates with the following schedule:

- From Sharjah Airport Terminal: 06:00 to 23:05, every 20 minutes

- From Al Rolla Terminal: 05:45 to 23:10, every 20 minutes

These timings ensure that passengers have frequent and reliable service throughout the day, accommodating both early and late travel needs.

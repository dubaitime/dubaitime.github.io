1. ADNEC: Starting point near the exhibition center.

2. Zayed Military Hospital: Access to specialized medical services.

3. Wakr Street: A bustling commercial area.

4. Ministry of Foreign Trade: Important government office.

5. Rabdan Street: Connecting residential and business hubs.

## Journey Experience

The bus goes through landmarks such as Mushrif Mall, Central Souq, Hamdan Street and other landmarks and passengers can enjoy bustling markets, the vast buildings, lively streets as the bus passes by.

Notable stop near Al Mariah Mall is also an excuse for detour into luxury shopping and dining, or admire the combination of traditional and contemporary Islamic design of the Hazaa Bin Zayed mosque. A route along that will provide easy access to Corniche Beach for relaxation. The dynamics of moving to another city are at full swing here; from a commute to a cultural experience  Abu Dhabi manages to effortlessly fuse tradition and modernity.

Travel Tips for Bus L41

- Plan Your Trip: Real time updates are available through the AD Mobility app.
- Off-Peak Travel: Plan your ride during off rush hours for a quieter ride.
- Cashless Payments: Make sure you have enough top up on your Hafilat Card.

ADNEC provides an important link of Abu Dhabi Bus Routes with bus L41 between ADNEC and Corniche Hospital for the residents and visitors of Abu Dhabi. Bus L41 is a reliable and comfortable journey, whether you are commuting to work or leisure.

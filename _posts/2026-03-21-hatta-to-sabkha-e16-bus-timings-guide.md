## Bus Route E16 (Best Option)

Bus E16 is your go-to for traveling from Hatta to Al Sabkha. It offers a direct, cost-effective route with multiple stops along the way.

- Estimated Travel Time: 2 hours and 54 minutes

- Cost: AED 15-25
- Bus Frequency: Every 3 hours

Journey Overview

Step 1: Start by taking a taxi from Hatta to Emirates NBD 2 (17 minutes, 7.1 km, AED 25-30).

Step 2: From Emirates NBD 2, board Bus E16. The bus journey to Al Sabkha takes approximately 2 hours and 37 minutes.

## Travel Tips

- Best Time to Travel: Plan your trip during off-peak hours to avoid delays, as traffic can affect travel times, particularly when passing through busier parts of Dubai.

- Nol Card: Make sure your Nol card is topped up before boarding. Nol cards can be purchased and refilled at any metro station, convenience store, or online.
- Checking the Bus Timings: It’s recommended to check the Dubai RTA app for real-time bus updates, or you can check the Bus E16 Timings here.
- Comfort: Dubai RTA buses are air-conditioned and equipped with comfortable seating, making the longer journey more pleasant, especially during the hotter months.

The E16 bus is a reliable and cost-effective way to travel from Hatta to Al Sabkha. With scenic views along the route and key stops connecting you to various parts of Dubai, it’s a great option for budget travelers. Make sure to check the bus schedule in advance and have your Nol card ready for a seamless trip. Whether you're heading into the city for shopping, business, or leisure, Bus E16 has you covered.

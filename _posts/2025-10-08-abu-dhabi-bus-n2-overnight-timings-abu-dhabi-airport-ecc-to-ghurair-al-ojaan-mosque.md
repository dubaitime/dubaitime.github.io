Public transportation in Abu Dhabi plays a major role in commuting, forming a reliable and affordable network for traveling in the city even during ungodly hours. Bus N2 is one of the key routes providing overnight service from Abu Dhabi Airport ECC to Ghurair Al Ojaan Mosque. It’s also a useful route if you’re traveling late at night or early in the morning. In this guide, we’ll look at the timetable, main stops, price of tickets, and travel tips for taking Bus N2.

Ticket Pricing: Bus N2 passengers can now use the Hafilat Card, Abu Dhabi’s easy to use cashless payment system. Within a single zone, fares are AED 2 plus AED 0.05 per kilometre travelled. Hafilat card top up is easy with kiosks available all over the city or do it online.

The N2 bus route links important stops in Abu Dhabi from a few key landmarks to essential services. Here’s an overview of the stops along the N2 route:

2. Abu Dhabi Airport Terminal A Arrival: Access to those arriving at the airport.

3. Al Jazeera Sports Club: A popular stop for sports enthusiasts.

4. Mohamed Bin Khalifa Street: A major street connecting various city districts.

5. Abu Dhabi Bus Station: The central hub for intercity bus connections.

6. Mariam Bint Said Mosque: A key religious landmark in the area.

## Nearby Attractions and Landmarks

Passengers on Bus N2 can conveniently access several popular attractions in Abu Dhabi:

1. Gold Center: A shopping haven for gold lovers and those interested in fine jewelry.

2. Zayed The First Street: A major road lined with shops, restaurants, and cultural spots.

3. Emirates Tower: A significant business area with plenty of restaurants and shops.

## Journey Experience

Taking the Bus N2 (overnight) from Abu Dhabi Airport ECC to Ghurair Al Ojaan Mosque offers a unique and convenient way to experience the city at night.

The journey also takes you by Hazaa Bin Zayed Mosque, an important cultural site.

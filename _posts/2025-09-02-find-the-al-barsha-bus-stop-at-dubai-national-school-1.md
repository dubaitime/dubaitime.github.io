## Bus Route Details

The Al Barsha, Dubai National School 1 Bus Stop is serviced by two primary routes:

- F35 Route: This route connects Al Barsha to The Greens. Buses begin service at 9:06 AM and run with regular intervals until 2:42 PM. It provides consistent and reliable service throughout the day.

- 84 Route: Linking Al Barsha with JBR Marina, the 84 route operates from 9:15 AM to 2:12 PM. Buses on this route run approximately every 10 to 15 minutes, making it ideal for accessing the popular JBR Marina area.

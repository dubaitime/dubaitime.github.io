The Abu Dhabi public transportation system offers a convenient and affordable way to travel, and Bus 390 is a perfect example of this. Connecting Al Ain Bus Station to Al Shwaib (Souq), this route serves both residents and visitors, providing access to essential services and attractions along the way.

## Operational Details

Bus 390 operates four times a day, ensuring consistent service for travelers. Below are the key timings for Bus 390 departing from both Al Ain Bus Station and Al Shwaib (Souq):

Ticket Pricing: Payments for Bus 390 can be made using the Hafilat Card, Abu Dhabi's cashless payment system. The fare is AED 2 for travel within one zone, with additional charges of AED 0.05 per kilometer. Passengers can easily top up their Hafilat cards online or at kiosks located throughout the city for a seamless travel experience.

## Travel Tips for Bus 390

- Plan Ahead: Check the bus timings to avoid unnecessary waiting, especially during peak hours.

- Use the Hafilat Smart Card: This allows for quick, cashless payment on all Abu Dhabi buses.
- Monitor Bus Updates: Use the AD Mobility app for real-time updates and schedules to optimize your journey.
- Explore the Souq: Make sure to allocate time to visit Al Shwaib Souq for a taste of local culture and shopping.

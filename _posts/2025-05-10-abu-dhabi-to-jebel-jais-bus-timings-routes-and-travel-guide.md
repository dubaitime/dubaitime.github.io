## 1. Bus Route via Abu Dhabi Central Station (Best Overall)

A well-balanced option between cost and travel time, this route combines buses and metro services for a seamless journey.

- Estimated Travel Time: 6 hours 14 minutes

- Cost: AED 783  AED 1,075

Journey Overview:

Step 1: Start at Abu Dhabi, Central Bus Station  2 hours 5 minutes, AED 110  AED 160, Bus: E100 (Every 30 minutes), Stop: Max Metro Bus Stop, Al Karama, Dubai.

Step 2: Transfer to Max Metro Station, Al Karama  19 minutes, AED 21  AED 29, Metro Line: MRed (Every 10 minutes), Stop: Airport Terminal 3 Metro Station, Dubai.

Step 3: Bus from Terminal 3  1 hour 3 minutes, AED 150  AED 240, Bus: T1/T3 (Every 4 hours), Stop: Hilton Garden Inn, Ras Al Khaimah.

Step 4: Taxi from Hilton Garden Inn to Jebel Jais  1 hour 45 minutes, AED 500  AED 650, Stop: Jebel Jais, Ras Al Khaimah.

## Travel Tips for Jebel Jais

- Best Time to Visit: The cooler months (October to April) offer the best weather for outdoor activities at Jebel Jais.

- Activities: Don’t miss the world’s longest zip line, mountain climbing, or simply enjoying the stunning views.
- Packing Tips: Be sure to bring comfortable clothing, sunscreen, and plenty of water for your hike or adventure.

The winding roads up the mountain offer breathtaking views, making the trip as memorable as the destination itself. So, sit back, relax, and enjoy the adventure that awaits!

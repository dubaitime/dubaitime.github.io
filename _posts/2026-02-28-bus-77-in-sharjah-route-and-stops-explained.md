## Bus Ticket Pric

The fare for Bus 77 is aligned with Sharjah's standard public transportation rates. Passengers can use their Sayer card to pay for their trips, making it easy and convenient. The cost per trip is AED 8 if paid in cash, or AED 6 with a Sayer Card, offering a more economical option for frequent travelers.

## Bus 77 Station Overview

### Al Saja’a Terminal

Al Saja'a Terminal is a significant bus station located in the industrial area of Sharjah. This terminal serves as a major transit point for workers and commuters traveling to and from the bustling industrial zones. Its strategic location makes it a crucial hub for those needing to access the industrial sector, offering connections to various bus routes that serve the broader region.

### Sharjah — Al Muwaileh Terminal

Passengers traveling on these two routes have the convenience of transferring between them at this station.

This intersection allows for seamless travel across Sharjah and to neighboring emirates, enhancing overall accessibility and mobility for public transport users.

Using the Careem app, you can conveniently travel to Sharjah — Al Muwaileh Terminal, the furthest point on the 77 bus route starting from the Al Saja'a Terminal. Although Al Saja'a Terminal cannot be directly pinpointed in the app, you can select a nearby location along the E88 route as your starting point. The fare for this journey typically ranges between AED 59-73, offering a flexible and affordable alternative for reaching your destination.

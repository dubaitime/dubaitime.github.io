## E400 Bus Route

Covering a distance of approximately 40 kilometers, the journey typically takes around 50 minutes, depending on traffic conditions.

The E400 bus route starts at Al Musallah Bus Station in Ajman and ends at Union Bus Station in Dubai. The route includes several strategically placed stops to serve major residential, commercial, and institutional areas. Here is a detailed list of the main stops along the E400 route:

This route connects major residential, commercial, and institutional areas, ensuring that passengers can easily access key destinations in both emirates.

Other RTA Dubai bus routes you can explore include the E306, which travels between Al Ghubaiba and Sharjah — Al Jubail Bus Station, and the F62, which connects Nadd Al Hamar to Umm Ramool and then loops back to Nadd Al Hamar.

How to Buy the Ticket?

Purchasing a ticket for the E400 bus is straightforward and can be done in several ways. You can buy tickets using a NOL Card, at ticket offices, or via the online and mobile app. The fare for a one-way trip on the E400 bus is highly affordable, ranging from AED 12 to AED 25 (approximately USD 4 to USD 7), making it one of the most cost-effective transportation options for traveling between Ajman and Dubai.

## Journey Experience

The E400 bus route from Ajman to Dubai provides a long yet smooth journey, connecting the traditional charm of Ajman with the modern allure of Dubai. Passing through Sharjah, passengers can catch a glimpse of cultural landmarks like the Sharjah Museum of Islamic Civilization.

A unique highlight along the route is its proximity to Al Mamzar Beach Park, offering a scenic seaside escape. Despite the lengthy travel time, the trip blends cultural richness with Dubai’s cutting-edge developments, making it both diverse and enjoyable.

- Economical Choice: At only AED 15 per trip, the E400 bus is a budget-friendly alternative to taxis or ride-hailing services.
- Comfort and Convenience: The buses are equipped with air conditioning, comfortable seating, and designated priority seating for women and children.
- Environmental Benefits: Public transportation reduces vehicle numbers on the road, lowering traffic congestion and carbon emissions, contributing to a greener environment.

## Bus Ticket Price

The fare for Bus 12 follows Dubai’s standard public transport pricing. A single journey typically costs AED 3 with a standard Nol Card. For multi-zone travel or if you have different Nol Card options, prices may vary. To get the most accurate fare information, passengers should check the latest rates through the RTA Dubai app or at metro stations.

## Bus Booking

Bus 12 does not offer online booking options. Passengers can board the bus directly at any of the stops along the route. Always check the app for any service changes or disruptions before traveling.

## Bus 12 Station Overview

### Al Quoz Bus Station · 3

Al Quoz Bus Station · 3 is a significant transit point located in the Al Quoz area, serving as a crucial stop for Bus 12. This station is well-equipped with modern amenities including seating, ticket counters, and real-time schedule updates, making it a comfortable and efficient spot for passengers. Its central position in the Al Quoz district ensures easy access to nearby commercial and residential areas, and it provides seamless connections to other bus routes and local services, enhancing the overall commuting experience.

### Ghubaiba Bus Station 43

Ghubaiba Bus Station 43 is a major transportation hub where Bus 12 makes a stop.

It serves as a key connection point for passengers traveling to various parts of Dubai, with well-maintained infrastructure that supports efficient travel. Its strategic location makes it a central point for both local and intercity bus routes, providing convenient access to multiple destinations and services.

## Tourist Destination Along the Way

Bus 12 passes by several notable tourist attractions and destinations throughout Dubai. Here are some highlights along its route:
1. Gold & Diamond Park: A popular shopping destination known for its wide selection of gold and diamond jewelry, offering a unique shopping experience for visitors interested in high-end accessories.

2. Dubai Center Driving School: While primarily a driving school, its location is a good reference point for those interested in Dubai's modern infrastructure and education.

3. Box Park: An urban shopping and dining area with a distinct, modern design, offering a variety of shops, eateries, and entertainment options. Box Park 1 and 2 are particularly popular among locals and tourists alike.

4. Spinneys: A well-known supermarket chain that offers a range of international and local products, convenient for travelers seeking familiar grocery options.

5. Jumeira American School: While not a tourist attraction per se, the school is located in the Jumeira area, known for its upscale residential and commercial facilities.

6. Iranian Hospital: Located in the vibrant Jumeira area, this hospital is part of the healthcare infrastructure serving both residents and visitors.

7. Al Mina Road and Department of Ports & Customs: This area is significant for those interested in Dubai's port operations and customs activities.

These locations make Bus 12 a practical route for both tourists exploring key shopping and dining spots and for those needing convenient access to various facilities throughout Dubai.

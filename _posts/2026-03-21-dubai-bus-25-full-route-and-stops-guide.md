## Bus 25 Station Overview

### Gold Souq Bus Station · 24

Gold Souq Bus Station · 24 is a key transit point situated near the bustling Gold Souq area, a popular destination for shopping and exploring traditional markets in Dubai. The station is well-equipped with essential amenities, including sheltered waiting areas, ticketing services, and real-time schedule updates.

Its central location makes it a convenient stop for shoppers and tourists visiting the Gold Souq, providing easy access to the vibrant marketplace and surrounding attractions. The station’s facilities and strategic placement enhance the overall travel experience for passengers heading to and from this lively commercial area.

### Intersection of Bus Routes at Burj Nahar Intersection

This central location offers seamless connections between these diverse routes, making it a crucial point for passengers traveling throughout Dubai. The stop is well-equipped with essential amenities and real-time schedule updates, providing a convenient and efficient travel experience for commuters using these intersecting bus services.

### Rashidiya Bus Station Parking

Rashidiya Bus Station Parking serves as an important transportation hub in the Rashidiya area, offering a dedicated parking area for buses and convenient access for commuters. The station provides essential amenities such as sheltered waiting areas, ticketing services, and up-to-date bus schedule information.

Its location supports efficient transit operations and facilitates smooth travel for passengers in the Rashidiya region. The parking facility ensures that buses can efficiently manage passenger flow while offering a reliable transit option for local residents and visitors using the Rashidiya Bus Station.

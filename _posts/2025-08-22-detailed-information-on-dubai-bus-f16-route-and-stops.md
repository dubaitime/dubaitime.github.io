Dubai Bus F16 provides an essential transportation link between various residential and commercial areas in Dubai. It caters primarily to commuters traveling between the Onpassive Terminus and FARNEK Staff Accommodation, serving multiple stops along the route.

## Bus Timings

At the Onpassive Terminus departure point, the F16 bus operates at frequent intervals throughout the day. Services start as early as 08:17 in the morning and continue until late at night, with the last bus departing at 00:10. This schedule ensures convenient access for commuters at various hours.

## Bus Ticket Price

Fares for Dubai's F16 bus depend on zones traveled, not a flat rate. This means you can expect to pay the minimum fare for a single zone trip, which is around AED 3.

## Journey Experience

The F16 bus route from Onpassive Terminus to FARNEK Staff Accommodation and back offers a long but insightful journey through a mix of urban and industrial areas in Dubai. Starting at Onpassive Terminus, the bus navigates through bustling commercial districts, showcasing Dubai’s modern infrastructure.

As the journey progresses, it transitions into quieter, more industrial zones, providing a glimpse into the city’s expanding workforce hubs and staff accommodation areas. This route highlights a different side of Dubai, focusing less on tourism and more on the practical infrastructure that supports the city’s workforce. Despite its length, the F16 route offers a unique perspective on how Dubai balances its vibrant urban life with the needs of its working population.

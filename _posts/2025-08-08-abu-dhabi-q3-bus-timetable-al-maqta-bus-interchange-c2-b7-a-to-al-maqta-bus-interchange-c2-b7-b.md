Bus Q3 Timetable

The Q3 bus runs every 10 minutes during operational hours, allowing passengers to travel with minimal wait times. For the full schedule, you can visit the AD Mobility official website or follow this link for Q3 Bus Timings.

Ticket Pricing: Payments for the Q3 bus are made using the Hafilat Card, Abu Dhabi’s cashless payment system. The fare for travel within one zone is AED 2, with additional charges of AED 0.05 per kilometer. Passengers can conveniently top up their Hafilat cards online or at kiosks located throughout the city, ensuring a smooth and cash-free experience.

## Accessibility and Services

The Q3 bus is designed to accommodate all passengers, ensuring a comfortable journey for everyone. The buses feature:

- Ramps for wheelchair access: Making it easy for passengers with mobility challenges.

- Designated seating: Reserved for those with special needs.
- Audio-visual aids: Supporting passengers with hearing or vision impairments.

This inclusive design ensures that everyone can enjoy Abu Dhabi’s public transport services.

## Travel Tips for Q3 Bus

- Plan Ahead: Always check the bus timings to avoid unnecessary waiting, especially during peak hours.

- Use the Hafilat Smart Card: This allows for quick, cashless payments on all Abu Dhabi buses.
- Monitor Bus Updates: Use the AD Mobility app for real-time updates and schedules to enhance your travel experience.
- Travel Off-Peak: If you prefer a quieter ride, avoid traveling during the busiest periods, typically in the morning and late afternoon.

## Bus C10 Station Overview

### Hassa Bint Al Murr Masjid

The Hassa Bint Al Murr Masjid bus station is strategically located near the Hassa Bint Al Murr Mosque, serving the surrounding residential and commercial areas. This station is designed to accommodate local commuters and visitors, offering essential amenities such as comfortable seating, ticketing services, and real-time schedule updates.

Its proximity to the mosque makes it a convenient stop for those attending religious services or engaging in community activities. The station’s location supports smooth connectivity to nearby neighborhoods and other key destinations in the area.

### Abu Hail Center 2 Bus Stop Intersection

The Abu Hail Center 2 Bus Stop serves as a crucial intersection for Bus Routes 64, 13B, C09, and C10. This stop connects passengers to various destinations across Dubai, making it a vital hub for commuters in the Abu Hail area. Its proximity to the Abu Hail Center ensures convenient access to shopping, dining, and other services, catering to the needs of both locals and visitors.

### Hamriya Port, Control Tower Terminus

The Hamriya Port, Control Tower Terminus is a significant bus station located within the Hamriya Port area, a major commercial and industrial zone in Dubai.

Its strategic location near the port and control tower ensures easy access for workers, business operators, and visitors. The station plays a vital role in facilitating transportation within the port area and connecting passengers to broader city transit networks.

## Bus Ticket Price

Passengers can enjoy affordable fares when traveling on the C10 bus route (typically around AED 3-5), with ticket prices designed to accommodate daily commuters and occasional travelers. Prices are set competitively to provide cost-effective transportation across Dubai.

## Bus Booking

While the C10 bus route does not require advanced booking, passengers can conveniently plan their journey using real-time bus schedules available through the Roads and Transport Authority (RTA) Dubai app or website. This accessibility allows travelers to check bus timings and plan their trips accordingly, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free commute.

## Journey Experience

The C10 bus route from Hassa Bint Al Murr Masjid to Hamriya Port offers a long yet captivating journey through Dubai, passing through vibrant commercial hubs and residential areas. Along the way, passengers can enjoy views of the historic Dubai Creek, a vital waterway in the city's trading past.

The route also takes travelers near the Deira district, home to the famous Gold Souk and Spice Souk, where the rich heritage of old Dubai thrives amidst the bustling markets. While the trip is lengthy, it provides a unique mix of modern city life and historical charm, making it a fascinating ride for both locals and tourists.

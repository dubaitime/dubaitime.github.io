Ticket Pricing: Payments for Bus A40 can be made using the Hafilat Card, Abu Dhabi’s convenient cashless payment system. Fares for journeys within a single zone are AED 2, with an additional charge of AED 0.05 per kilometer for extra distance. Passengers can easily top up their Hafilat cards online or at kiosks throughout the city, providing a hassle-free, cashless travel experience.

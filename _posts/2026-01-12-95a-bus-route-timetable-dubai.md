RTA Dubai Bus 95A is a practical and budget-friendly transport option for both residents and visitors. It is especially useful for daily commuting, as the route passes through Jebel Ali, Parco Hypermarket, and several important industrial and business areas. With clearly defined stops and a fixed timetable, Bus 95A offers convenient access for passengers traveling across this part of Dubai.

## Operational Details
Bus 95A operates every day, providing a consistent and reliable service throughout the day.

Passengers can view the full and most up-to-date timetable via the official RTA website under [95A Bus Timings](https://www.rta.ae/wps/PA_JourneyPlanner/DownloadTimetableServlet?lineId=dub:0195A:%20:H:y08&lineName=bus%2095A).

**Fare Information:**  Traveling on Bus 95A costs **AED 3** when using a personal Nol card or Silver Nol card, covering all zones on this route. Nol cards are widely used across Dubai’s public transport network and make boarding quick and cash-free. Top-up facilities are available at many metro stations, bus stations, and retail outlets across the city.

## Route Overview

![RTA 95A Bus Route Map](https://uaetiming.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/RTA-95A-Bus-Route-Map.jpg)

Bus 95A connects residential communities with industrial zones and commercial centers. Below are some of the main stops along the route:

1. **Jebel Ali, Parco Hypermarket:** A popular shopping and community destination.  
2. **Jebel Ali Industrial Area, Qaser Al Kabayel Center:** Serves the surrounding industrial workforce.  
3. **Jebel Ali Industrial Area 11:** An important stop for labor housing and nearby businesses.  
4. **Nuzul Labour Accommodation:** Provides access to workers’ residential areas.  
5. **Al Khaleej Al Madina Supermarket:** Convenient for daily shopping needs.  
6. **Amssar Wood Manufacturing:** Supports employees in the manufacturing sector.  
7. **UAE Exchange Metro (Seaside):** A major interchange with the Dubai Metro.  
8. **JAFZA One:** A central hub for corporate offices and professionals.  
9. **Marina Homes:** Connects nearby commercial and residential locations.  
10. **Baker Hughes:** Serves business visitors and staff.  
11. **Gulf Perlite:** Links workers to surrounding industrial facilities.  
12. **CEVA Logistics:** A key logistics and freight center.  
13. **Jebel Ali Waterfront, DUCAB HQ:** An important industrial and business stop.  
14. **DUCAB:** Serves employees of the cable manufacturing company.  
15. **Veneto:** The final stop near developing residential and commercial areas.

## Accessibility Features
Bus 95A is designed with inclusivity in mind. Low-floor boarding allows easy access for wheelchair users and passengers with limited mobility. In addition, audio and visual announcement systems are available to assist riders with hearing or vision impairments, ensuring a comfortable journey for everyone.

## Connectivity and Transfer Points
One of the strengths of Bus 95A is its strong connectivity. The stop at **UAE Exchange Metro Station** allows passengers to transfer seamlessly to the Dubai Metro and other bus routes, making it easier to travel across the city. The **Veneto** terminal also provides access to additional local transport options.

## Nearby Attractions and Landmarks
Passengers on Bus 95A can conveniently reach several notable locations, including:

1. **Jebel Ali Waterfront:** A growing area with waterfront views and commercial developments.  
2. **JAFZA One:** A major business and corporate district.  
3. **UAE Exchange Metro Station:** A key transport hub linking bus and metro services.

## Journey Experience
Traveling on Bus 95A from Jebel Ali to Veneto offers a unique look at Dubai’s industrial backbone and waterfront expansion. The journey begins in the busy Jebel Ali area, passing through factories, labor accommodations, and logistics centers. Along the way, landmarks such as JAFZA One and UAE Exchange Metro Station highlight the area’s economic importance, before the route transitions into the calmer surroundings near Jebel Ali Waterfront.

Arriving at Veneto, passengers will find a quieter area within the Dubai Waterfront development, offering a contrast between industrial activity and future residential growth. The route reflects Dubai’s balanced approach to development, blending business, transport, and living spaces.

## Tips and Travel Advice
To make your trip smoother on RTA Bus 95A, consider the following tips:

- Check the bus timetable in advance to minimize waiting time at stops.  
- Ensure your Nol card has sufficient balance before boarding.  
- Use the RTA mobile app for live bus tracking and real-time service updates.

## Bus Ticket Price

The fare for Bus 34 in Abu Dhabi is in line with the standard public transportation rates. Passengers can expect to pay approximately AED 3.50 per trip using a Hafilat card, which is widely used across the city’s bus network. The affordability of this route makes it an attractive option for both daily commutes and occasional trips. This cost-effective pricing ensures that traveling across Abu Dhabi remains budget-friendly for everyone.

## Bus Booking

While Bus 34 operates on a first-come, first-served basis, passengers can plan their journey using the Darb app, which provides real-time updates on bus locations and arrival times. Additionally, the Hafilat card can be topped up online, ensuring a seamless and hassle-free travel experience.

## Bus 34 Station Overview

### Qasr Al Watan

The station provides convenient access to the palace grounds and other nearby attractions. With its strategic location, it is a preferred stop for those looking to delve into the cultural and historical aspects of the city.

### Abu Dhabi Courts

Located near the Abu Dhabi Judicial Department, the Abu Dhabi Courts bus station is a crucial stop for legal professionals, government employees, and individuals involved in legal matters. The station is well-connected to key areas of the city, making it easily accessible for daily commuters. Its proximity to the courts and other governmental offices underscores its importance as a transit hub for those engaged in legal and administrative activities. Modern amenities and clear signage ensure a smooth commuting experience for passengers.

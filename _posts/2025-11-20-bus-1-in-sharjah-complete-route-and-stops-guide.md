## Bus 1 Station Overview

### Sahara Centre Terminal

Sahara Centre Terminal is a well-known bus station located adjacent to the popular Sahara Centre shopping mall in Sharjah.

The terminal is equipped with basic facilities, and its strategic location near the Dubai-Sharjah border makes it a convenient stop for those traveling between the two emirates. With various bus routes connecting through this terminal, it ensures smooth transit for passengers heading to different parts of Sharjah and Dubai.

### Al Rolla Terminal

Al Rolla Terminal is one of Sharjah's busiest bus stations, located in the heart of the city. This terminal is a major transit hub that connects various bus routes, making it a central point for commuters traveling within Sharjah and to other emirates.

The terminal is well-equipped with amenities such as seating areas, ticket counters, and nearby shops, providing a comfortable waiting environment for passengers. Its location in a densely populated area ensures that it serves a large number of daily commuters, making it a vital part of Sharjah's public transport network.

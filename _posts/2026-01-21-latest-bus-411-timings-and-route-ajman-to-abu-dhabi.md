Bus 411 provides a crucial link between Ajman and Abu Dhabi, offering a convenient and affordable transportation option for residents and visitors. Running between the Ajman Bus Station and Abu Dhabi’s Main Bus Station, this route simplifies the journey for those commuting for work or leisure. With multiple departures throughout the day, Bus 411 ensures a reliable and comfortable way to travel between these two emirates.

## Operational Details

Bus 411 operates with multiple departures each day, giving travelers flexibility based on their schedules. Here are the timings for the service:

### Ticket Pricing

The fare for Bus 411 is set at an affordable rate, making it an economical choice for those traveling between Ajman and Abu Dhabi.

- Service Fee: AED 47 for a one-way trip.

Passengers can purchase tickets either with cash or through available ticketing options at the bus station.

## Route Overview

Bus 411 starts its journey from Ajman Bus Station, making a stop at Thumbay Hospital before reaching its final destination at Abu Dhabi Bus Station. This straightforward route caters to passengers looking for a smooth and uninterrupted trip between Ajman and Abu Dhabi. Key stops along the route include:
1. Ajman Bus Station  The starting point for the journey, offering easy connections to local transportation services.

## Nearby Attractions

Bus 411 provides convenient access to several key landmarks and attractions along its route, making it a great option for both commuters and visitors.

- Thumbay Hospital  A major medical center in Ajman, offering healthcare services for residents and visitors.

- Abu Dhabi Corniche  A scenic waterfront destination perfect for relaxation and outdoor activities.
- Marina Mall Abu Dhabi  A premier shopping and entertainment destination located near the Abu Dhabi Bus Station.
- Qasr Al Hosn  A historical landmark in Abu Dhabi, offering cultural and historical insights into the UAE's heritage.

## Journey Experience

The route provides a mix of urban views and desert landscapes, with camel farms visible along the way.

Bus 411 serves as an essential transportation option for those traveling between Ajman and Abu Dhabi. With multiple daily departures and a direct route, it caters to both regular commuters and occasional travelers. The affordable fare, combined with the convenience of well-timed schedules, makes it a top choice for inter-emirate travel.

## Bus F22 Station Overview

### Al Qusais Industrial Area 2 Terminus

Al Qusais Industrial Area 2 Terminus is a significant bus station situated in the industrial sector of Al Qusais. This terminus serves as a major transportation hub for the industrial and commercial areas nearby, providing vital connectivity for workers and business professionals.

The station's strategic location ensures easy access to various local industries and businesses, making it a crucial part of the transportation network in the Al Qusais Industrial Area.

### Intersection at Al Qusais Industrial Area 3, Mustaqeem Mosque Bus Stop

This strategic location provides passengers with convenient connections between these diverse routes, facilitating efficient travel across the region. The stop is equipped with essential amenities, enhancing the overall travel experience for commuters navigating through the Al Qusais area.

### Stadium Bus Station · 5

Stadium Bus Station · 5 is a prominent transit hub located near major sports venues and facilities.

It offers essential amenities such as sheltered waiting areas, real-time schedule updates, and ticketing services to ensure a comfortable travel experience. Its location close to stadiums and sports facilities makes it a convenient option for sports fans and event-goers, providing seamless access to and from major sporting events and local attractions.

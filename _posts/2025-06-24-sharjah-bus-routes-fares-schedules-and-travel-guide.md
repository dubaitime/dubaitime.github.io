## Find Sharjah Bus Stations with RIHLATI

RIHLATI by Mowasalat uses satellite and GSM technology to help passengers check bus routes and schedules across Sharjah. Whether you’re commuting or exploring, RIHLATI provides real-time updates to ensure a smooth travel experience. Access this service by visiting the RTA Sharjah website or downloading the RIHLATI app from the App Store or Google Play. The app allows you to select bus stations from a dropdown menu, aiding efficient journey planning within Sharjah.

## Mowasalat Sayer Card

The Sayer card is an electronic payment method for public buses in Sharjah. All Mowasalat buses are equipped with card readers, allowing passengers to pay without cash. You can purchase a Sayer card from any Mowasalat bus or terminal, making travel more convenient.

Types of Sayer Cards:

- Blue Card: Offers a 25% discount on regular fares, making each ride AED 6. It’s a cost-effective option for frequent travelers.

- Gray Card: For AED 225, this card provides unlimited bus rides for a month, ideal for daily commuters.
- White Card: Available to people of determination, senior citizens, and those receiving social assistance, this card provides free bus travel within Sharjah for a year.

### Mowasalat Monthly Subscription Card

For regular bus users, the Mowasalat Monthly Subscription Card is a beneficial option, offering rewards and convenience. Purchase or top-up this card directly on Mowasalat buses by paying the driver. The card costs AED 225 and is valid for 30 days, with a one-time purchase fee of AED 5.

## Sharjah Intercity Bus Routes

The intercity buses are designed to offer a comfortable and reliable service, catering to both daily commuters and occasional travelers.

Travel time is typically around 30-45 minutes, depending on traffic conditions. The fare for a one-way trip generally starts at AED 15.

- Abu Dhabi: Intercity buses to Abu Dhabi connect Sharjah with the capital city, providing a crucial link for those traveling to and from the UAE's largest city. The journey usually takes around 1.5 to 2 hours, with fares beginning at AED 25.
- Ajman: Buses to Ajman offer a shorter commute compared to other intercity routes, with travel times of approximately 20-30 minutes. The fare for a single trip is around AED 10.

Schedules and frequency for these routes can vary, so it's advisable to check the latest information on the official RTA Sharjah website. The site provides comprehensive details on routes, timings, and ticket prices, ensuring you can plan your journey effectively.

## Taxi Services

In Sharjah, taxi services are accessible and versatile, catering to a wide range of needs through the SRTA app. The app allows you to book various types of taxis based on your requirements:

- Normal Taxis: These are standard taxis suitable for everyday travel within Sharjah and to nearby areas. They offer a reliable and affordable option for most passengers.

- They are driven by female drivers, ensuring a safer experience for women traveling alone or with children.
- Family Taxis: Ideal for families, these taxis offer extra space and can accommodate larger groups with children. They are equipped to handle the needs of traveling families, including child seats if required.
- Special Needs Taxis: These taxis are equipped to assist passengers with special needs or disabilities. They offer accessible features to ensure a smooth and comfortable journey for all passengers.

Taxi fares are determined by the type of vehicle and the distance traveled. The SRTA app provides a fare estimate before booking, helping you plan your travel budget. These services provide various vehicle types and can often be more flexible in terms of payment methods and vehicle choice.

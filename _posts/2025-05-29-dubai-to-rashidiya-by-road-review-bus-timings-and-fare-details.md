Traveling from Dubai to Rashidiya is a straightforward journey, with several convenient options available.

## Overview of Travel Options: Duration, Cost, and Distance

The distance between Dubai and Rashidiya is approximately 26 km, with a road distance of about 22.4 km. Here are the primary travel options:

- Bus: The Dubai RTA Bus Line 102 offers a cost-effective and direct service between Global Village 1 and Rashidiya Bus Station Gate 11. The journey takes around 33 minutes and costs between AED 30 and AED 42.

- Taxi: For a quicker journey, taxis can travel from Dubai to Rashidiya in about 15 minutes, covering the same 22.4 km distance. Taxi fares range from AED 230 to AED 290.
- Car: Driving yourself from Dubai to Rashidiya takes approximately 16 minutes. The cost of fuel for this trip ranges between AED 38 and AED 55.

## Bus 160 Station Overview

### Khalifa City, Souq

The Khalifa City Souq bus station serves as a key transport hub in the bustling Khalifa City area, providing convenient access to the local market and shopping area.

It features essential amenities to enhance the shopping experience, such as ticket counters and seating areas. The station's proximity to the souq ensures that passengers can easily access a variety of goods and services in this vibrant community space.

### Capital Park

The Capital Park bus station is situated near the expansive Capital Park, a prominent green space in the heart of the city. This station is a convenient access point for individuals visiting the park, whether for leisure, sports, or community events.

It offers modern facilities and ample connections to other parts of the city, making it easy for visitors to reach the park and enjoy its recreational facilities. The station's location supports a seamless transition between urban transit and outdoor activities, enhancing the overall accessibility to the park's amenities.

Bus N43 (overnight) is one of the main routes, running from Corniche Hospital to Qaryat Al Beri Souq. This is an overnight service, ensuring you can travel easily across the city at any hour. The following guide is a detailed walkthrough of the N43 bus route, with timings, major stops, and some useful travel tips. This information allows passengers to enjoy smooth and efficient travel.

## Operational Details

The bus N43 runs 24 hours a day and is therefor the perfect connection for locals as well as tourists. Below is the detailed schedule for Bus N43, departing from both Corniche Hospital and Qaryat Al Beri Souq:

Bus N43 TimetableFor the full schedule, visit the official AD Mobility website or follow this link for the N43 Bus Timings.

Ticket Pricing: Bus N43 Travel payments can be made using Abu Dhabi’s convenient cashless payment system, the Hafilat Card. For single zone journeys, fares are AED 2 and an additional AED 0.05 per kilometer for additional distance. For a cashless, hassle free experience, passengers can top up their Hafilat cards online or at kiosks across the city.

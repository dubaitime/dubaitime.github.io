## Bus Ticket Price

The fare for Bus Route J02 is designed to be affordable, typically ranging from AED 5 to AED 8 depending on the distance traveled. This pricing structure makes it a cost-effective option for daily commuting and occasional travel between Dubai Production City and Arabian Ranches.

## Bus Booking

Currently, Bus Route J02 does not offer online booking options. For a seamless travel experience, passengers can utilize the RTA’s Nol card system, which is accepted on all public transport in Dubai, including Bus J02. This system allows for quick and convenient fare payment, enhancing the overall travel experience.

### Arabian Ranches 2, Main Entrance Bus Stop Intersection

The Arabian Ranches 2, Main Entrance Bus Stop is a central hub where Bus Routes J02 and F32 intersect. This key location provides convenient connections for residents and visitors traveling within the Arabian Ranches area and beyond. The intersection ensures efficient transit options and easy access to various destinations, making it a vital stop for commuters in the region.

## Touris Destination Along the Way

Bus Route J02 travels through several areas in Dubai that offer a range of attractions and destinations:
1. City Centre Meaisem: This shopping mall provides a variety of retail options, dining choices, and entertainment facilities, making it a popular destination for shopping and leisure.

2. The Dubai Sports City also features residential complexes and commercial areas.

3. Dubai Cricket Stadium: A prominent venue for cricket matches, this stadium hosts international and domestic cricket events, attracting fans and tourists.

4. The Sustainable City: This eco-friendly community is notable for its sustainable design and green initiatives. It features parks, a solar farm, and various environmental attractions.

5. The area also includes retail centers and schools.

6. Dubai Studio City: A commercial and residential zone geared towards media and entertainment industries. It includes studios and production facilities, contributing to Dubai's vibrant media landscape.

7. Mira Oasis and Mira Towncentre: These are part of the Mira community, known for its residential and retail options, offering a suburban feel with convenient amenities.

Taking the J02 bus from Dubai Production City, Centrium Tower to Arabian Ranches, Savannah Gate provides a unique view of Dubai's diverse neighborhoods.

As you approach Arabian Ranches, you'll be greeted by luxury villas and lush greenery, offering a refreshing contrast to the typical desert landscape. Overall, the J02 bus trip combines convenient transportation with an enjoyable sightseeing experience, revealing the charm of Dubai's suburban areas.

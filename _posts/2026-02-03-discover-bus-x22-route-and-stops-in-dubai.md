## Bus X22 Station Overview

### Al Qusais Industrial Area 2 Terminus

This station caters to the needs of workers, businesses, and residents in the area.

It is a crucial hub for those traveling to and from the industrial zones, providing efficient connections to various other parts of the city and facilitating smooth daily commutes.

### Intersection at Al Qusais Industrial Area 3, Mustaqeem Mosque Bus Stop

This strategic location provides passengers with convenient connections between these diverse routes, facilitating efficient travel across the region. The stop is equipped with essential amenities, enhancing the overall travel experience for commuters navigating through the Al Qusais area.

### Intersection of Bus Routes at Al Nahda 1, Terminus

This strategic location facilitates seamless connections between these routes, providing convenient transit options for passengers traveling within and beyond the Al Nahda area. The terminus offers essential amenities and real-time schedule updates, enhancing the overall travel experience and making it a key hub for commuters using these various bus services.

### Business Bay Metro Landside

The Business Bay Metro Landside station is strategically located to serve the vibrant Business Bay district, a key commercial and residential area in Dubai. Positioned adjacent to the Business Bay Metro Station, this bus stop provides seamless integration with the metro system, enhancing connectivity for both professionals and residents.

The station is designed to accommodate high passenger volumes, offering amenities such as sheltered waiting areas and digital schedule displays. Its proximity to major business hubs and residential complexes makes it a vital component of the city’s public transport network.

## Bus Ticket Price

The ticket price for the X22 bus route is structured to be affordable and accessible to all passengers. Standard fare for a single trip on the X22 route typically ranges from AED 3 to AED 10, depending on the distance traveled. Passengers can use Nol Cards, which offer various fare options and discounts.

## Bus Booking

Booking a seat on the X22 bus can be done through various methods. Passengers can board the bus directly from any of the designated stops along the route. For those who prefer a more planned approach, the RTA (Roads and Transport Authority) website and mobile app provide options for checking schedules and planning routes.

This offers a seamless travel experience by integrating both private and public transportation options.

## Journey Experience

Despite its length, the X22 route offers a comprehensive view of Dubai’s industrial, residential, and commercial zones, making it an engaging and well-rounded trip for both locals and visitors.

## Traveling with Careem

With the Careem app, you can conveniently travel to Business Bay Metro Landside, the furthest point on the X22 bus route from Al Qusais Industrial Area 2 Terminus. The fare for this journey typically ranges from AED 88 to AED 106, providing a seamless and efficient option for reaching your destination.

The X22 bus route is an essential link in Dubai’s public transportation network, providing efficient and affordable transit options between Al Qusais Industrial Area 2 and Business Bay. With frequent service intervals, numerous stops, and convenient ticketing options, the X22 bus ensures that passengers can travel comfortably and reliably across the city. Whether you are commuting for work, school, or leisure, the X22 route offers a practical solution for navigating Dubai.

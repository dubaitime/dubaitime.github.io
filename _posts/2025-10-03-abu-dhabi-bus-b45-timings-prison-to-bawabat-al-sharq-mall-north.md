## Journey Experience

The B45 bus from Prison to Bawabat Al Sharq Mall North offers an engaging ride through Abu Dhabi's vibrant neighborhoods. Departing from Lane 2003 Souq, the bus passes the Al Rahman Mosque, a stunning spiritual landmark, and the local Farmer’s Market, where fresh produce awaits. Educational spots like Maryam Bint Omran School showcase the community's commitment to learning.

As it approaches the mall, passengers can enjoy views of the lush greenery around Al Wathba Mosque and the bustling Municipal Market. This route not only connects essential points but also highlights Abu Dhabi's charm and cultural richness, making for a memorable travel experience.

## Travel Tips for Bus B45

- Plan Ahead: Check the bus timings to minimize waiting times, especially during peak hours.

- Use the Hafilat Smart Card: This allows for quick and cashless payments on all Abu Dhabi buses.
- Monitor Bus Updates: Utilize the AD Mobility app for real-time updates and schedules to enhance your travel experience.
- Avoid Peak Times: For a quieter ride, try to travel outside of the busiest morning and afternoon periods.

Through its services, the Roads and Transport Authority (RTA) guarantees the cleanliness, adequate, and comfortable transport vehicles for every passenger. But as in all public service there are certain procedures which have to be observed to ensure the smooth running of the system.

It will also be useful to know what is prohibited if you travel by RTA Dubai bus line or use other public transport facilities. Their violation can not only interfere with your trip but also end up getting a fine. In this article, you will learn about five things that you should not do on an RTA bus and the consequences for their violation.

## 1. Sleeping at the Bus Stop

Although one might think it reasonable to doze off for a few minutes before the bus comes, it is actually against the law to sleep at the RTA bus stop in Dubai. The city authorities appreciate an order and safety; people sleeping on the benches are disturbing the other passengers. This is wrong; if you are found sleeping at the bus stop, you will be fined 300 AED. Thus, the best thing you should do is remain wake and be prepared to board the bus when it comes!

## 2. Using a Fake or Expired Nol Card

Dubai Nol card is used to pay for travel on RTA buses, trains, and even water transport means in Dubai. The Nol card should be both valid and original, so you should check all the above-mentioned facts before using the card. Taking a Nol card that is fake or that has expired in order to get on the bus is a violation. If you are caught then you could be fined up to 200 AED. Ensure you check your card balance and its expiry date before heading out and ensure it is charged adequately.

## 3. Eating or Drinking on the Bus

If you are on the move you can just grab a snack or beverage to consume while on the move. Apart from that it provokes the creation of unnecessary clutter, it can also be annoying for other passengers. You better not be eating or drinking while traveling because you will be charged 100 AED. If you are feeling hungry or thirsty, it’s safer to hold on until you get where you are supposed to take a snack or drink.

## 4. Littering or Spitting on the Bus

The DO NOT’S on the RTA bus include littering or spitting. Hygiene is a key priority in Dubai and keeping the transport means clean is part of thisengan. Littering or dropping food on the floor or even heaving a loach on the streets is both unhygienic and Against the law. If the law catches you doing either of the two, then you will be asked to pay a fine of 200 AED.

## 5. Selling Goods or Advertising on the Bus

The Dubai public transport system is not a place for conduct of trading activities and the selling of products or advertising is not allowed. Be it promoting a product or dropping flyers, all such activities hinder the normal flow of the bus service and can cause a near riot. The fine for the offense can range from 200 to 500 AED if you are caught selling goods or even advertising on the bus. Do not engage in any business related matters during your ride and avoid causing offence to other users of the bus space.

All these actions come with penalty charges.

But in order to have a nice ride and not to feel uncomfortable it is better not to trespass these rules and at least know them. Stay informed, be courteous and ensure you have a smooth time traveling!

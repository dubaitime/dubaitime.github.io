Public transportation system in city of Abu Dhabi vividly promotes reliability and accessibility to its residents as well as visitors. One of its main routes is Bus X70 which operates from Abu Dhabi Bus Station to Mirfa Bus Station, with stop at Tarif Bus Station. The route, travel times, major stops and travel tips of Bus X70 are explained in detail in this guide.

Ticket Pricing: Fares begin from AED 2 for single zone travel and can be paid through Hafilat Card. There are additional charges for longer journeys. Recharging the Hafilat Cards is also quite easy at kiosks or online.

## Journey Experience

Bus X70 from Abu Dhabi Bus Station to Mirfa Bus Station is a long journey but a scenic one which shows you a bit of Abu Dhabi’s varied landscapes. The route takes us through Tarif, passing from the hustle and bustle of Abu Dhabi to the peaceful coastal vibes of Mirfa.

Tarif is a noted midway point, close to desert dunes and a place of quiet charm. Travelers arriving in Mirfa are treated to beautiful views of the Arabian Gulf and the town’s annual al Dhafra Water Festival brings a splash of cultural richness.
